No one wants to do a breast implant without a lift. Why?
Implant weight will make the breast fall
May 31, 2022
Hello, thank you for question. It seems in your pictures that you have breast ptosis (breast falling); so if an implant is placed without performing a lift, the implant will result in ptosis worsening, because the implant is extra weight added to your breast tissues weight, so; if you already have ptosis, expect worse ptosis and results after just placing an implant.

Is there a reason a surgeon would suggest a small implant?
Implant size related to breast base and tissues
May 27, 2022
Hello, thank you for your question. Most of the time in suggesting an implant size a surgeon take into account the breast base measurement; as it is the most important and limiting measure in implant choice; it is not advisable to choose and implant with a base larger Than the breast base; as the corners of the implant could become visible. Also, tissue elasticity and cushion are take into account; as they are limiting in containing certain implant sizes.

Would I benefit from internal bra with lift?
Internal bra and sagging skin
May 24, 2022
Hello. Thank you for your question. An internal bra will push the implant up and medially, when we a re performing a breast augmentation this is desirable; but in breast lifts, if you have very saggy skin; the implant could stay high and the breast tissue slide down, giving you a waterfall deformity.
