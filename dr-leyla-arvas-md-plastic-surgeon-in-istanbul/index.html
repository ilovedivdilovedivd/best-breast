Statement: Dr. Leyla Arvas graduated from Istanbul University Medical Faculty in 1998 and commenced her specialization in Plastic, Reconstructive, and Aesthetic Surgery. Throughout her educational journey, she gained experience in renowned clinics and hospitals across different countries.

She pursued further studies in microsurgery, focusing on head and neck reconstruction as well as breast surgery, at Taiwan Chang Gung Memorial Hospital.

During her tenure at TWMU Hospital in Tokyo, she honed her skills in laser treatments for various conditions, including acne, hyperpigmentation, wound scars, wrinkles, and acne scars.

As a fellow at IDB (Instituto Javier de Benito) in Barcelona, Spain, she actively engaged in clinical research, with a particular emphasis on facial and breast aesthetics. Dr. Arvas attended multiple Silhouette Lift and Silhouette Soft meetings in Barcelona and has since applied these techniques for over four years.

Following the completion of her plastic surgery specialization, she assumed the role of Assistant Professor of Plastic Surgery at Yeditepe University Hospital in Istanbul.

In 2011, she became a plastic surgery consultant at Bosphorus Anatomica Plastic Surgery Center, where she performed a wide range of aesthetic procedures and minimally invasive treatments, including Botox, filler injections, mesotherapies, laser treatments, chemical peelings, and thread lifts.

Since 2015, she has been running her private practice at Quartz Clinic in Istanbul’s Nisantasi district. Dr. Arvas conducts surgeries at well-established hospitals such as Acibadem Taksim Hospital and Amerikan Hospital on the European side of Istanbul.

Her expertise lies in various procedures, including Rhinoplasty, Blepharoplasty, Face Lifts (including endoscopic face lifts), Liposuction, Breast Lift, Augmentation, and Reduction Mammoplasty. She also specializes in Tummy Tuck procedures, commonly referred to as body sculpting procedures.

Dr. Arvas utilizes VASER liposuction technology for body reshaping and has a particular interest in High Definition Liposculpturing, especially for individuals seeking a more athletic appearance.
