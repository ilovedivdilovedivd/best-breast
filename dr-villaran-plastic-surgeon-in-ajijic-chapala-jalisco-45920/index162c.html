9 mths after tummy tuck, I feel tingling & vibration surge on left side when I rub my fingers
Post-TT tingles
Nov 6, 2015
Tingling and strange sensations are called paresthesias, and are normal some months after the procedure, as it means the nerves are reconnecting. You may also feel sharp little twinges in different parts of your tummy. These usually subside after approximately a year, and if not are treatable with medications for that address neuropathic pain.My advice is to keep calm and talk to your surgeon about it.
