Cohesive breast implants

Thank you for your inquiry and photos. You have great results and the incisions look like they have healed well. I always recommend that my patients start breast massages 3-4 weeks after their surgery. Whether it be lymphatic massages with a massage therapist or at home massages, they are very important to avoid capsular contraction. This will also help the implants drop a bit and they will feel softer as well. Always address any concerns with your board certified plastic surgeon.

Redness after surgery
Apr 1, 2019
Thank you for your inquiry and photos. Usually redness accompanied with warmth can be a sign of an infection starting. Since your physician prescribed anti-biotics, I would wait a few more days to see if the redness subsides. It can take a few days for the anti-biotics to kick in. Continue with the bathing instructions as instructed by your physician to keep the incisions clean and follow up with him/her if there is any change, whether it is for better or worse.
