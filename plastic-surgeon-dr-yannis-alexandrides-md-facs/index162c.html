Anesthesia for people with alopecia?
Consult With A Dermatologist
Oct 11, 2023
There is a connection between general anaesthetic and alopecia, it's not an absolute contraindication, it's just a risk factor. For more details, consult with a Dermatologist.
Can my downturned crooked nose be corrected?
Rhinoplasty and Septoplasty Can Achieve Your Goals
Oct 11, 2023
This is a case where a Rhinoplasty combined with a Septoplasty would be the right answer to fix both the external appearance of your nose and the droopy tip.
Rhinoplasty and facial asymmetry
Consider A Combined Septorhinoplasty
Oct 11, 2023
I am unsure if this condition has affected your nose, it is possible, however, it could be other regions that have caused deviation of the nose and septum in general. In order to improve this, the procedure I would perform would be a combined Septorhinoplasty.
I feel like my nose tip is uneven. What treatments would be best for me?
The Procedures You've Mentioned Could Be Applied
Oct 11, 2023
I would say all the procedures that you've noted could be applied. I would consider an Open Tip Rhinoplasty in order to refine the tip and give more definition, a small Chin Implant would give your more projection too and Buccal Fat Removal would also be great way to emphasise your high cheek bones.
Rhinoplasty - what would you do to improve my nose?
Perhaps a Tip Refinement
Oct 11, 2023
I would say that overall your nose looks very nice, you don't have many sharp angles – perhaps just the front tip – this is potentially why I would suggest that a Tip Refinement surgery would be a great way to get a more of a softer look which would also be able to lift the tip slightly up.

It would be better if I could see a picture of your nose from the profile view to make the best decision, however, I would perform an Open Tip Septorhinoplasty and most likely I would use a Tip Graft.

What are my options to correct the slope of my lower eyelid?
More Photos Needed to Decide Best Surgical Course of Action
Sep 28, 2023
In my opinion, there are no non-surgical options for correcting the slope of your eyelid. There are operations that reposition the lateral campus which is the corner of the eye and this is performed with various techniques... Some of them are called Canthopexy or Canthoplasty, however, because the photo you have shown me is very narrow, I cannot see the rest of your face to decide which one would be appropriate for you.
Age 32, what procedure would correct the puffiness and shadows under my eyes?
Both Surgical and Non-Surgical Options Available
Sep 28, 2023
My advice would be to consider a laser treatment that resurfaces the skin, or Ameela (a polynucleotide injectable treatment to target puffiness and dark shadowing). Surgically-wise, a Blepharoplasty with Micrografing would also help.
