Is it true that Power Assisted Liposuction is performed under local anesthesia?
Lipo without general anesthesia
Nov 21, 2023
You may have liposuction of a limited region under local anesthesia +- IV sedation. Best examples are upper arms and the neck. Just lower abdomen in a low BMI patient is also feasible. However, in larger areas general anesthesia is more safe and comfortable due to lidocaine toxicity and the extend of the surgical field. Power assisted devices (vibrating cannulas) do not decrease the need for general anesthesia, as well.
I am a smoker – why can I not have implants?
Smoking & Implants
Nov 21, 2023
You may have silicone breast implants but if you need a concomitant lifting as well, smoking will cause wound dehiscences, skin loss and even loss of areola-nipple complex. So you have to quite smoking totally at least 4 weeks prior to surgery and do not smoke in the post period until after all the wounds are healed. You may use some supporting medication to help you quit smoking.
