Do I need a Breast Lift – the doctor I seen says no?
depends
Apr 7, 2022
What happens when you lift your arms above your head? If your nipple is 5-6cm above the fold of your breast then no lift needed. If less than that, or if you are particularly looking for a full upper pole of the breast, then an uplift may be required. Be sure to tell your surgeon EXACTLY what shape you are looking for and they should be able to advise you accordingly

Will I gain (and be unable to lose) weight after a tummy tuck?
All about stability
Apr 7, 2022
It’s all about thermodynamics. You can’t create mass from nothing. So, if you have been able to maintain a stable weight prior to TT then there should be no reason for you not to maintain a stable weight afterwards. If you do put on weight after the surgery then it is possible that the weight you would have put onto your lower tummy will go somewhere else since your lower tummy is no longer there!
Am I too slender and petite for a tummy tuck?
TT likely to be fine
Apr 7, 2022
If you can pinch the excess skin in your tummy so that the skin above your belly button can reach the top of your pubic area then a TT would be a good option. Even if you cannot, a small vertical scar can be a small price to pay for getting rid of all that excess skin. See a plastic surgeon for a full exam to go through your options
