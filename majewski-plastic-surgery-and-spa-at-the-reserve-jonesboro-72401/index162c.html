Is CoolSculpting contraindicated if you have neuropathy?
CoolSculpting
Jul 29, 2019
Neuropathy is not a contraindication for CoolSculpting. I would recommend that you consult your physician that is treating your neuropathy prior to treatment. I would also mention this condition to your CoolSculpting provider so they are aware prior to treatment.
I have systemic scleroderma, can I do cool sculpting?
CoolSculpting
Jul 29, 2019
Scleroderma is not a contraindication for CoolSculpting treatment. I would advise you to discuss the treatment with your dermatologist prior to being treated.
What can I take for pain relief 4 days after CoolSculpting?
CoolSculpting
May 2, 2019
Bruising, tenderness and swelling are normal side effects after CoolSculpting. These side effects normally resolve in a few weeks. With the amount of bruising that you have I would recommend Tylenol for the pain. You may also want to use a heating pad on the areas to see if that will help with the pain. If the pain persist, then you may want to contact your provider. They may prescribe something like Gabapentin for the pain.

Laparoscopic surgery after CoolSculpting. Will the surgery affect my results from my CS se
CoolSculpting
May 2, 2019
Laparoscopic surgery should not affect your CoolSculpting results. CoolSculpting addressed subcutaneous fat only. Once these fat cells are frozen, they are already in the dying process. The laparoscopic surgery only uses tiny incisions which cause minimal trauma to the area. You should see your results 2-3 months after your second treatment. You should be reevaluated at that time to see if more treatments are needed.
