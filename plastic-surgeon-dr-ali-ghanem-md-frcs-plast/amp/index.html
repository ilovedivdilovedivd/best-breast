How Much Does Botox for Hyperhidrosis of the Underarm Cost?
London Prices for Hyperhidrosis
Sep 6, 2020
Prices for Hyperhidrosis treatment will depend on the Botulinum toxin units actually being used. In our clinic prices start from £490. We also use a special device to make the procedure as painless as it can be (The Enerjet 2.0). When using the device, prices start from £790.
What is Ellanse and is it reliable?
Very reliable
Sep 6, 2020
Ellanse stimulates the natural production of collagen in the body. That is to say, Ellanse is designed to engage the body over time, unlike Hyaluronic Acid injections which only provide a temporary filling effect. Ellanse does not contain Hyaluronic Acid. I find the product to be very reliable, especially for clients that are looking for long term results.
What Areas of the Face Can Botox Be Injected?
Areas best treated with Botox
Sep 6, 2020
A good practioner with good knowledge of anatomy will commonly use Botox for:
1. The corners of the eyes (also known as crow’s feet)2. Forehead lines3. Frown lines (glabellar lines)
Botox can also be used to treat Hyperhidrosis (over sweating) and Masseter Hypertrophy (Jaw treatment).
How Long Does Botox Last?
2-4 months
Sep 6, 2020
The effects of botox injections may vary between individuals, however, results are expected to last between 2 and 4 months, sometimes longer, before it wears off and repeated treatment is required.
How Long Before Botox Results Can Be Seen?
About 7 days
Sep 6, 2020
After about a week, the toxin will take effect and you will notice a reduction in the movement of the targeted muscles, such as those responsible for frowning, raising eyebrows or creating crow’s feet.
This means that when you make facial expressions the wrinkles in the injected area will be reduced significantly, or even cease entirely (depending on the dosage). I seek to produce natural-looking results and do not advocate a ‘frozen look’ that prevents from expressing facial emotions.
