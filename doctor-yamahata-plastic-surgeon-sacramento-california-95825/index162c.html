What can I do about implants that are too big for my frame?
implants that are too big for my frame
Nov 17, 2023
Unfortunately, there is no way to achieve you goals without surgery. To reduce your volume, you could replace your implants with smaller prostheses. Smaller implant will also reduce the width and the width can be further reduced by changing the profile. Going from your current 415cc implants to 350cc may be inadequate for your goals. A 65cc reduction is only a change of 2.2 ounces. Discuss your concerns and options with your surgeon.

Scar has moved from my breast fold
Nov 17, 2023
