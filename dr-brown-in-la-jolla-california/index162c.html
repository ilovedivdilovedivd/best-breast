What do you think the mark is on my top lip and how can I make it go away?
Red mark after lip filler
Jan 30, 2022
Hi – If this has been present for weeks to months it is not a broken blood vessel. Depending on the brand of HA lip filler injected, it could be an inflamed granuloma forming as the body reacts to the presence of the foreign product. I recommend dissolving that area and trying a different brand. I hope this helps. Dr. Brown
What’s causing bruising from filler after 4 months?
Bruising 4 months later
Jan 30, 2022
Hi – we have learned that when bruising is still present months after injections, it’s no longer a bruise at all. It is post inflammatory hyperpigmentation. This is most likely to occur in mixed race patients in my experience. It is best treated with skin-lightening treatments such as topical medications, chemical peels, and broad band light. I hope this helps. Dr. Brown
