Can muscle be trimmed (removed) during a facelift?
What is done in a standard facelift?
Apr 19, 2020
Dear Ms Mann,
There are many different types of facelift but in a standard facelift, both your skin and the underlying support layer (SMAS in the face, platysma muscle in the neck) are lifted and repositioned. The underlying SMAS and platyma may be trimmed, tightened or overlapped during the lift and excess skin is also removed. Most facelifts are variations of this and treat both of these layers - skin and SMAS/platyma.
I hope this helps.
Best wishes,
Nora Nugent
