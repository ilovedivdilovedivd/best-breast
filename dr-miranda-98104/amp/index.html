Statement: Dr. Suzette Miranda is a board-certified plastic surgeon practicing in Seattle, Washington at Swedish Plastics and Aesthetics. While she performs all types of plastic and reconstructive surgery of the face, breast, and body, she specializes in mommy makeover surgery and breast enhancement, including breast augmentation and breast reduction.

Dr. Miranda has a patient-focused philosophy with a commitment to empowering patients to feel their best while achieving the most beautiful, natural-looking results. Through a combination of artistic surgical skill and exceptional patient care from consultation through recovery, she helps her patients feel more confident in their unique beauty. Dr. Miranda also understands that everyone has their own aesthetic goals, so she ensures that each patient is heard, informed, and supported throughout their experience with her practice.

After graduating from medical school at the University of California, San Francisco, Dr. Miranda pursued her general surgery training at UCSF. She then went on to receive specialty microsurgery training through a fellowship at the Buncke Clinic, followed by a plastic surgery residency at the University of Washington. Dr. Miranda gained further expertise through a fellowship with renowned plastic surgeon Dr. Mark Codner in Atlanta, Georgia.

Dr. Miranda is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.
