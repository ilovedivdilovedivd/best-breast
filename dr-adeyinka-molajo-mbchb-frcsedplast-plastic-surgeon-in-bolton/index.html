Dual plane placement and inframammary fold incision
May 11, 2021
Thank you for your question. Before undergoing surgery both you and your surgeon must decide on the incision position and the pocket position. Your surgeon will explain the pros and cons of each incision and pocket.
In my practice I routinely use the infra-mammary fold as it is generally well hidden and gives great access for pocket dissection during surgery. I regularly combine the infra-mammary fold with dual plane pocket position.
I hope this helps your decision making.
Good luck

What happens if you get sick with a cough or cold before surgery?
Sep 25, 2020
Safe surgery is my number one concern. I always want you and any patient to be safe as possible. if you come down with a cold or any illness before surgery you must let your surgeon know. As a surgeon I would be happy to postpone surgery until you are well again
