Is it normal to heal faster on one side then the other after wisdom teeth removal?
Healing and Wisdom Teeth
Dec 13, 2018
HI There, You have pointed to exactly why you are experiencing a difference in your healing from right to left.  “They had to dig more.”  The trauma from that digging, with it’s resultant swelling, will have your right side heal differently than the left.  Not sure it is slower, it is different – more to heal – than your left.  If you are not experiencing pain, bleeding, or redness/heat – all signs of inflammation/infection – then you are on the  “normal” path of healing.
