How long after microneedling can I get Botox?
Microneedling and Botox
Jul 18, 2019
Thank you for your question. It is typical for patients to wait a few days before injecting Botox after micro needling. It is typically ok to undergo Botox after the healing from micro needling has occurred. This can be determined by a decrease in the erythema and scabbing from the procedure. The amount of days will depend on your individual healing process, but is usually ok after 4-7 days. Good luck from Orlando!
