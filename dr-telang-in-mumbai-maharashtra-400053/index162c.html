Fillers vs face lift to add more youthful appearance after weight loss?
Facelift or Fillers
Oct 2, 2016
Hello, you have got a significant sagging of the mid face due to your weight loss. The correction needs volume replacement. Fillers would do the trick if you want something temporary ( for a year or so). A minimal incision facelift is done through a small incision in the hairline.
