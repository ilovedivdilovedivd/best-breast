Tummy Tuck
Nov 8, 2023
Thank you for sharing your question and picture. For a tummy tuck, the incision length can vary based on your anatomy and the amount of skin to be removed. Typically, it’s from hip to hip, slightly longer than a C-section scar. Surgeons aim to place the scar low enough to be concealed by underwear or a bikini. A higher scar isn’t usual, as the goal is to tighten and remove excess lower abdominal skin. The specifics can be tailored to your body and desires in consultation with a plastic surgeon.
