How long do drains typically stay in after a tummy tuck?
Drains post-tummy tuck
Feb 3, 2019
Congratulations on your tummy tuck, CLayneB!
The length of time drains stay in place depends on your condition and the discretion of your plastic surgeon.
In general, drains stay in place for two weeks. But if there’s a lot of liquid accumulating inside, then it’s okay to extend it for a couple more weeks.
Just follow the instructions of your reputable and board-certified plastic surgeon and you’ll be fine. He knows your condition best so trust his recommendations.
Best of luck!
