Deep plane vs smas

This must be individualized to the patient,

Will the eye shape be changed with a Midface lift (MFL) performed from incision under the eyes?
Removal if excess skin with a midface
Jun 7, 2020
If as you say you have excess skin currently under your eyes, a midface will only contribute more skin to your lower lids as it is a vertical lift. The cheek can be elevated with endoscopic dissection or under direct visualization. The skin is remove through an incision under your lashline. There may be some early changes in the shape of the eye as the lid is often tightened as a component of this procedure. This typically resolves over 6-8 weeks.
Is a midface lift a very “involved” procedure or is it just lifting of the malar fat pad?
What is a midface?
Jun 7, 2020
A midface is a vertical lift. The procedure is performed starting at the area just outside the corner of your eye and then heading along the rim under your eye onto your cheek. The entire cheek is released and elevated and then suspended to the area where the surgery started as well as the temproal region within your hair. Once the midface is elevated, there will be some excess skin contributed to the lower lid which should be conservatively removed with an incision under your lash line.

This is much less involved than surgery and very well may address your concerns.
