What would be the best laser to treat a semi fresh (1 month old) curling iron b
Pulsed dye laser
Nov 9, 2018
I suspect from the photo that this was a deep dermal burn and you have been left with a vivid erythema following healing. Vascular laser will help with the redness and other issues such as itching or sensitivity. I use the pulsed dye laser (585) and see a real benefit in our patients, although this may require several sessions a monthly intervals.
Two weeks after fractional laser. Is this normal? How can I fix this texture??
Fractional Laser redness
Nov 9, 2018
Redness and textural changes in the skin are essentially a normal part of the healing process following fractional laser and this should resolve and fade with time. I often find the use of a mild steroid ointment such as 0.5% or 1% hydrocortisone cream at night helps significantly with these post procedure issues. This should only be used for 3 weeks. Combined with regular use of emollients and I would suggest ‘Biafine’.
