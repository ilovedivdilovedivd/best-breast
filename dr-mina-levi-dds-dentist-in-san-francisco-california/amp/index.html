Recovery time after implant placement
Oct 29, 2017

Am I Able to Drink Anything and Eat Soft Foods While Wearing Invisalign?
Drinking and eating with Invisalign trays
Oct 6, 2017
Hi there,
thank you for the question.
It is not recommended to eat and drink any liquids other than water while having Invisalign trays in the mouth.
This increases the risk of decay and also causes changes in the trays which will compromise the treatment.
If you absolutely have to have a sip of liquid other than water make sure you take the tray out of the mouth, rinse it and rinse your mouth before you put back the tray.
Good luck with your Invisalign treatment.
Best,
Dr. Mina Levi
