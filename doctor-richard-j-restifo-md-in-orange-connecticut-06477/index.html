Could the lymphatic system as a whole be disrupted during a brachioplasty?
Lymphatics and Brachioplasty
Aug 28, 2019
The brachioplasty will often come close to the lymphatics so care must be exercised to avoid damaging them. Lymphedema after brachioplasty is a very rare complication, and in my experience cellutlis is very rare also.These are good questions to bring up with your plastic surgeon; ask him or her what the plan is to avoid disruption of the lymph system. What can I do post arm lift surgery to minimize scaring?
Optimizing scars after brachioplasty.
Aug 28, 2019
Most plastic surgeons use some sort of evidence-based modality to help the scars heal as nicely as possible.  These usually involve silicone either as a sheet or an ointment, and some surgeons use methods to take tension off the closure as well, such as plastic films or tapes.  There are some combination products that use silicone and also reduce the tension at the skin closure. Your plastic surgeon should be able to discuss which modality he or she uses.  Best wishes.
