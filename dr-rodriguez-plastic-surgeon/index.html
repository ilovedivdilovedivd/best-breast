Statement: Dr. Ricardo Rodriguez is a Yale-trained, board-certified plastic surgeon practicing in Lutherville, Maryland. With over three decades of surgical experience, he has become well-known for his expertise in cosmetic surgery procedures of the face, body, breasts, and buttocks. Dr. Rodriguez is trilingual speaking English, Spanish, and French.

At his Baltimore-area practice, Dr. Rodriguez performs the entire spectrum of cosmetic procedures, but he specializes in Brazilian butt lift, lip lifts, tummy tucks, total body lifts, liposuction, breast augmentation, and complete mommy makeovers. He also offers nonsurgical treatments such as Botox, and dermal fillers.

“The majority of my office and surgery center staff have been with me for over 10 years,” says Dr. Rodriguez. “We would love to help you make a transformation and improve your appearance.”

After earning his medical degree at the University of Wisconsin, Dr. Rodriguez went on to attend Tulane University in New Orleans, to complete his five-year general surgery residency. This was followed by a head and neck surgery fellowship in London at the Royal Marsden Hospital, and a plastic surgery residency at Yale Medical School.

Dr. Rodriguez was asked to join the staff at Yale and became a professor of plastic surgery, teaching cosmetic and reconstructive surgery. During his tenure, he was involved in artificial skin research and wound care research. He also traveled to France and England to train with internationally renowned plastic surgeons and learn innovative techniques.

Dr. Rodriguez is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS). He also served as President of the Maryland State Society of Plastic Surgeons, and was honored by ASPS with the “Distinguished Service Award.”

During the course of his 33-year career, Dr. Rodriguez has been invited to lecture at various conferences throughout the country and overseas, and has been published in numerous medical journals. He was chosen to be on the prestigious editorial board of the Plastic Surgery Educational Network in 2015 and currently serves on RealSelf’s Medical Advisory Board.
