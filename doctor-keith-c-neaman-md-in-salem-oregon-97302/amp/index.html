Are infections and seroma after a tummy tuck common and what causes this?
Infections / Seroma
Dec 19, 2022
Both infections and seromas are rare occurrences following a tummy tuck. Seromas can occur when the body isn’t able to handle the amount of fluid the body creates following surgery. Infections on the other hand can occur for a variety of reasons. Both this and infections are usually not a sign of poor surgical technique but a sign of poor patient selection.
I have titanium markers in both breasts and I want to get a breast implants. Is it a problem that I
Titanium Markers
Nov 9, 2018
Generally titanium markers are not a contraindication to undergoing a breast augmentation. They are traditionally used to mark suspicious lesions or areas of concern on mammography. Given that the implants are placed underneath the muscle in a majority of surgeries they should have no impact on the ability to visualize the markers during future mammography.
What will get rid of these dark spots, mask, melasma?
Melasma
Sep 22, 2016
Melasma can be treated using a 4% hydroquinone and retinoid. This works for a few months and then needs to be transitioned to a non hydroquinone skin brightener with retinol. Best of luck.
