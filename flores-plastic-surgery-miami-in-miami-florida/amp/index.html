JODI SCHAEFER, RN

Name: JODI SCHAEFER, RN
Position: Registered Nurse
Can answer: 1
Biography: Registered Nurse, Jodi Schaefer, joined Flores Plastic Surgery in 2015 after she met Dr. Jaime Flores on the volleyball court during a charity event. Jodi immediately jumped at the opportunity to join Dr. Flores’ team as the Registered Nurse Clinician. Her efforts focus on patient education, preoperative preparation of surgical patients and their intricate postoperative care.

Jodi graduated Valedictorian with a B.S. in Nursing from Fortis College and specializes in both the clinical and surgical care of plastic and reconstructive patients. Prior to becoming a registered nurse, Jodi was a Chief Nuclear Medicine Technologist for 15 years. She received her first B.S. degree from the University of Wisconsin, La Crosse in nuclear medicine.
