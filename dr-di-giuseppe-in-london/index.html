Is Male Breast Reduction Painful?
Is Male Breast Reduction Surgery Painful
Aug 6, 2013
Anxiety is completely natural but don’t be afraid. It is not painful. The procedure is carried out under local anaesthetic and is treated as a minimally invasive day case. You can even have sedation if you’d feel better. With a minimally invasive procedure such as VASER, your surgeon can minimise scarring. The cannula would just be used for gentle aspiration of the fat. You may need drains for a day post op, and compression garments for 4 weeks.

Breast Surgery Without Implants?
Breast Augmentation or volume enhancement without Breast Implants
Jul 23, 2013
An alternative option to implants is fat transfer – which is a volume enhancement procedure for breasts. Suitability is an issue here, as you must have enough fat to harvest and transfer for your augmentation, from areas such as your abdomen, flanks, thighs, etc.
Is There a NON-surgical Way to Correct my Breast?
Sagging Breasts – Uplift and Volume Enhancement
Jul 11, 2013
The most suitable treatment for you would be a breast uplift, either with or without implants. As your breasts are saggy and empty, you’ll need the lift, and the implants will give you more volume.
Can Fat Be Transferred from the Upper Arms for Breast Enhancing Fat Transfer Surgery?
Breast Enhancement with Fat Transfer
Jul 11, 2013
In order to achieve satisfactory results with fat transfer, you’d need a fair amount harvested – usually around 200-300ml for each breast depending on the desired volume.

To get a total of 400 to 600 ml of pure fat, we need around 1500 ml f fat extraction from body areas.

Arms don’t tend to have much fat – normally around 300ml, which isn’t enough for breast enhancement.

Results are permanent as long as you maintain weight.
