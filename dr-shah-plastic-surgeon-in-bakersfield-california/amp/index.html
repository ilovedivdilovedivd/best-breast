Do swollen legs increase the risk of DVT?
swollen legs
Nov 26, 2019
Thank you for your question.  Swelling fo your legs can occur due to abnormal accumulation of extracellular fluid in the tissues of the lower extremity. Causes of leg swelling include salt retention, cellulitis, congestive heart failure, venous insufficiency, pregnancy, and medication side effects. It is important that you see a cardiologist before you undergo the procedure for a work up and a clearance.
