Christine A. Hamori, MD

Name: Christine A. Hamori, MD
Statement: Dr. Christine A. Hamori is a board-certified plastic surgeon specializing in facial plastic surgery and body contouring surgery in the Boston area. She is also a leader in the area of vaginal rejuvenation and labiaplasty.

After receiving her MD from Tulane University School of Medicine in New Orleans, Louisiana, Dr. Hamori completed both an internship and residency in General Surgery at Boston University. She then completed a fellowship in Plastic Surgery at the University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia.

“Surgery has always fascinated me,” says Dr. Hamori. “I like the pace and the attention to meticulous detail that is required. In medical school, I could not wait to participate in the operating room. I love that plastic surgery is not limited to one body part, and we can improve both function and external appearance.”

Dr. Hamori is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and a member of both the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons. She is a medical advisory board member for The Plastic Surgery Channel and has been published in Aesthetic Surgery Journal and Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery.

“I enjoy optimizing a patient’s natural beauty,” says Dr. Hamori. “Healthy patients sometimes need help improving their self-esteem, and I can make aesthetic improvements that last a lifetime. If patients are happy, I have accomplished my goal.”
