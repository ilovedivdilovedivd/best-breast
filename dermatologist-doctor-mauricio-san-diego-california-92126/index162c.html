Will my new hair growth fall out after I get PRP?
PRP for Hair Growth
May 2, 2018
If maintenance of PRP for hair growth is not maintained, then hair growth and retention does slow and eventually return to that person’s normal hair loss. Maintenance treatments are recommended once every 6-12 months depending on your own growth response to treatment.
Do you think microneedling will lead to more keloid scars?
Microneedling
May 2, 2018
Microneedling, if done properly, does not lead to more keloid scars. If one does have a history of keloid scars, precautions are taken to ensure the treatment is suitable for their skin type.
