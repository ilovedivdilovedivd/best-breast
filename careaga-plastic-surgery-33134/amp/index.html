Rhinoseptoplasty and filler. Will the filler be an issue if I go under the knife?
Filler would definitely need to be dissolved!
Jun 28, 2022
The actual anatomy of your nose can be seen once the injectable filler has worn off, making rhinoplasty surgery more precise. In most parts, hyaluronic acid fillers stay for several months, but frequently longer in the nose. Asking the doctor who performed the filler about the dissolving time would be best. In general, having had filler in your nose at any point makes healing more unpredictable.
