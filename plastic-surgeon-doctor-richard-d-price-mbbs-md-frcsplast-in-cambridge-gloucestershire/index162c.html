UK surgeon willing to work with larger implants?
Your decision
Jun 5, 2018
Deciding to have big implants is not a problem but the ability to put them in will depend on your natural size, shape, etc. If you are a 32A now, it is unlikely you will get a very big implant inside without the risk of the wound coming apart.
You should also consider the look you are trying to achieve – big implants can look very obvious – which is fine if that’s what you want.
Good luck!
Richard Price

Breast augmentation before our family is complete. Yay or nay?
I would wait
Jul 13, 2017
If you fall pregnant after breast implants, you may find that as your breasts change in pregnancy and then shrink once you have stopped feeding your child, that they change shape completely. It may be that the initial result from a breast augmentation is, to some extent, lost as your breasts return to normal.
