Statement: Dr. Robert T. Heck is a board-certified plastic surgeon at Columbus Aesthetic & Plastic Surgery in Columbus, Ohio. With over more than two decades in practice, Dr. Heck has devoted his career to the science and artistry of plastic surgery. He has become nationally recognized for his impeccable attention to detail, advanced surgical techniques, natural-looking results, and commitment to providing his patients with the best possible care.

Dr. Heck specializes in breast enhancement, tummy tucks, liposuction, and complete mommy makeovers. However, he has expertise in a broad range of procedures for the face and body, including labiaplasty, eyelid surgery, body lifts, facelifts, and neck lifts, as well as nonsurgical treatment options like CoolSculpting and Cootone; injectable wrinkle relaxers like Botox and Dysport; and dermal fillers such as Juvéderm and Restylane; and facial rejuvenation procedures like Ultherapy.

After graduating from the Medical College of Ohio with honors and earning membership in the prestigious Alpha Omega Alpha Honor Society, Dr. Heck remained there for his general surgery residency before completing a two-year plastic surgery residency at the Medical University of South Carolina. During his time in medical school, Dr. Heck was awarded the Marion C. Anderson and Toledo Surgery Society Awards for his academic achievement in surgery.

Dr. Heck went on to complete an aesthetic and breast fellowship at the University of Southern California, training under the highly respected surgeon, Dr. Grant Stevens, MD, FACS. During the fellowship, Dr. Heck refined his aesthetic surgical skills and learned innovative techniques for which he is known today, like the Laser Bra method.

Dr. Heck’s recognitions include America’s Top Plastic Surgeons, Best Doctors in America, Castle Connolly Top Doctor, and RealSelf Top 100. He has also been voted one of Columbus’ best plastic surgeons and received a Prism Award for his expertise in breast surgery.

Dr. Heck is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons. He serves as a board member of the Riverside Outpatient Surgery Center, which provides a safe and relaxed environment for outpatient procedures.
