Can a bridge attach to a dental implant? Will I need another graft and implant?
Not recommended
Nov 22, 2022
It is generally not recommended to bridge an implant to a natural tooth. However, it is possible to attach 2 crowns to an implant... once you are not a grinder or clencher. Your Dr will make that decision based on your specific situation.
Will I need a new bridge after a root canal?
You might need a new bridge.
Nov 22, 2022
There are many variables that go into this question. How long is the bridge? How many units? One of the abutment (supporting ) teeth of the bridge is compromised. Is there decay on the tooth under the bridge? Will the bridge be removed prior to the root canal procedure? If the procedure is done while the bridge is still on your teeth,, it may be difficult to remove all decay. You should plan for the possibility that a new bridge may be needed.
