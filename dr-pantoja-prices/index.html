Implant sizing for breast augmentation

There’s such a small difference between 375 and 400 cc that you’ll be fine either way. I would leave this to your surgeon’s discretion.

I personally put in the larger size if the patient’s tissues can tolerate it, as most patients do prefer to be larger; it’s very unusual for anyone to come back in wishing they were smaller! I suspect that you’ll be happy either way.

650cc Max For My Body?

Over the internet very hard to advise for extra large implant placement. Best is to seek kore in person evaluations.

Implant placement

I would consider sub glandular (above the muscle) placement of a cohesive gel shaped device. If you are very athletic, you may prefer the appearance of the shaped implant.

Lift?

In short, whether or not you get a lift depends greatly upon your surgical goals. If you are content with having a breast that sag, but have increased volume then it appears that you can get away with implants alone.

You lack upper pole fullness which can be corrected with this method (no lift). Please be seen in person by a board certified plastic surgeon to determine what would be best for you.

Too big

“Too big” is a value judgement based on the eyes of the beholder and whether anatomically that is “too big” requires an examination.

Second consultations are helpful when concerns and size issues need be discussed.

Every plastic surgeon’s office is different. You might call the original plastic surgeon’s office to see if they can accommodate your need for a second visit. We do not charge patients for their cosmetic surgical consultation.
In our practice we encourage the undecided and unsure to return again when they are ready to re-evaluate their size selection and have further question answered about the surgery. Having breast implant surgery must be a comfortable marriage of the patient’s expectations and needs with the ability of your surgeon to reach your goal.

These pre-surgery visits are always time well spent.

Breast implants with only a modest increase in cup size.

it’s not uncommon in my practice of a person to request just a modest change in their overall cup size. If you are just looking for a modest increase in breast fullness, there is no reason you must choose larger implants than desired.

Asymmetrical breast.

In my opinion your right breast has a very nice shape, the problem is the left one, and a reduction plus lift is a good choice. Now, careful, because they are going to be asymmetrical anyway, to achieve exactly the same shape as the right one is almost impossible.
There are going to be small differences between them. The goal of the procedure is to reduce the asymmetry, not to make it perfect.

A Lift with Implants possible

There are different types of lifts available. We would need actual measurements to tell which type of lift might be best. Implants can be placed at the same time. The size of implant needed really depends upon your wishes.

Depending upon the type of lift, your breast might be compacted (made firmer). It may have a perkier, but different look than you had before pregnancy. A Board Certified Plastic Surgeon should be able to evaluate you and answer all of your questions.

BREAST ENHANCEMENT

Breast Augmentation is a very personal decision. From your story and photos it appears you would be an excellent candidate. Find a plastic surgeon in your area and explore your options.

Cohesive Gel Implants may not be all you want them to be

Because of your heritage and what sounds like a strong family support system, you may be one of the few patients for whom it is safe to have surgery abroad… and there are some very good plastic surgeons in Brazil.

On the other hand, it is not uncommon to have a breast implant complication after 30 days, and you need to consider what you would do if you did have a problem after your return to The States.

You may find you have a hard time finding a surgeon willing to take care of the problem knowing they weren’t your choice in the first place… Furthermore, many surgeons are unwilling to manage complications of surgery performed outside the US because when something goes terribly wrong as a result of the surgery, the patient may look for an attorney, who will then inform them that the foreign plastic surgeon is beyond their reach- but the Good Samaritan Plastic Surgeon in the US is fair game.

Dr Pantoja Tijuana

Not fair. Give this some thought. The other concern I have is that you are so strongly committed to this particular implant- while even those of us who do a large amount of aesthetic breast surgery are not yet convinced that the cohesive gel implants represent a meaningful advancement for breast augmentation patients.

Many of us are of the opinion that they are inferior to our current gel implants aesthetically. Going all the way to Brazil for this questionable benefit and at possible risk should be something you deliberate on very carefully.

Pointy breasts?

You have a very nice early result! Your body is just getting used to the presence of the implants 500/525 cc implants are pretty large and it will take some time for your body to accommodate to the presence of the implants.
It is very early in your post operative course, and I know it is difficulty, but be patient. Over the next several weeks to 3 months, the implants will descend, the skin of your breast will accommodate to the presence of the implant and the pointiness that you describe will soften.

Again, you have a very ice early result. Massage can help the skin stretch and keep the implant soft, Stay close with your surgeon.,.

Your height and weight do not make it impossible for you to have breast implants. Breast implants are available in a variety of sizes and can be fitted to your particular chest wall dimensions. See a board certified plastic surgeon.

They can interview and examine your and review your options with you.
Would Moderate or High Profile Saline Implants Be Better for my Deflated Breasts?

After reading your text many times i feel only in person evaluation can determine the exact surgery you would be best with. My guess is a lift + HP implants in the 300 to 350 cc range.

The implant alone would most likely give a low riding effect.
