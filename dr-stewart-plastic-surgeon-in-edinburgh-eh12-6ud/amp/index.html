I've noticed my ear piercing has been stretching.
A very common problem
Nov 5, 2019
Stretching of ear piercings is very common. It can be attributed to heavy ear rings, nickel allergy and repetitive pulling. We all see massively enlarged ear piercings caused by stretchers
