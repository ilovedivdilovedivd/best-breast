Dr Gabriel Chiu Plastic Surgery Prices

Silicone gel filled implants are roughly twice the cost of saline filled. Some surgeons may mark up the implant price by the size. So, it is conceivable that you may hear different prices for different sizes.

Silicone feels more natural

There is no question that silicone generally will feel more natural because the density of silicone is closer to the density of fat in the natural breast. however, the more natural breast tissue and fat you have in the chest area, the less important the material in your implant.

– because there is more padding over the implant. i always place my implants under the muscle to decrease the risk of capsule formation and to make them feel more natural.

Silicone or Saline Breast Implants

Both saline and silicone breast implants can provide a natural looking result. The breast augmentation gallery of more than 90 breast augmentation patients includes many examples of both silicone and saline implants. For a woman with thin skin and / or limited breast tissue, silicone breast implants can look more natural as there are no folds or ripples.

To determine what is best for you, a plastic surgeon should provide a consultation. Look at many, many before and after images, check the education and background of your plastic surgeon and then schedule some consultations.

the ideal plastic surgeon for you will really listen to your goals and provide a customized plan to give you results you want.

Implant choices

In my practice I use Allergan and Mentor, smooth, textured and anatomical. The silicone cohesive gel implants have a gummy bear quality and feel very natural to the touch compared with saline. Allergan’s implants are available in Truform 1, 2 and the anatomical’s slightly firmer texture.
I make recommendations based on the patient’s anatomy and expectations. I think it is important that your Plastic Surgeon explain the pros and cons of each choice and recommend the implant that they feel will give you your personal best outcome.

Choose a board certified Plastic Surgeon who specialises in breast augmentation and ask lots of questions at your consultation.

Lifting with implants

If the nipples are below the breast fold, generally a lift will be necessary. If the nipples have descended to the level of the fold, perhaps placing a high profile, cohesive 2, gel implant above the muscle will prevent the need for a lift.

It’s import for your doctor to check if you have enough tissue for adequate implant coverage in this plane .
The best incision for breast augmentation depends on where the implant will be placed

For several years I would put the implants through the tummy tuck incision by tunneling up under the breast. While the results were good it seemed that it was an extreme effort to get a good result.

Now days with newer techniques of abdominalplasty I do not elevate the tissue above the belly button except in the small area between the muscles. Regardless of other procedures I now chose the incision on the location of the implant.

If it is to be placed under the muscle I put the incision in the arm pit. This gives the best most unnoticeable incision there is. It puts the incision directly over the muscle and gives a great result.
If I am going to put the implant under the breast then I use a peri areolar incision which in my hands gives a far better scar than an incision under the breast. I believe that the most important thing for a plastic surgeon to do is to do what ever it takes to give the patient the safest and best results possible.

Breast augmentation question

An in person consultation is required to decide what type,profile and size implant you require. The best size implant for you will be determined by the characteristics of your breasts, chest wall and your desires.

560 mL is a large implant. Larger implants are associated with more postoperative complications following breast augmentation. See a board-certified plastic surgeon for a consultation.

Saline breast implant appearance over the muscle

The answer depends on how much breast tissue you have to start with. If you have very little breast tissue, then you will most likely be able to see the implant and get the “implant” look.

If you have a moderate amount of breast tissue, then you will most likely have a more natural look. Consider placing the implants under the muscle or silicone implants for a more natural look and feel.

After breast augmentation weight 2 by new bras until the implants have dropped into their final position

Following breast augmentation implants can take 6-12 weeks to reach their final resting position. Buying a new bra is difficult because of the changing position of the implants during the first 6 or so weeks after breast augmentation.
If possible I would wait to spend significant money on new bras until your breasts have moved down into a normal position.

Choosing your surgeon

I would encourage you to consider if you feel that you can trust your surgeon. It sounds like you have some doubts. The best experiences come when both the patient and the surgeon have a good line of communications, common goals for outcomes, and generalized trust.

You might consider getting another opinion from a different surgeon in your area. You don’t have to go ahead with a surgeon you don’t feel comfortable with. You’d hate to second guess your decision after surgery if there was anything you weren’t happy about.
What type of bra to wear right after surgery or later?

In our practice, we provide patients with a post op comfortable bra. We see the patients frequently after surgery to help guide them when it is feasible to start wearing an underwire bra. The underwire bra may help create the internal scar that can hold the implant.

I think long term bra wearing (even a little something at night) can be helpful). This may support the skin overlying the implant. But there is no science to this. It is mostly based on experience.

Breast implants

The implants with the highest ripple rate would be textured and saline. The implants that have the lowest ripple rate would be highly filled silicone implants and smooth and placed under the muscle. Depending upon your size you probably have a very low rate of ripple in anyway.
