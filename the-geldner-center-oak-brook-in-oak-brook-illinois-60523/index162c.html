Doctors

Peter D. Geldner, MD

Name: Peter D. Geldner, MD
Statement: Dr. Peter D. Geldner is a board-certified plastic surgeon in Chicago. Dr. Geldner specializes in mommy makeovers, breast enhancement procedures, and tummy tuck, though he offers a wide variety of cosmetic surgeries, including facelift, Ultherapy skin tightening, and nonsurgical body contouring with CoolSculpting and EmSculpt.

The Geldner Center, his private practice, has locations in Hinsdale and in the Chicago metro area, near Grant Park. Dr. Geldner and his staff offer a wide range of aesthetic services, including both surgical procedures,

Throughout his 30-plus-year career, Dr. Geldner has mastered the art of cosmetic and reconstructive plastic surgery. He finds great satisfaction in helping his patients reach their goals and restoring their confidence with life-changing surgical results.

After earning his medical degree from the University of Wisconsin at Madison Medical School, Dr. Geldner enrolled in the general surgery residency program at the University of Chicago, and later began his plastic surgery residency at the same institution. He went on to complete his plastic and reconstructive surgery residency at Wayne State University in Detroit and the University of Texas Medical Branch in Galveston. He returned to Chicago to hone his skills at Michael Reese Hospital, where he ultimately served as the Chief of Plastic Surgery.

Dr. Geldner holds the position of assistant clinical professor in the department of surgery at the University of Illinois at Chicago and clinical instructor at the University of Chicago, department of surgery, section of plastic surgery.

He is certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. Additionally, he’s an active member of the American Medical Association, the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, the American Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, and the Illinois Society of Plastic Surgeons.

He has earned many prestigious awards and titles over the years, including Castle Connolly America’s Top Surgeons, Castle Connolly Top Doctors, and former president of the Chicago Society of Plastic Surgeons. He lends his knowledge and expertise to a variety of media outlets, such as NBC, ABC, Life & Style Weekly, Chicago Magazine, Chicago Tribune, and Larry King Live.
