Should I be concerned with getting the tummy tuck/mommy makeover?
Tummy tuck and SBO
Nov 5, 2019
Even though you have had previous intra-abdominal surgery the tummy tuck is performed superficial to the muscular abdominal wall and would not put the small bowel at risk. Nevertheless, there will be scar tissue in the subcutaneous tissue from the previous surgery and this will make the tummy tuck more difficult. Therefore you will need to be evaluated by a board certified plastic surgeon to determine your eligibility for the surgery.

It may be possible to fill the depression below the area of fullness (tear trough) with filler, effectively eliminating the contour irregularity without surgery. This is easily performed in the office and has instantaneous results.

How much weight should I lose before having a tummy tuck?
BMI
Nov 5, 2019
Your height and weight equates to a BMI of 39. This does not preclude a tummy tuck, but it does put you in a higher risk group for serious complications, especially clots in veins in your legs that can then travel to your lungs. This risk can be decreased with proper medication and stockings worn after surgery. Nevertheless, a complete evaluation would be necessary to determine if you are a candidate for surgery and if there are other risk factors that would need to be addressed.
Do I really need a tummy tuck due to loose skin after a BBL?
loose skin
Oct 24, 2019
It is possible that liposuction will produce some skin laxity. One option to prevent this and avoid a tummy tuck would be to use Vaser liposuction. This uses ultrasonic energy to help remove the fat cells and in the process produces heat that can stimulate skin tightening. The energy level can be set to avoid injury to the fat cells in order to allow adequate fat transfer.
Should I consider full tummy tuck or mini tummy tuck? Or maybe CoolSculpting?
tummy tuck vs. cool sculpting
Oct 24, 2019
Your issue is completely related to skin and muscle laxity, not fat excess. Cool sculpting would do nothing for the skin and muscle laxity, and since there is not significant fat excess it would only remove money from your wallet. A standard mini tummy tuck would not correct skin laxity above the belly button. Therefore, a full tummy tuck, or possible modification such as an umbilical float would be appropriate.
My doctor suggest me to try lipotropic injection instead of abdominoplasty. Does it work?
lipotropic injection vs. tummy tuck
Oct 24, 2019
Non-surgical injections might remove some fat from the abdomen, but would not provide for muscle or skin tightening as seen in tummy tucks. This would also apply to cool sculpting which is a non-surgical procedure and is primarily appropriate for patients with a limited amount of fat and no significant skin or muscle laxity.
How long will it take for my seroma to dry up? 1.5 year post TT.
seroma
Oct 24, 2019
A seroma will only resolve when the fluid is gone and the walls of the cavity adhere to each other. In order to achieve this, the cavity needs to be empty with either the drain in place or frequent aspirations. Allowing fluid to build back up only maintains the seroma. It is possible to inject a sclerosing agent into the seroma to encourage the cavity to scar in.

Vectra "breast width" vs Vectra "breast base width"?
Breast width
