tummy tuck after abdominal surgeries

If I get my umbilical hernia removed, will it mess up my tummy tuck?
umbilical hernia after tt
Apr 14, 2023
It should not affect the tt to have an umbilical hernia repaired afterwards. Ask that the surgeon use the already existing incision around the umbilicus so you dont have any new scarring.
Is a Tummy Tuck the only way to fix my loose skin?
loose skin
Apr 12, 2023
The only truly effective treatment for loose abdominal skin is an abdominoplasty. RF and lasers may improve the skin somewhat but will not be as dramatic as surgery.
1 year 3 months after tummy tuck with lipo & still very tight, will this disappear?
Massage and stretching
Apr 12, 2023
The tightness you are feeling is likely due to some scar tissue developing. Everyone develops differing amounts of scar tissue and you may develop more than the usual person. Having said that the way to combat this is deep massage of the area and stretching/exercise to soften the scar. The more you massage and use the muscles the more collagen will be converted to softer more organized fibrils of collagen and will make the scar more flexible.
Can I safey have a tummy tuck done with a small hernia?
Hiatal Hernia and TT
Apr 12, 2023
I would consider it safe to proceed with abdominoplasty with a small hiatal hernia that your gastroenterologist is comfortable watching. You should take the pantoprazole prophylactically for a week before and several weeks after the procedure to prevent any worsening of reflux due to increased pressure in your abdomen after the TT. Good luck!
Can my hernia and separation be corrected by suturing the muscle together without needing mesh? (phot
No mesh
Apr 12, 2023
There is usually only a need for mesh if the muscle cannot be brought together in the middle during the diastasis repair. This situation is rare, especially in a very fit patient like yourself. I would expect to be able to repair the diastasis which should correct the midline hernia as well. You will have to have a strict lifting limit and avoid any core straining for 6-8 weeks afterward to allow the muscle repair to heal.
Tummy tuck – is it okay to continue taking spironolactone up unto surgery day?
Spironolactone
Apr 12, 2023
It is fine to take it up to the day of surgery and afterwards but you should be sure to stay very well hydrated postoperatively as spironolactone can cause you to be dehydrated.
Can I do Ultherapy on my face, jaw line and neck?
Ultherapy after surgery
Apr 12, 2023
Ultherapy uses ultrasound energy to tighten the skin. This should not cause issues with the plates and screws that are in place. You should be fine to have this treatment.
Can I get breast implants without a lift?
yes
Apr 12, 2023
Your nipple position is still at or above the inframammary fold under the breast which means you can have a nice result without a lift.
Want more volume on the sides and bottom of breasts – can this be achieved with breast filler?
fat grafting to the breast
Apr 12, 2023
The only filler for the breasts is fat. Sometimes it takes several rounds of grafting to get the desired result in breast reconstruction (and augmentation), esp if the patient has very little fat elsewhere that is available for transfer. Continue to see your surgeon and follow up after the procedure.
My consultation was with the coordinator – is that the norm?
coordinator consult

That should not be the norm. You should definitely be evaluated by the doctor who is going to perform your procedure ideally in person. The doctor should examine you to see what is necessary.
What happens if I lose weight after having a deep neck lift?
weight loss after neck lift
Dec 7, 2022
If you have a deep plane neck lift the muscle layer of the neck is tightened along with the skin. The muscle tightening will not be affected by weight loss as there is not a lot of fat below the muscle. Skin can loosen if there is subcutaneous fat (fat between the skin and muscle) that reduces after weight loss.

Yes it can is the bottom line. Numbing cream helps but it does not eliminate the pain but does help. You should be able to tolerate it esp if it is done at a lower setting.
