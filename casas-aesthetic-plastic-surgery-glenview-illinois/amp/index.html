Doctors

Laurie A. Casas, MD, FACS

Name: Laurie A. Casas, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Laurie A. Casas, MD, FACS is a board-certified female plastic surgeon in the greater Chicago area with over three decades experience. Driven by a deep commitment to patient safety and excellent results in cosmetic non-surgical and surgical procedures of the face, breast and body, she forms collaborative partnerships with each patient. A trusted expert in cosmetic and reconstructive plastic surgery, Dr. Casas performs a range of procedures, including mommy makeovers, body contouring, breast augmentation, liposuction, and tummy tucks (abdominoplasty).

Dr. Casas received her medical degree at Northwestern University Feinberg School of Medicine in Chicago where she also completed residencies in general and plastic surgery. To complete her training, she completed three fellowships in aesthetic surgery of the face, breast and body.

A National Spokesperson for the American Society of Plastic Surgeons and the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, she is a fellow of the American College of Surgeons and a Clinical Professor of Surgery, Section of Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery, The University of Chicago Medicine.
