Is it normal for the body to reject the holding stitch after a bilateral endoscopic brow lift?

Dear Intuitive
Thanks for your question. It is very unlikely that your body rejects the sutures as all suture materials undergo extensive research and manufacturing controls to make sure they are “biocompatible”.
