Nov 9, 2017

Lasers work well, but they work well when there is only soft tissue that needs to be changed. If bone has to be reshaped, then the laser will not give you a lasting result. I would suggest an evaluation with a cosmetic dentist in conjunction with any surgical modifications of the gum line.
