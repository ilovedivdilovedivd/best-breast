Jul 31, 2018
Hi kmp323,
Sensitivity after a filling is normal, especially if your cavity was deep.
Sensitivity to cold can even last a few months!
Give it time and notify your dentist if the pain comes on spontaneously or when you sleep at night. That type of pain is usually from a distressed nerve due to a deep filling.
