HI,
The issue you have with the front tooth is decalcification, which means that the area of tooth has lesser calcium crystals during the formation. This usually results in white patchy/ chalky areas on the tooth.
The area is more vulnerable to chipping, pitting and caries formation.
You have the option of dental chair side bonding which is less expensive.
Other options are Dental Veneers or Crowns depending on the area and extent of the decalcification.
