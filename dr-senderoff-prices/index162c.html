Areolar incision

The incision in the breast crease has been shown to have fewer long term complications such as capsular contracture. Trying to put in a gel implant in a small incision even with a keller funnel may increase the risk of complications such as damaging the shell of the implant.

50 year old and implants

I have placed implants in lots of 50 year old healthy women.

Breasts may harden, implants do not

Breast implants themselves do not harden. The envelope of normally soft scar tissue that forms around every implant may thicken and tighten causing the breast to harden. The chance of this hardness occuring is very low, but is slightly higher for silicone gel implants.

Wait at least 3 months after breast feeding before considering breast augmentation

After you have completed breast feeding – its reasonable to wait 3-4 months to allow the breasts to return to a ‘normal’ state. At that time, breast augmentation can be considered. Your surgeon will also discuss if other adjunctive procedures, like a breast lift, will give you an optimal cosmetic outcome.

Implants for droopy breasts

if your breasts are dropping only moderately, sometimes implants alone will provide a nice result. However, if the nipples are near the lowest point on your breasts, they might not get elevated sufficiently with implants alone.

This is why you will require a proper assessment with a PS, to determine which surgery will best meet your expectations.

Breast lift needed?

Dr Douglas Senderoff New York Ny

A general rule concerning the need for a breast lift involves the level of the nipple in relation to the fold under the breast. If the nipple is at or below the level of the fold, you probably would benefit from a lift.

However, lifting involves more scars than simple augmentation. You should decide what works best for you, and then convey this to your plastic surgeon.

Breast Asymmetry is More Common Than Discussed.

Breast asymmetry is one of the most common normal findings in women. It affects up to 85% of women to some extent. This finding is quite normal. Women usually do not seek correction of the asymmetry unless it is moderate to severe.

If asymmetry in breast size, shape or feel occurs abruptly, it would be prudent to have a breast examination by your OB/GYN or a General Surgeon.

You Have Tuberous Breasts

It appears to me that you have a form of tuberous breasts. Here, the base width of your breast is tight and relatively small. Be careful in selecting a breast implant that is wider than the natural width of your breast.
Some lowering of the crease will be necessary to make this all work and, in my experience, if you try and do it too much you run the risk of a double bubble. I would have a frank discussion with your plastic surgeon about what they think the largest size that will fit and avoid this double bubble.

Breast implants take a few months to settle into position

Swelling and high-riding implants are common in the initial postoperative period after breast augmentation Your breasts will usually appear noticeably smaller once the swelling resolves and the implants settle into position.

The swelling should be mostly resolved after a few weeks, but the implants will take 3-6 months to settle into position.
