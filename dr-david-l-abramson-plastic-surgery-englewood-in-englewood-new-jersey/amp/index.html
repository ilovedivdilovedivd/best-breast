Statement: Dr. Abramson is a past president of both the New Jersey Society of Plastic Surgeons and the New York
regional Society of Plastic Surgery. He has been the Chief of Plastic Surgery at Englewood Health for 13 years and has recently completed a term as President of the Medical Staff.

Having published more than 30 articles and book chapters on various topics in Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, Dr. Abramson has become a popular guest lecturer at area hospitals and residency programs on current trends in Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery.

Dr. Abramson as an innovator has described new techniques for facelift, abdominoplasty, breast reduction and breast reconstruction. He is currently the President of the New York Regional Society of Plastic Surgeons and the President-Elect of the New Jersey Society of Plastic Surgery.
