Breast Implants Are Only Replaced When They Have A Problem

While your silicone breast implants may be in the antique range by automobile standards, they appear to be working just like they were new. As long as you are happy with their appearance and feel, and your mammogram shows no suspicion of rupture, there is no benefit to replacing them.

Breast implants do not need to be replaced just because they are old. Most patients will not get 30 or more years of uncomplicated use of an indwelling breast implant, but this is one time it is good to be the exception.

Without the benefit of examining you or knowing specific measurements of your chest wall and breast it is impossible to give you good advice regarding the size of your implants. I suggest scheduling another consultation with your plastic surgeon to discuss your concerns.

If you do not feel comfortable after that consultation you should consider a second opinion.

Breast Lift

You will have the best results with a breast lift. The result without the lift will be sub-optimal and unattractive. Breast lift and augmentation usually does not affect the ability to breast feed, however there is a risk that it could.

So if you are planning on having more children and breast feeding, it would be best to wait until you are finished.

When am I ready

I ask patients to wait 3 months after breast feeding and also I ask them to think about their body and if they think they will change it dramatically with diet and exercise if yes than I will suggest waiting until they are stabilized .
Non surgical breast enhancement

Noninvasive methods to enlarge your breasts are, in my opinion, inferior to traditional breast augmentation procedures utilizing implants. The most common method involves the BRAVA System which is laborious, time consuming, still involves surgery, and has inferior results to traditional breast augmentation.

Taking large amounts of hormones or endocrine type medications can be dangerous and often produce minimal results. Being nervous about undergoing surgery. is understandable. This is why it is incredibly important to sit down and speak with a board certified plastic surgeon who can answer any and all questions you may have regarding the different options available to you so that you may achieve optimal results and your expectations may be met.

I certainly understand.

Can Breast Implants placed through the nipple also be placed under the muscle
Yes, an incision around the nipple can be used for under the muscle placement as long as your nipple diametic is wide enough.

Do you think that I need a lift or will implants under muscle suffice? Scarring scares me!

I think you can obtain a nice natural result with a breast augmentation alone and forego the lift. If at a later point in time your breasts sag further a lift can then be performed.

Wait six months after breast feeding for implants

There are a variety of opinions, but lactation will stop at different times for different individuals, and any breast stimulation, even running can prolong lactation. We suggest at least six months to allow your breast to reach the ‘true’ size, and judge the real skin envelope.
Three months wait? Why rush. Your investment is significant and the downside risks are real.

Downsizing breast implants

If you don’t need a lift currently, it is unlikely you will need one if you go down a cup size. However, it is impossible to say for sure without pictures.

Breast Augmentation – Size Selection

“Tissue based planning” relies on your chest and breast measurements and is the safest way to ensure good long term results. Your surgeon should determine what size and projection are necessary to give you your desired result based on your body’s measurements.

The best way to assess and give true advice would be an in-person exam. Please see a board-certified plastic surgeon that specializes in aesthetic breast surgery.

Lift vs augmentation alone for drooping breasts

Dr Sergio Alvarez Miami

It seems as though you have adequate skin with inadequate volume. I would recommend augmentation alone to start with (save the money and the scars that attend a lift (mastopexy)) and see how you look.

I think adding volume alone will adequately improve your skin/volume proportionality. After the augmentation wait 3-6 months and then decide if you want a lift. This approach burns no bridges and potentially saves you the cost and scarring of a lift.

In my practice I tell patients in your position that if a lift is necessary I will discount the price so that your total expense is the same whether you do the lift at the same time or later.

They will not look normal if you just have the implants removed

Dr Sergio Alvarez Plastic Surgeon

After removing large implants that have been there for years, you will experience severe sagging and most surgeons will recommend a lift at the same time.

I personally refuse to remove large implants without a lift unless the patient does not have much sagging and has enough breast tissue left which is rare and you already have a lot of sagging as you described.

you can also try a smaller implant below the muscle along with the lift because a lift alone rarely provides much upper pole fullness.

Needing a lift as well as implants?

You should have a great result with the combination of a lift and an augmentation. The scars will fade with time and you will be happier with the shape that results.!.
Going to Brazil for cohesive implants

I agree with the assessment of the other plastic surgeons. The are many well trained plastic surgeons in Brazil and if you have family there and can stay for at least two weeks during your post op recovery and save money, it may be a good choice for you.

The “gummy bear implants” or cohesive gel implants are not as yet FDA approved in the US. They have been available in Europe and South America for some time now. The are some advantages (no rippling) and some disadvantages of these implants.

They are more firm and require a much longer incision than the saline or the gel implants currently available in the USA. The new cohesive gel implants available in the US are more pliable and are quite good.
Another consideration is to think of what would happen if you had a delayed post op problem such as seroma, infection, capsular contracture or asymmetry. Would you be able to seek treatment from a local plastic surgeon (who may not agree to treat you), or would you have to hop a plane and go back to Brazil?

Which surgeon to choose

The anatomy of your breast make your case a bit more difficult than the typical breast augmentation. If your surgeon did not mention the lower portion of your breast being a little bit tight and the need to perform maneuvers to expand the lower breast, and potentially lift and reduce the nipple, I would go with the other surgeon.

In fact, do not be in a rush to get the procedure done on a timeline. Much more important is choosing the right surgeon, so why not make a few more consultations?!
