Doctors

Kahlil Andrews, MD

Name: Kahlil Andrews, MD
Statement: Dr. Kahlil Andrews is a double-board-certified plastic surgeon with over a decade of experience based in Coralville, Iowa. Though he performs a wide range of procedures across the face, breast, and body, he is best known for outstanding outcomes performing mommy makeover plastic surgery, breast enhancement, and abdominoplasty. Dr. Andrews combines great technical skill with his background in the arts to create beautiful, natural-looking results. “I understand how important it is to look and feel your best at any age, and no matter what condition you have,” he says.

The Los Angeles native received his MD from UC San Francisco. After finishing his residency in general surgery, Dr. Andrews pursued his MBA at UCLA, then completed a fellowship in plastic surgery, his ideal blend of art and science. He holds board certifications in general and plastic surgery. In addition to treating patients at his practice in City Center Square, Dr. Andrews also operates at Compass Memorial Healthcare in Marengo, Iowa.
