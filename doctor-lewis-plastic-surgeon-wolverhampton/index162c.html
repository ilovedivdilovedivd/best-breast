Why is an embolism more likely to happen with a Tummy Tuck?
Blood Clots After Surgery
May 23, 2021
Hello,
Blood clots (DVT and PE) can occur after any surgery or period where you are not as mobile. A tummy tuck is a bigger operation with a longer recovery period than a breast augmentation so the risk is statistically higher. It can also depend on your own general health and body weight etc.
If you are a worried, have a chat with your surgeon about their DVT/PE risk prevention policy – they should have one.
Good luck.
Will I be able to take care of myself after a tummy tuck?
Recovery After Tummy Tuck
May 23, 2021
Hello,
I personally think that you would find it difficult being on your own in a foreign country after a tummy tuck operation. It’s a big operation and complications do occur, even with the the best surgeons and hospital facilities.
Once back in the UK, if you have problems, you may have to seek out a local surgeon at additional cost if anything goes wrong.
It would be best to clarify with your surgeon what aftercare package they and the hospital are able to provide to suit your situation.
