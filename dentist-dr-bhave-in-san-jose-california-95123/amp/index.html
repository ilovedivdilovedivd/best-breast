Dental Implant Installed on an Angle – what can I do to fix?
Correction of angled implants
Jan 24, 2018
Hello,
If the implant was placed at an angle, the angle can be corrected by using angled or custom abutments. I would recommend doing a zirconia abutment and a cement retained crown for any front tooth as it is more esthetic, especially if the implant was angulated. Please see your dentist to discuss these options.
I need information about implants. Is it possible to do them after I had surgical removal of my
Dental implants as a treatment option
Jan 24, 2018
Hello,
Yes, it is definitely possible to place dental implants after teeth have been extracted. Please see an implant specialist for a consultation. They will take a 3D X-ray to check if you have adequate bone to place the implants, whether you need any bone grafting and plan the position of the implants. The cost will be based on the number of implants and the type of crowns you will receive.
Can veneers fix my crooked teeth? Am I a candidate for veneers on those two teeth?
Veneers for crooked teeth
Jan 9, 2018
Hello,
I believe you can consider veneers to fix your lateral incisors. I recommend doing veneers on the front six teeth at least since this will give a better esthetic result. The teeth will have to be reduced more than they would if you first bring them into the correct position with braces or Invisalign and then do veneers. The dentist can show you a diagnostic wax-up and determine ahead of time how much reduction will be required in order to give you the smile you want.
Cold sensitivity in implant. Is this something to be concerned about?
Cold sensitivity to implant
Jan 9, 2018
Hello,
Implants do not have any nerve endings and hence there is no sensation with them. I suggest that you have your dentist evaluate the adjacent teeth and even the lower teeth. Sensitivity could be coming from any tooth in that area.
