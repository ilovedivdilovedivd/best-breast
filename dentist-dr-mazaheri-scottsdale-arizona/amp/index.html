When you do either bonding or veneers and need your teeth whitened, aren’t you supposed to wait a
Teeth whitening
Oct 7, 2017
After  teeth whitening (such as Zoom in office whitening) we advised our patients to wait about 7-10 days to take a final shade for their cosmetic procedures. We sometimes also proceed with the procedure (e.g. Veneers, Crowns) if patient is on a time crunch, then do the whitening while patient is in temporary stage and take a shade in 7-10 days and call the shade to the lab. Please consult with a experienced cosmetic dentist to evaluate all of your options.

Which is best for All-on-4, acrylic or porcelain?
Zirconia Vs. Acrylic
Oct 7, 2017
Most of the all on four prosthesis are made of acrylic, however many patients do upgrade to the Zirconia. At our office we do both and the Zirconia although is stronger and more aesthetic it is considerably a heavier prosthesis. Most patient won’t notice the difference in weight.
