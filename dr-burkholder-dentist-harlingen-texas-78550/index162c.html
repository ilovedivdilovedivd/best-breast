How is better to make porcelain crowns one by one, or tie multiple crowns together?
Splinting Crowns Together
Apr 21, 2017
Jasti, The only reason you would want to splint crowns together is if the teeth were mobile or slightly loose. This treatment is considered as a last resort to save teeth, but can be risky as you could lose one of the teeth, and the crowns may have to be remade. If your teeth are solid and without periodontal disease, then the crowns should be made separately. Please see your dentist and ask their opinion.
Zirconium vs. Titanium. Which would you recommend?
zirconium vs titanium
May 9, 2015
I assume that you are referring to dental implant abutments. Zirconium which is white, is usually used in the esthetic zone, that is, where you can see when you are smiling in the front. If titanium is used, sometimes you can see the metal through the gum, or if the gum recedes over time you will see metal. Titanium is stronger. So, we use titanium on the back teeth areas where there is more force with chewing.
