Breast implant removal and lift under local or general anaesthesia – which one is safer?
General or local anaesthetic
Oct 21, 2023
Thank you for the question and this is a very common concern among many patients. The risks from general anaesthetics are usually very low these days especially if you are in good health. For implant removal and an uplift we would usually recommend a general anaesthetic since this can be a long procedure. If any capsule work is required during the removal then a general anaesthetic is again preferred to reduce pain or discomfort.
Will Emsculpt affect my hip and butt fillers?
Fillers and Emsculpt
Oct 13, 2023
Thanks for the question. There is a possibility that the Emsculpt treatment may break down the fillers you have had. Emsculpt Neo also uses heat to shrink fat and this can also contribute to breaking down the fat.
Should I have FaceTite or thread lift?
Threads or facetite
Oct 13, 2023
Thanks for the question. I would firstly say that each technique works differently and so will give different results. A threadlift may be useful for improving jowls or sagging skin. Facetite works by shrinking fat and causing skin contraction. If you have some fat then Facetite may help with this whilst threads will not.

Can I get an implant without a lift?
To lift or not to lift
Oct 6, 2023
Thanks for posting this question and your photos. We always ask what your expectations are from the surgery.
