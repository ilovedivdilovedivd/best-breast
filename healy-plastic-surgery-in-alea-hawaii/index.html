Jeff T. Healy, MD

Name: Jeff T. Healy, MD
Statement: Dr. Jeff T. Healy, MD is a double-board-certified plastic surgeon and the founder of Healy Plastic Surgery, serving the great Honolulu, Hawaii region. With two decades in practice, Dr. Healy specializes in plastic surgery of the face, breast, and body. He is particularly noted for his excellence in tummy tucks (abdominoplasty), breast enhancements—including breast augmentation, lifts, reduction, and reconstruction—and complete mommy makeovers.

Dr. Healy strongly believes that aesthetic surgery requires a collaborative approach, to achieve the best results. “I provide a unique approach to each patient’s individual concerns, and together we arrive at the best possible solution for their needs,” he says. “From the first consultation until the final postoperative visit, I believe in a relationship built on mutual trust and respect.”

Dr. Healy earned his medical degree from Tulane University School of Medicine in New Orleans, Louisiana. He went on to complete an internship in General Surgery at Madigan Army Medical Center in Tacoma, Washington, followed by a residency in General Surgery at Tripler Army Medical Center in Honolulu, Hawaii and a fellowship in Plastic Surgery at the Mayo Clinic in Rochester, Minnesota.

Today, he is double board certified by both the American Board of Surgery – General and the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He is also a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ASAPS) and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS).

Not happy with my recent breast augmentation. 2 months post op. I can still fit my old bras. Should I
Breast Augmentation Revision
May 29, 2019
Two months is still too early to judge the final results.  The implants are still settling and rounding out at this point.  I would advise you to wait longer before undergoing surgery again.  We take out first post-op photos at 3 months after surgery and tell our patients it can take up to a full year to get the final results.  I wouldn’t focus so much on a particular bra size, but instead try to achieve the look that you want and is realistic. 
Is yellow skin at 2 weeks post op normal healing?
Healing after a Tummy Tuck
Apr 8, 2019
A tummy tuck is a big operation and there are different stages of healing that each patient will go through.  At 2 weeks you are still very early in the healing process.  The yellow color you see on your skin is most likely the last stages of the bruising as it fades away.  This is very normal.  You should always follow-up with your surgeon so he/she can assess the progress of your recovery.  Follow your surgeon’s recommendations and you should have an uneventful recovery.
