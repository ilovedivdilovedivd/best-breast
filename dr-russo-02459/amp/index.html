Do facelifts and neck lifts turn out worse when the patient has had RF microneedling previously?
facelift
Feb 20, 2020
Anything that causes injury and subsequent inflammation in the skin can cause scar tissue to form. This definitely makes the surgery more difficult, but surgeons are used to working under these circumstances, and it should not affect your result

Feb 20, 2020
In general, once a woman loses fat from her face, it is difficult to gain it back despite weight gain. It is not uncommon to have filler placed to regain that youthful look
