Will a light chemical peel remove hair follicle from face???
Chemical Peels and Hair Removal
Jun 14, 2017
A light chemical peel will not remove any hair or destroy/remove the hair follicle. The only treatments to destroy the hair follicle would be Laser Hair Removal or Electrolysis. Some treatments like Dermal-planning can shave the hairs, but they will quickly return as the follicle was not destroyed or affected in any way.
I had laser treatment 2 months ago. I can see facial hair. Can I shave them as I can't have laser n
Shaving with Laser Hair Removal
Jun 14, 2017
Yes, you can shave between treatments and while you are on hold for treatments. You would just want to make sure you aren't plucking, waxing or using hair removal creams if you are going to start your treatments again. The laser goes after the root of the hair and other methods of hair removal (plucking, waxing, threading etc.) will remove the root and there will be nothing for the laser to treat when you restart your sessions.
