Is it bad to have had a bit of foundation on the face while having laser hair removal treatment?
Laser hair removal
Jun 11, 2016
I would advise against having anything on your skin during laser assisted hair removal. If your foundation has any color to it then it is possible that the laser could interact with the foundation and not with the hair follicles (since pigment is the ultimate target of the laser). If this interaction occurs then superficial burns may result.

Would laser resurfacing treatment help with my mild acne scarring and hyperpigmentation?
Laser for acne and acne scars
Nov 2, 2015
A combination of lasers would most likely be your best option to improve the general appearance of your skin and to help address scarring and reduce active breakouts. I would suggest a fractionated resurfacing laser with simultaneous pulsed dye laser. A series of 1-3 treatments may be necessary for optimal end result. Good luck.

What filler is the best to treat very deep nasolabial fold ?
Long lasting fillers
Oct 19, 2015
I also like Radiesse as a filler for the nasolabial fold area. Radiesse gives a very natural appearance and may last upwards of one year. I have been injecting Radiesse for about 12 years now and most patients report that after a couple injections over a year or more that the filling effects persist much longer. Radiesse stimulates new collagen formation and this partially contributes to the filler lasting longer and longer over time. Good luck.
