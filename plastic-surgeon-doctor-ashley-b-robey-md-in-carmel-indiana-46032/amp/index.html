Can I still get the Morpheus 8 if I took Ibuprofen a couple days before the procedure?
Morpheus 8 and Ibuprofen Use
Sep 12, 2023
Taking ibuprofen around the time of a minimally invasive procedure like Morpheus can increase your risk of bruising and swelling. The main concerns are aesthetic. However, taking this medication around the time of surgery could increase your risk of bleeding, specifically the kind of bleeding that could require a return trip to the operating room.

But, yes, the bruising and swelling would eventually go away; the amount of those side effects would be potentially more pronounced.
