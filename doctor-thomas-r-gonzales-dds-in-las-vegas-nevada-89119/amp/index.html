All on Four process?
All on Four Process
Jan 7, 2016
The All on Four procedure will require time for the implants to integrate into the bone to be completely stable. This time usually take approximately 6 months from the placement of the implants to the delivery of the final prosthesis. During that time, you will have a temporary prosthesis placed, which will be replaced at the end of the procedure. Can I get the All-on-4 implants only on top?
Single Arch All on 4
Jan 7, 2016
In a very simplified-generalized answer, yes you can get a single arch all on 4. For a complete answer you would need to have a full complete exam and ensure that you are a candidate for an all on 4 procedure. A dentist would like to know if your opposing teeth are healthy enough to function against a hybrid prosthesis and if it is a possible treatment to place an all on 4 prosthesis.
Is this tiny screw the entire implant that came out of my mouth?
Implant screw
Dec 31, 2015
The part that fell out of your mouth is most likely a healing abutment. It is used to cover the implant to protect it. They fall out occasionally and just need to be tightened back on. It is not something to be overly concerned about but make sure you see the dentist soon to get it replaced.
