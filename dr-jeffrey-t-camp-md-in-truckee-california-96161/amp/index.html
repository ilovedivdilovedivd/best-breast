Long-term Effects of Coolsculpting?
Fat Cells
Jul 24, 2017
Both Coolsculpting and Sculpsure reduce around 22% of fat in an area per treatment. Once those fat cells are gone they don’t grow back however the remaining fat cells in the area can enlarge if caloric intake exceeds expenditure. They usually enlarge, however, proportionally to the other fat cells in the rest of the body.
