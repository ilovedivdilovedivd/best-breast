Statement: Dr. John LoMonaco is a double-board-certified plastic surgeon in Houston, Texas, with over 22 years in practice. He specializes in breast enhancement and body contouring, such as breast lift, tummy tuck, arm lift, thigh lift, liposuction, and lower body lift, with a special emphasis on post-bariatric surgery body lifts.

At his personalized practice, the goal is always quality, not quantity. “Our motto is that our body of work speaks for itself,” says Dr. LoMonaco. “Each and every patient must stand out as an example of our best work.”

Dr. LoMonaco earned his medical degree at the University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston. From there, he went on to complete nine years of postgraduate training in general and plastic surgery, at major hospitals in the Texas Medical Center, including Memorial-Hermann Hospital and M.D. Anderson Cancer Institute, participating in over 5,000 surgical procedures during this time.

As a recipient of The Thomas R. Cronin Fellowship in Plastic Surgery, Dr. LoMonaco performed laboratory research aimed at developing skin substitutes for burn patients.

After graduation, he served as assistant professor of plastic surgery at the University of Texas Medical School at Houston, as well as director of plastic surgery at the LBJ Hospital for two years.

Dr. LoMonaco is certified by both the American Board of Surgery and the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He is also a fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS) and an active member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS) and Weight Loss Surgery Foundation of America (WLSFA).
