Can loose skin be fixed with exercise or will I need a tummy tuck to repair the
Loose tummy skin and exercise
Nov 27, 2019
Exercise is of most benefit to your overall health and well-being. Unfortunately, loose skin and gapping of the rectus abdominis muscles (diastasis recti) cannot be completely corrected without surgery. An abdominoplasty (tummy tuck) will help remove the excess skin and tighten the muscles too. I would recommend seeing a board certified surgeon for a full review. Good luck with your journey.
