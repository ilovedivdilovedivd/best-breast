What bra should I wear?
What bra should I wear?
Mar 17, 2017
Dear Jess,
I advise my patients to wear a comfortable, well-fitting non-wired bra for 6 weeks, as much as possible. As you are now 4 weeks, you may benefit from a further 2 weeks in such a bra. It will help to support your augmentation as the soft tissues settle down. After 6 weeks, I would advise that you could start to wear wired bras, but use the non-wired bra for comfort if you want to. I would definitely advise a sports bra for exercise and gym work in the long-term.
