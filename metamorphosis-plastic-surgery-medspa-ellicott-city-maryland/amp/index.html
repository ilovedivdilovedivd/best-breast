Daniel P. Markmann, MD

Name: Daniel P. Markmann, MD
Statement: With a wealth of experience spanning over 25 years as a board-certified plastic surgeon, Dr. Daniel P. Markmann is deeply dedicated to providing a comprehensive array of plastic surgery procedures to the residents of the Baltimore Metropolitan area. Employing state-of-the-art technology and advanced surgical methodologies, he extends unique and impactful cosmetic solutions to uplift his patients' appearances and bolster their self-confidence.

Born and raised in Pennsylvania, Dr. Markmann pursued his academic journey, earning a bachelor of arts degree in biology from LaSalle University and a medical degree from Jefferson Medical College at Thomas Jefferson University. Following the attainment of his degrees, Dr. Markmann embarked on his medical career, completing his internship and residency at Union Memorial Hospital in Baltimore. Additionally, he underwent two fellowships specializing in plastic surgery at John Hopkins University in Baltimore and Rush University.

Dr. Markmann maintains active membership in various esteemed plastic surgery societies, including the American Society for Plastic Surgeons, the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, and the John Staige Davis Society of Plastic Surgeons of Maryland.

For specialized, professional plastic surgery procedures such as abdominoplasty, breast augmentation, rhinoplasty, and post-weight loss surgery, Dr. Markmann stands as the premier choice. The paramount objective of our practice is to ensure that patients are not only satisfied but enamored with the transformative and enhanced results they achieve.
