Why do my 480cc UHP implants seem small?
Don’t panic!
Feb 10, 2019
It is way too early to panic about size. You actually chose pretty large implants for your frame! It does not look like your body would have been able to handle much larger anyway. Please sit tight and let the surgical swelling go down, allow your skin to stretch, and thenimplants to “settle”. The pec muscle is very swollen now and distorts the result. I always tell my patients that your breasts will look like “two weird headlights” for a while. Be patient.

Will Medicare pay for breast implant removal if ruptured?
Insurance for implant removal
Apr 16, 2018
Generally speaking insurance will not cover removal of implants that were placed for cosmetic purposes. Depending on the situation is MAY be possible to remove a ruptured saline implant under local anesthesia in an office setting, but it is not likely that a ruptured silicone implant can be safely or effectively removed in an office setting.
