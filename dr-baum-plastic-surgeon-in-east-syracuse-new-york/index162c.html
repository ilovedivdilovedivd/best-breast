Does stopping Botox do more damage than never having it?
Does stopping Botox do more damage than never having it?
Jul 14, 2021
This is a very common question I get from our patients. You will not have more wrinkles as you get older if you start Botox and then stop. Stopping Botox usage does not result in more “damage” than if you never started using it. It typically lasts 3-4 months so if you get it once per year then most of the year your wrinkle muscles will be working normally. I hope this helps.

Should I get permanent mesh (such as prolene ethicon mesh) or galaflex for bottoming out?
Should I get permanent mesh (such as prolene ethicon mesh) or galaflex for bottoming out?
Feb 13, 2021
I think there are advantages to each type of mesh but Galaflex is certainly the most common at this time because it provides structural support yet it eventually gets absorbed. We hope that your body will form new scar tissue around your implant so that as the Galaflex gets absorbed, the new scar capsules will hold your implants in the correct position. Theoretically, there’s less chance for infection with absorbable mesh. I hope this helps. Best wishes on your revision surgery.
