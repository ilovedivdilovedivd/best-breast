Average Cost For Female Breast Implants

Average Breast Implant surgery costs range from $4,000 to $6,500 depending on the style of implant in my San Antonio, TX clinic. Saline cost between $4,000-$4500, Silicone cost $5,000-$5,500 and the new 410 Gel “Gummy Bear” Implant costs $6,500-$7,000. My costs include everything (facility, Board Certified Anesthesiologist, Physician, Garment, and Implants).

Total costs can vary depending on where you live. Don’t let price be the only factor when deciding on your surgeon. Make sure your Plastic Surgeon is Board Certified in Plastic Surgery.

I would also recommend looking at their before/after pictures, patient reviews, and asking friends or family who they may recommend from a firsthand experience.

Lastly, make sure you like your Plastic Surgeon. You need to feel comfortable with the surgeon and trust that they have your best interest in mind. (Bernard Kopchinski, MD, FACS, San Antonio Plastic Surgeon)

Average breast implant cost Mexico

Be certain that the quoted fee includes the surgeon, implant, anesthesia, and facility fees at a minimum. Other fees to be aware of are prescription medications, garment such as a sports bra and other specialty items such as a Keller funnel which your surgeon may use. These can all add up! Once you have the full price in hand, other things to consider when choosing your surgeon is how much he or she charges for revisionary procedures if needed. For example, ask your surgeon or patient coordinator how much it would be out of pocket if you were to develop a complication such as a capsular contracture. Cost of Breast augmentation

Breast augmentation costs between 4500 to 7000 depending on the difficulty of your breasts and whether you want saline or silicone implants.

Difficult breasts include breasts that are sagging, are asymmetric, or have breast mound deformities. (Arian Mowlavi, MD, Orange County Plastic Surgeon)

Breast augmentation costs vary from region to region, and even within a regional marketplace. The cost of any surgical procedure includes the cost of the surgeon’s time (Surgeon’s fee), the cost of supplies (in this case, breast implants), the cost of anesthesia (particularly if general anesthesia and an anesthesiiologist or nurse anesthetist is used) and the costs of the facility in which the surgery is performed.

In South Florida, breast augmentation (assuming no lift is required) ranges from about $4500-6000. Silicone implants may cost 200-300 dollars more per breast compared to saline implants, mostly reflecting the higher cost charged to the surgeon by the manufacturers.

Presumably, it costs more to make a silicone gel-filled implant than a saline implant. Simultaneous breast lifting (mastopexy) increases the costs due to added surgeon’s time, added facility OR time, and added anesthesia time.

This can add another 2000-3000 to the total cost in some cases. There are many types of breast lifts of varying complexity. (Tim A. Sayed, MD, FACS, Boca Raton Plastic Surgeon)

Cost of breast implant surgery

Across the country the prices for breast implant surgery very quite significantly. Breast augmentation surgery costs have 4 parts, or components. The surgeon’s fee, anesthesiologist’s fee, implants cost, costs of the facility are 4 components of breast implant surgery costs.
