Hard lumps and neck bands 6 months after chin liposuction - what can I do?
Massages and heat therapy
Jan 16, 2023
Greetings, it's not uncommon to develop fibrosis after the procedure and even though typically at this stage they should have resolved on their own it's important to keep in mind that everyone heals differently so I highly advise you do heat therapy like ultrasound or radio-frequency at a certified spa or masseuse to help soften the scar tissue and aid in your recovery.
Am I candiate for buccal fat removal OR massateur botox?
Buccal fat pad removal
Jan 13, 2023
Upon seeing your pictures you are a candidate for the buccal fat pad removal, the procedure will make your mid cheeks area slimmer and highlight your cheekbones. In my experience it's very difficult for a patient with a round/square face to have a gaunt result because the fat removed isn't enough to dramatically change your features. Either way my advise is to always do a lot of research and to only move forward once you're completely sure that the procedure is a match for you.
Will using lipo foam under chin strap deliver better results from chin liposuction?
Beneficial
Sep 30, 2022
Lipo foams help evenly distribute the compression so it's beneficial to use them under your garment; not many patients decide to add them since they are bulky and add volume to the area but if you do not mind that they will definitely help your healing.
How to reverse a bad bichectomy?
Fat graft
Aug 29, 2022
The procedure can be reversed by having fat taken from a sterile site such as abdomen or thighs and placed in the area. I would highly suggest you search for a PS that specialize in facial procedure so that they can determine the best way to approach and correct your case.
Weak chin & double chin - would only fat chin augmentation make a huge difference?
Chin lipo
Aug 27, 2022
