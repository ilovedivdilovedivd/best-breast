Can my teeth been saved? i lost all my back teeth and now my front teeth are loose. I am in pain mo
Loss of back teeth leads to bite collapse
Nov 26, 2018
The back teeth serve to support the bite and the jaws.  If they are lost, the front teeth receive all the pressure when you close your teeth together.  This can lead to the loosening of the front teeth.  It is difficult to know what the condition is of your front teeth but I would suggest that you seek the help of a very knowledgeable and experienced dentist to restore the bite in the back of your mouth and assess and treat the issues in the front of your mouth.
