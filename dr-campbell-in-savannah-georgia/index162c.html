Breast Asymmetry after Breast Augmentation
Apr 22, 2020
It is not abnormal for slight asymmetries after a breast augmentation. The implants will settle at different rates and can be alarming at times for patients. Give it a few weeks and then re-evaluate. However, if you had these asymmetries before surgery then you will likely have some asymmetries after surgery as well.

Breast Augmentation
Apr 17, 2020
Loss of a portion of the fat grafts is a very common occurrence after fat grafting (as high as 50% is common). Fat transfer for breast augmentation is definitely more subtle than with implants. Typically you would want to wait until everything is healed which would usually be roughly 6 months from your last surgery.
