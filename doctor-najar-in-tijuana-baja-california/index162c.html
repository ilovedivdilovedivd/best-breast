What causes lumpy stomach lipo?
lipo on stomach
Feb 24, 2023
It’s imperative that liposuction on your stomach is done right, both technique and recovery. If you get lumps it’s very hard to remove, but vaser technology has been know to help address.
Is it safe to get Liposuction & J-Plasma together?
J plasma and Lipo
Feb 24, 2023
J plasma and Liposuction are safe together, but in my opinion you should reverse the order and consider doing a tummy tuck then lipo.
Why are most doctors against liposuctioning the upper abdomen?
ab liposuction
Feb 24, 2023
If you’re doing a tummy tuck it’s in the patients best interest not to lipo the upper abdomen to avoid skin necrosis due to lack of blood flow. If you are doing lipo only, upper ab liposuction should not be an issue.
