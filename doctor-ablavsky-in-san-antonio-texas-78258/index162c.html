Do I need to have a combination reduction and lift for a lifted breast?
Difference between breast lift and breast reduction.
May 15, 2023
The technique and scar placement is similar for breast reduction and breast lift procedures. Both narrow the breast, and raise the nipple and overall breast itself into a higher position on the chest wall. Although both procedure reduce the overall volume and weight of a breast, a breast reduction typically removes more than 250 grams of weight from each breast and can be greater depending on the goals.
