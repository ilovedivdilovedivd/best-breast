Oct 30, 2023
From you photo, you do not need an extended tummy tuck. The incision should go just across your lower abdomen. It should take out and be a little longer than your C-section scar. To treat the skin above the belly button, if you did a mini-TT you would need to "float" the belly button which would put it in a slightly lower position.
