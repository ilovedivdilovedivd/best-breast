Doctors

Bradley L. Ashpole, MD

Name: Bradley L. Ashpole, MD
Statement: Dr. Bradley Ashpole is a double board-certified plastic surgeon with offices in Libertyville and Schaumburg, Illinois. He is best known in the Chicago area for his expertise in breast augmentation, mommy makeovers, and body contouring after significant weight loss. He also offers a variety of effective facial rejuvenation procedures, including Morpheus8 RF microneedling, chemical peels, and dermal fillers.

Dr. Ashpole’s guiding philosophy is to help his patients feel confident in achieving their cosmetic goals by ensuring they feel heard, informed, and supported throughout their experience.

After attending medical school at the St. Louis University School of Medicine in St. Louis, Missouri, Dr. Ashpole completed both an internship and residency in general surgery at Rush University in Chicago. From there, he headed to Albert Einstein College of Medicine at Montefiore Medical Center in Bronx, New York for a residency in plastic and reconstructive surgery before returning to Rush University for a fellowship in hand surgery.

Dr. Ashpole is board certified by both the American Board of Plastic Surgery and the American Board of Surgery – General. He is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons and has been named one of Castle Connolly’s Regional Top Doctors as well as Chicago Magazine’s Top Doctors in 2021. He was also recognized for his teaching accomplishments, with an induction into the prestigious Leo M. Davidoff Society.

Can liposuction surgery remove lipomas and love handles?
Lipoma and love handles.
Feb 9, 2020
Thank you for your question. Liposuction is an excellent way to help shrink love handles. A lipoma, which is a well defined fatty “tumor”, is best treated with surgical excision. It is best to visit a board certified plastic surgeon who can accurately diagnose your problem and offer you options for treatment.
