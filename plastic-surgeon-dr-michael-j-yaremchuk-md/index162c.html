Chin
Aug 20, 2020
The chin should lay just posterior to the bottom lip on the profile view. Your chin certainly extends far anterior to this bringing disharmony to your face. Botox will not correct this. A chin reduction of the actual bone is what you need to bring more harmony to your face.
Moderate plus or high profile? Hoping to get between 200cc-235cc.
Implants
Aug 20, 2020
Your implants are designed to sit behind the breast. The surgeon should begin by measuring the base width of your breast and pick implants based on this base width. Then, differing volumes can be accomplished by adding or taking away projection. Many surgeons have implants that you can try on in the office to give you at least some impression of what differing sizes would look like on you.
