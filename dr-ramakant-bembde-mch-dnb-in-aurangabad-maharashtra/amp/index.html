What Can I Expect From Mole Removal Recovery By Excision?
Mole removal by excision
Mar 3, 2019
Mole removal by elliptical excision is a standard method used by plastic surgeons. Generally the wound heals in a week. The scar of excision in longer than the diameter of the mole. The scar maturation takes six months to one year to look better. If needed CO2 fractional laser can be used for the scar for better cosmetic appearance.
