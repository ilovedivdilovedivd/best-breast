Jul 3, 2018
It is true that implants can lift sagging breasts…. to a certain degree. Try the pencil test: Put a pencil in the fold under your breasts. If they stay there you will need a breast lift to correct the “drop.” If the pencil falls out, an implant may give the lift you desire. D cup breasts can be augmented to DD in large framed, tall women. I would not recommend this approach to petite or medium build women,as it wound make them too top heavy.
How long do you recommend waiting to fly post breast augmentation?
Flying After Breast Augmentation
Jul 3, 2018
Flying after breast augmentation poses no medical risk. The implants will expand moderately at high altitude and contract when landing…. like that water you carried on board in its plastic bottle. Yes, there may be discomfort from this process which will be felt more acutely in the first week or two post augmentation. 800 mg of ibuprofen (Advil) taken every 6 hours should make you comfortable on your flight.If possible, wait a couple weeks after surgery to fly.
