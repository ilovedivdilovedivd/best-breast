Is it necessary to be hospitalised after gynaecomastia surgery and how long should one wear compressio
Hospitalisation after gynecomastia surgery
May 2, 2016
Hi,    Thanks for your question. I perform the procedure under local anesthesia. About 15-20% of our cases(those with less pain tolerance or anxious individuals) require a light sedation along with the local anesthesia. We have an anesthetist present for even those surgeries performed under local anesthesia. The person is usually discharged 1-2 hours after the procedure. The compression vest is to be worn for 4-6 weeks.

Can liposuction help get rid of a pear shape, or a protruding butt?
Liposuction for pear shape and protruding butt
Mar 1, 2016
Hello,     Thanks for the question. Liposuction works very well in cases with localized excess fat deposits(like prominent butts,saddle bags etc.). Would it help to wear a faja or compression garment 4 month after abdomen lipo to help retract the ski
Compression garment 4 months after liposuction
Feb 17, 2016
A compression garment is used to support and provide compression to the area after liposuction. It is usually recommended to be worn for 4-6 weeks, post the procedure. It would not help to use one 4 months after liposuction.
Can hair implant be done from beard to head?
Beard hair transplant
Feb 17, 2016
Beard hair, taken usually from the shadow area is used as a donor area for the head. Scalp hair is the preferred choice when adequate number of grafts are available. Beard hair can be used in between the scalp hair to increase the density(particularly when the scalp donor area is insufficient). Used alone, beard hair may appear unnatural,as they are coarser and thicker than the scalp hair.
