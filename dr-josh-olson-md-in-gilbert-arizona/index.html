Statement: Dr. Josh Olson is a board-certified plastic surgeon based in Paradise Valley and Gilbert, Arizona. He is highly adept in all aesthetic procedures of the body, breasts, and face, but is especially known for his talents in mommy makeovers, breast enhancement, and tummy tuck surgery. His practice, Advanced Plastic Surgery Institute, also offers non-invasive treatments such as Botox, Dysport, CoolSculpting, Cellfina, and laser hair removal.

Dr. Olson believes plastic surgery is more than just aesthetics. It can be a life-changing experience with the power to bring healthy self-esteem, renewed confidence, and overall happiness. Dr. Olson makes sure every patient is informed and comfortable throughout their entire journey, and he understands the emotional impact that surgery can have. His patients—many of whom come from Chandler, Mesa, and Tempe—appreciate not only his extraordinary skill in the operating room, but also his warm and approachable personality.

After attending Brigham Young University for his undergraduate degree, he earned his medical degree at the University of Arizona College of Medicine. From there, he ventured to the University of Utah Hospitals and Clinics to complete a plastic and reconstructive surgery residency in Salt Lake City. He then expanded his training at the esteemed Ivo Pitanguy clinic in Rio de Janeiro, where he mastered his skill for body contouring procedures.

A RealSelf Top Doctor, Dr. Olson is certified by The American Board of Plastic Surgery, and is a member of the American Medical Association, the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, and the Utah Medical Association. Aside from helping patients in Arizona to achieve their dreams, Dr. Olson also provides voluntary medical services to people in need overseas.
