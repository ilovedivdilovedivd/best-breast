Statement: Dr. Frederic Corbin, MD, is a board-certified plastic surgeon with almost 40 years of experience and two practices, located in Brea and Beverly Hills, California, serving patients from all over Orange County and beyond. While he specializes in all areas of cosmetic aesthetics, he is especially known for his expertise in mommy makeovers, breast enhancement, and body lift surgery, from tummy tucks to arm lifts.

Aside from aesthetic surgery, Dr. Corbin is known for his skills with nonsurgical treatments such as injectables, body contouring procedures, and other skin rejuvenation techniques. He is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons. He is regularly sought out by news publications for his expert knowledge and opinion, and has appeared on MSNBC, BBC, TLC, Inside Edition, and more.

Before embarking on his medical career, Dr. Corbin attended Brown University for his undergraduate education. He then went on to complete his medical degree at New York University School of Medicine, which was followed by surgical training at the University of California, San Diego, and as residency at Boston’s Tufts University. Finally, Dr. Corbin completed his plastic surgery training at Columbia University.

In addition to the work he performs at his Brea and Beverly Hills practices, Dr. Corbin travels the world with Operation Smile, surgically treating children with facial defects and other congenital deformities.
