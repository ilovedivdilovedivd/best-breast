Age 31, would clitoral hood reduction prevent clitoros from enlarging?
The short answer is no
Jun 22, 2022
Thanks for the question. A clitoral hood reduction WILL NOT affect the clitoris itself. You will appreciate that no surgeon will cut into the clitoris itself. If anything a clitoral hood reduction may expose the clitoris and make it look more obvious.
Enlarged clitoris, is it clitoromegaly and what causes clitoromegaly?
Clitoral enlargement?
Jun 22, 2022
Hi and thanks for the question.
PCOS can cause clitoral enlargement as can other hormonal imbalances (but your endocrinologist has tested for these). It is also reassuring that your gynaecologist feels your clitoris is within the normal range.
Clitoral hood reduction will reduce the skin (hood) around the clitoris but it WILL NOT reduce the clitoris itself. I would follow the advice of your gynaecologist as they are best placed to advise on this issue and not plastic/ cosmetic surgeons.

If I want to get breast implants & lipo at separate times, which one should I do first?
Why not do both?
Jun 22, 2022
Depending on how much lipo you want/ number of areas, there is no reason why you cannot have both done at the same time. Patients have breast surgery with a full tummy tuck so breast augmentation with lipo is not a major undertaking.
Speak to your surgeon about the logistics. There are pros and cons but having combined surgery is usually cheaper and you only have one lot of downtime.
