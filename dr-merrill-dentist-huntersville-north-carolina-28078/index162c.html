Best way to close gap between my two front teeth?
Mind the Gap
Sep 7, 2021
An ideal solution would be to slightly broaden the 2 front teeth with composite and to close the remainder with orthodontics. The tissue is an advantage is that it will fill in from the gum side.
Option 2 is to bond the front four teeth to reshape and to close the gap–it would be an artistic challenge but an experienced cosmetic dentist could accomplish this for you–be aware there are not many out there of this caliber.
