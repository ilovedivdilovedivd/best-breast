Doctors

Reza Rod, MD

Name: Reza Rod, MD
Statement: Dr. Reza Rod is a board-certified plastic surgeon practicing in Goodyear, Arizona, with nearly two decades of experience. He specializes in cosmetic surgery of the face, breasts, and body, with particular expertise in breast enhancement, mommy makeovers, and body contouring.

Dr. Rod is the founder of Dermacare Plastic Surgery, serving patients across the greater Phoenix and Scottsdale area. His practice is dedicated to helping his patients enhance their natural beauty using both surgical and nonsurgical techniques, including Botox and Dysport, dermal fillers, laser treatments, and other skin rejuvenation treatments.

Dr. Rod obtained his undergraduate degree in biochemistry from Washington University in St. Louis, Missouri, where he graduated magna cum laude. He then obtained his medical degree at the University of Wisconsin in Madison, before completing his surgical residency at the Phoenix Integrated Surgical Residency Program. The five-year training program honed his skills in general surgery, as well as vascular and trauma surgeries.

During his surgical residency, Dr. Rod focused on his passion for aesthetics. He was selected for a plastic surgery fellowship at the world-renowned Mayo Clinic in Rochester, Minnesota, where he received advanced training on cosmetic procedures and cutting-edge technologies.

Dr. Rod is certified by the American Board of Surgery and the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He is also a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.
