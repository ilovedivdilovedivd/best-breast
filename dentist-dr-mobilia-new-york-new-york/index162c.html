Can fillings be done on the back of lateral incisor near the gumline where cavity formed?
Placing Tooth Colored Fillings at the Gumline of Lateral Incisors.
Jul 20, 2014
Yes, tooth colored fillings can be placed at the back of lateral incisors near the gumline in order to fill cavities. Tooth colored bonding is technique sensitive so the dentist must be careful not to have the filling and receptor site area get contaminated with either blood or saliva. The area must be isolated and sometimes if the cavity is below the gumline, we use our Diode laser to clear out the soft tissue in order to have the filling rest on a solid and visible foundation.
