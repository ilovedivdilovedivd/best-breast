How can I tell if I have a loose screw or loose implant.
Check with your dentist
May 7, 2018
Both a loose screw and a loose implant have similar clinical characteristics …. the crown restoration moves. To determine which is which, the best thing to do is see your implant dentist. The dentist will take an x-ray to look at the bone health around the implant. The dentist may also need to disassemble the crown to figure it out.
Correct /adjust teeth middle line, would veneers be a quick solution?
Braces are the answer
May 7, 2018
To correct the tooth midline to face midline problem, braces is the best and potentially only solution. This is because the tooth root as it is position now is directly at the midline. There is no veneer that can correct this

Would braces or dental bonding be best for me?
Braces
May 5, 2018
Bonding alone will make your teeth too wide for how tall they are. It would look aweful. It is possible that you may have too much gum tissue covering your teeth. In that case a gum lift followed by bonding would be ok. You need to diagnose whether the gum line is in the correct position or whether you have “altered passive eruption”. Also, consider no prep porcelain veneers over bonding.
What can be done to close the gap in my front teeth?
Braces
May 4, 2018
Closing this space with bonding or veneers will make the teeth look too wide. Use braces to pull the teeth together
Uneven gum line with new front teeth crowns
Get the gumline correct first!
May 4, 2018
When having cosmetic dentistry performed it is important to asses the gumline first. The gumline gives the tooth its size, shape, and character. The gumline can be altered through either a gum lift or gum replacement if need be. Once the gumline is even and symmetrical, the crowns or veneers can be placed. In this case, the gumline can be still be corrected but one or more of the crowns will likely need to be replaced.
