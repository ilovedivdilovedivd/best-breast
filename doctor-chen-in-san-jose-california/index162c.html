I am having a hysterectomy in 4 weeks. Is it ok for me to have SculpSure done on my arms now?
You can have Sculpsure done on your arms
Jun 11, 2018
You can have Sculpsure on your arms prior to hysterectomy. Sculpsure is a treatment that targets the fat cells (under the applicators) so there is no effect on your surgery. Good luck!
Sculpsure applicator placement
placement of applicators
Mar 6, 2018
There are many different combinations that you can place the applicators. The belly button can be avoided by using the little spacer that connects two separate brackets, or use the special bracket that has a wider gap. As long as the brackets are fitted to get more "pillows", you should have great results.
SculpSure: Are 2 treatments needed 100% of the time in 100% of clients?
treatments of Sculpsure
Feb 26, 2018
It is generally recommended to have at least 2 treatments per area for best results. You do not "have" to do 2+ treatments, but we do find that the results are much better and patients are much happier with at least 2 treatments. Some people may need more as the area being treated has more fat content, each treatment that is done can reduce up to 24% of fat. Overall, it is up to you and how your body response to the treatment. Hope this helps...
Can a stomach full of stretch-marks benefit from lasers or micro needling?
Icon 1540 fractional laser can help
Feb 26, 2018
Stretch marks are difficult to get rid of (also depending on the size and width). It will take multiple treatments (5+) and each treatment is about 4-6 weeks apart. The fractional 1540 laser works by breaking down stretch marks with pulses of laser, this process will trigger new production of tissue below your skin. The new collagen will help improve the overall appearance of the stretch marks.
