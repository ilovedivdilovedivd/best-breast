Statement: Dr. Courtenay Poucher is a board-certified OB-GYN, cosmetic gynecologist, and founder of the Labiaplasty Center of Los Angeles in Valencia, California. Her practice is devoted to delivering an exceptional, often life-changing experience for females of all ages—from teenagers to women in their 70s and older—who are seeking both aesthetic and functional vaginal treatments.

Dr. Poucher offers surgical and nonsurgical vaginal rejuvenation treatments, including labiaplasty, labia augmentation, clitoral hood reduction, vaginoplasty, perineoplasty, hymenoplasty, the O-shot, ThermiVa, Viveve, and PRP (platelet-rich plasma) injections.

“Since 2001, I have dedicated my career to women’s health and sexual wellness,” says Dr. Poucher. “I’m so happy that I’ve been blessed with the ability to help women have a procedure in the office, and then be able to go back out into the world with newfound confidence and comfort.”

Dr. Poucher completed her undergraduate studies at the University of California, Davis, and earned her medical degree from the Hahnemann School of Medicine, now called Drexel University College of Medicine. She completed an internship at UCLA Medical Center and her residency at Harbor UCLA Medical Center.

Dr. Poucher is board-certified by the American Board of Obstetrics & Gynecology.

In addition to a quiet, confidential, and beautifully appointed office, Dr. Poucher has an all-female team, which adds to the feeling of comfort that’s essential when seeking care for such an intimate area.
