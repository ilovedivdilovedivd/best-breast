Statement: Dr. Harry T. Haramis is a board-certified plastic surgeon based in New Jersey, with 26 years of experience in breast enhancement, tummy tucks, facial injections, body contouring, and an array of other surgical cosmetic procedures. As an experienced member of the surgical team at Associates in Plastic Surgery, he sees patients at New Jersey offices in Edison, Marlboro, Warren, and West Orange.

He received his medical degree from the University of Medicine and Dentistry of New Jersey (now known as Rutgers New Jersey Medical School), then finished a five-year residency in General Surgery at St. Barnabas Medical Center in Livingston, New Jersey. He went on to complete a fellowship in Plastic Surgery at Wright State University / Kettering Medical Center in Dayton, Ohio.

Dr. Haramis is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, a Fellow of the American College of Surgeons, and a member of the prestigious American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.

His many years experience and dedication to the field of plastic surgery means he has mastered a variety of surgical subspecialties and techniques and has become an expert in some of the more complex skills and technologies. His knowledge and artistic eye are highly sought after by patients, and he has a reputation for high-quality results and exceptional patient care, both before and after surgery.
