Can I get implants after having wore dentures for several years?
Time is not on your side…
Jun 16, 2017
The truth is the longer that you wait to have implants placed the more bone will be lost. This bone remodeling process continues until the implants are placed as they help stabilize and stimulate your bone cells.
Grafting can be done later but is far less predictable and even grafting requires some existing bone in order for it to be successful. My suggestion is that if you are thinking about dental implants you have them done sooner rather than later.
