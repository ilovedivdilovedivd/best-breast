Doctors

Jason Pozner, MD

Name: Jason Pozner, MD
Statement: Jason Pozner, MD is an esteemed aesthetic plastic surgeon and an authority on nonsurgical devices and techniques. He is the founder of Sanctuary Plastic Surgery and co-owner of Sanctuary Medical Aesthetic Center in Boca Raton, Florida. “At Sanctuary Medical, I work to enhance all aspects of the face, breasts, and body,” says Dr. Pozner, who was awarded Top Doctor status by RealSelf. “Whatever your challenge, I’m here to help you feel your confident, youthful, most attractive self.”

As someone who loves to fix things, Dr. Pozner is known as an expert in revision surgery—70% of his busy surgical practice is spent on “challenging, but fun” secondary breast, face, and lipo cases. Dr. Pozner is up-to-date on less-invasive procedures and new technology. For instance, he’s considered an authority on laser skin resurfacing, which can be performed on its own or as a complement to surgical facelifts.

Dr. Pozner received his medical degree at Mount Sinai School of Medicine with a Distinction in Research, which means he conducted and published the results of an original research project during medical school. He trained for five years in general surgery, completing an intensive internship and residency at Mount Sinai Medical Center. He completed a plastic surgery residency at SUNY Downstate Medical Center. He went on to complete two fellowships: one in microsurgery, at Montefiore Medical Center, and another in aesthetic and endoscopic plastic surgery at the Plastic and Aesthetic Surgery Center of Maryland. While in Maryland, he was an assistant professor of plastic surgery at the esteemed Johns Hopkins Medical Center. He is currently an adjunct clinical faculty member in the department of plastic surgery at Cleveland Clinic Florida.

Dr. Pozner is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He’s a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, the American Society for Laser Medicine and Surgery, and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons. He’s also a fellow of the American College of Surgeons.

The 5,000-square-foot surgical facility at Sanctuary Plastic Surgery is fully accredited by the American Association for Accreditation of Ambulatory Surgery Facilities, the State of Florida, and the Florida Board of Medicine. Dr. Pozner regularly sees out-of-state and international patients and his staff works with patients to make sure the process goes as smoothly as possible.
