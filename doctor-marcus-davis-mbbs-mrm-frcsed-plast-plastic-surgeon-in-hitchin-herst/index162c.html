What can I do about the filler in my nose glowing red in the sun?
HA translucency in sunlight
Jul 19, 2019
Many thanks of your question and pictures. HA filler allows light to shine through it so if you have had a good quantity injected under thin skin then this can occur. I would suggest that you apply a sunscreen with some primer/tint within it that will help to block the passage of light and thus the red glow. Should this not work sufficiently well for you then your only option is to have the HA dissolved.
