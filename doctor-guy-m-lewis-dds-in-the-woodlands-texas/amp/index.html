Should I consider Invisalign before dental implants?
Invisalign before dental implants
Aug 23, 2018
Yes, it is recommended to have Invisalign treatment prior to dental implants. You want to make sure spacing is corrected prior to having implants placed. Make sure you are going to an experienced Dentist that can work with the dentist placing the implants to determine exact spacing that is needed for the dental implants.

How long do crowns and veneers last
Dec 15, 2016
My experience has been that veneers and crowns can both last a very long time, especially if they are cared for properly. I have some some patients that have crowns and veneers that are over 30 years old! If placed by an experienced dentist, they can last a long time. I do tend to see less recurrent decay under veneers as opposed to crowns...that could be due to the fact that they are up front and people take better care of them.
