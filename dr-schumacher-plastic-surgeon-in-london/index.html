Is my labiomental fold too deep for a chin implant?
Labiomental fold
May 10, 2022
Hello,
Thank you for your enquiry. The photographic information is very helpful.
There is no definite right or wrong answer, so what I am saying is just my personal professional opinion. Your labiomental fold sits slightly high and has a slight convexity (=depth). My own preference would be to move the bone forward instead of an implant to reduce the risk of an overpowering chin following surgery.
I would suggest to discuss this with your surgeon who has more information about you than I.
Would I be a candidate for genioplasty?
Genioplasty
May 8, 2022
Hello,
Thank you for your enquiry. The photographic information is very helpful.
It would be a good idea to discuss this with your dentist or maxillofacial surgeon first, since bite problems are usually corrected surgically with moving the jaw which will also alter the chin. If there is no need for surgery to correct the bite, a genioplasty might be a good option for you to bring the chin forward and downwards.
