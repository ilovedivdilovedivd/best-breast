Statement: Dr. Elisa A. Burgess is a double board-certified plastic surgeon and the founder of her own private practice, Burgess Plastic Surgery, in Lake Oswego, Oregon. She is best known for her expertise in breast enhancement procedures, including breast augmentations, reductions, and lifts. She skillfully performs cosmetic and reconstructive procedures across the face, breast, and body, including facial rejuvenation surgeries, body sculpting, and abdominoplasty.

Dr. Burgess is a native Oregonian, born and raised in Portland. She graduated with her bachelor’s degree from Oregon State University before earning her medical degree from Oregon Health Science University (OHSU).

After completing an internship in general surgery at the University of California, San Francisco, Dr. Burgess returned to OHSU to pursue fellowships in general surgery, wound healing, and plastic surgery. Upon finalizing her formal training in 2001, she received teaching and research honors from OHSU.

Dr. Burgess holds two board certifications, one from the American Board of Surgery and the other from the American Board of Plastic Surgery.

When not serving her patients in Lake Oswego and the greater Portland area, Dr. Burgess attends plastic surgery seminars and conferences to stay on the cutting edge of advancements in technology and techniques. She frequently brings new procedures to the Portland area that include the medspa services at In My Own Skin Medspa within the practice. The layering of surgery and medspa services lends to a very natural and layered result.

Outside of her practice, Dr. Burgess maintains memberships in professional organizations such as the Oregon Society of Plastic Surgeons, American Society of Plastic Surgeons, and the American Medical Association.

Dr. Burgess also uses her surgical skills to help improve the lives of patients in underserved communities. She recently traveled with her husband, also a physician, to the Philippines, where they worked together to repair cleft lips and palates.

She enjoys spending her free time with her family.
