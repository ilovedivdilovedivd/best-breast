When can I wear makeup over chin Laser Liposuction scars?
Makeup after chin liposuction
Mar 20, 2019
I definitely do not recommend wearing makeup that soon after chin liposuction. Typically sutures will stay in for about 7 days and at that point, you would be able to conceal them, but should really try and stay away from any harsh chemicals or makeup for at least 2 weeks in that area. Best of luck!
Input please, regarding IPL treatment a week before face and neck lift.
IPL prior to Facelift
Mar 20, 2019
I recommend cancelling the IPL treatment prior to your surgery. You can reschedule for sometime 6 to 8 weeks after your facelift. Although an IPL is noninvasive and superficial, it may cause swelling which could interfere with your surgical results. Best of luck!
How long after upper eye blepharoplasty can you use Latisse?
Latisse Use After Eyelid Surgery
Mar 20, 2019
I recommend that you do not begin using Latisse until about 6 weeks post surgery. You want to wait until the incision is completely healed prior to putting any type of chemical on or near the eyelid. Hope this answers your question. Best of luck!
