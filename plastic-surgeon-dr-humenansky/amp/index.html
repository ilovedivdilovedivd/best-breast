What do you think my end result will look like after explant? Do I need an uplift?
Upper pole volume
Dec 15, 2023
If you remove your implants and do not replace them, you will lose the fullness in the upper portion of your breast. Your nipple position appears appropriate and would likely not require a lift for repositioning; however, a breast lift would help tighten any excess skin following explant. You may also want to explore options such as autoaugmentation with fat transfer following explant to help maintain some volume. Ultimately, you have a variety of options to discuss with your plastic surgeon.
Will revision surgery address my low implant?
Double bubble
Dec 15, 2023
I believe this appears as a “double bubble” where a small portion of your implant is sitting below your inframammary fold (natural crease at the bottom of the breast). Recreating your inframammary fold by reattaching your scar to the chest wall should achieve a similar appearance to the other breast. You may also consider talking with your surgeon about supporting the implant with sutures or mesh.
Will scar from mole removal improve?
Scar improvement
Dec 6, 2021
After removal, the scar continues to remodel for 6 months. I would continue post-excision scar management including keeping the scar moist and protected with sunscreen. The redness generally tends to fade over time. If you were to have the area removed for a straight line, the line would be longer than the current red area, so keep that in mind when considering removal. I would work with the surgeon who performed the excision for a result you are both pleased with.

Getting filler for dimpleplasty recovery? Thoughts?
Dimpleplasty and Filler
Nov 16, 2021
Yes, you are correct about having an indentation when you’re not smiling – while you recover from your surgery. I would recommend avoiding any filler product to the area while it is healing. The dimpleplasty procedure relies on a scar forming between the undersurface of your skin and the muscle below. Placing filler may interfere with this process and decrease the effectiveness of the procedure.
