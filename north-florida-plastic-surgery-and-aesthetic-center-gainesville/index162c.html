Doctors

John W. Tyrone, MD

Name: John W. Tyrone, MD
Statement: Dr. Tyrone is a board-certified plastic surgeon practicing in Gainesville, Florida. He specializes in all aspects of facial rejuvenation, including brow lift, blepharoplasty, and face/neck lift. He is well-known for his breast enhancements, including breast augmentation, breast lift, and breast reduction. This is often paired with a body-contouring tummy tuck, which is known as a mommy makeover.

Dr. Tyrone wants the best results for each patient, and he believes patient education leads to the best results. During new-patient consultations, Dr. Tyrone and his staff evaluate and recommend the best treatment plan based on the patient’s desired results. Not a surgical candidate? Dr. Tyrone offers a variety of non-surgical options such as CoolSculpting, injectables, laser, and skin treatments to help you reach your individual goal. He also has a wellness program that allows future surgical patients to work with Dr. Tyrone’s nurse practitioner to reach his or her ideal weight. Being at a healthy weight before surgery allows Dr. Tyrone to safely treat and provide the best results. It’s important for patients to follow Dr. Tyrone’s pre and post-operative instructions to maintain their results and prevent any secondary procedures that may have been caused by weight loss postoperatively.

Patient satisfaction involves more than just the operating room. Dr. Tyrone handpicks each member of his staff to ensure he has full trust in the outcome for each patient. With over 19 years in practice, Dr. Tyrone has become one of the most sought-after surgeons in North Central Florida.
