Statement: Dr. John L. Burns Jr. is a board-certified plastic surgeon in Dallas, Texas with nearly two decades in practice. He is the president of the Dallas Plastic Surgery Institute, a state-of-the-art practice that is dedicated to providing the highest quality plastic surgical services in a warm, caring environment.

Dr. Burns specializes in breast augmentation (with both implants and fat transfer), liposuction, tummy tucks and other surgical body contouring, and complete mommy makeovers. Recognized by D Magazine as one of the best plastic surgeons in the city, he believes that a person’s natural beauty should be enhanced, not altered.

He also has expertise in facial rejuvenation and body contouring, and is nationally known for his clinical research studies focusing on new laser technology. That makes him a perfect fit in his role of medical director for the EpiCentre Skin Care and Laser Center, which offers a broad range of innovative nonsurgical treatments.

A Texas native, Dr. Burns graduated Summa Cum Laude from the Research Honors Program at the University of Texas Medical Branch (UTMB) in Galveston, where he remained to pursue a residency in plastic surgery. He then completed advanced training in microvascular surgery at the University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center in Houston and cosmetic surgery in Miami, Florida.

Dr. Burns went on to become the first plastic surgeon in the U.S. to complete a fellowship in laser surgery at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center in Dallas.

Outside of his private practice, Dr. Burns is a clinical instructor of plastic surgery at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center Department of Plastic Surgery and is involved with the Cosmetic Surgery Fellowship Program at the Dallas Plastic Surgery Institute.

Whenever possible, Dr. Burns uses his surgical expertise to give back. He serves on the Board of Directors and as a contributing surgeon for the Share-A-Smile Foundation, a non-profit that treats uninsured children with vascular anomalies. He is also active with LEAP Global Missions, an organization founded by his partner Dr. P. Craig Hobar, which strives to bring the finest reconstructive care to underserved countries around the world.

Dr. Burns is an active member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS).
