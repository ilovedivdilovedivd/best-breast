Breast Implants May Help Correct Asymmetry

Almost every woman has some size difference in their breasts, but you indicate that your case will need some correction. Implants of different sizes can help correct size differences, but remember that you also have a difference In the amount of skin on each side. So simply adjusting the implant size may not result in 100% perfect symmetry.

Breast implants and pregnancy

Pregnancy and hormone-mediated action will increase the size of the breasts during pregnancy. This will not affect the implants themselves. Experiencing abnormal pain is extremely uncommon. Stretch marks are more related to rapid weight gain and weight loss than anything else.

If you become pregnant with breast implants, make sure you wear a well-fitting and supportive bra, keep your tissues well-moisturized, and try to avoid rapid swings in weight.

High Profile Breast Implants

Deciding on the size and profile of your implants is a bgg part getting a breast augmentation. Talk to your surgeon about your desired result and listen to his advice. It sounds like the high profile implant will decrease the amount of sag or extra skin you are referring to.

It is absolutely possible to achieve a natural looking appearance with high profile implants. You are the only one who really knows what you want. Ask to see pictures of other women who were your stature and received 550cc high profile implants, you are not the only one who has ever been in this situation.

Implant exchange through armpits

I think that for any implant exchange going through the armpit would be almost impossible. Often a capsule develops that needs to be removed. Additionally the small incision in the armpit is usually not conducive to plasce silicone implants.
Breast implants don’t prevent breast feeding

The implants are always behind the glandular tissue, even when placed above the muscle. Anatomically there is no reason that you could not breastfeed after a breast enlargement. However, bear in mind that some women just can’t breastfeed properly regardless of having implants or not! So don’t feel guilty if after your breast enlargement your breast feeding doesn’t work as hoped for.

It’s probably not due to the implants.

Breast augmentation in a plus size patient

Any healthy of-age female should be a candidate for BA. Your implant selection should ideally be proportional to your body. So expect that you will have larger implants to meet that look. Definitely make an in-person consultation with a PS to evaluate you and discuss your options.

Steam Rooms and Breast Implants

Steam rooms will have no effect on your implants. Your body maintains a safe core temperature despite the temperature of your surroundings. So relax, enjoy the steam room, and do not worry about the heat effecting your implants.

Questions to Ask When Your Are Considering Breast Implants

Many components are involved when deciding to undergo breast augmentation surgery. First you have to decide if breast augmentation is right for you. Be sure that you are choosing this procedure for you and not for anyone else.

You also need to be sure that you are in good health, and can refrain from drinking and smoking for several weeks. There is a plethora of information available online. I would start here. You can learn about the procedure itself, along with researching various doctors and surgery centers.
When it comes to choosing your doctor, a good place to start is www.plasticsurgery.org. By choosing a board certified plastic surgeon, you can rest assured that your surgeon only practices in fully accredited facilities, upholds ethical standards and has completed rigorous specialized training in plastic surgery.

It is best to meet with multiple surgeons for consultations before deciding which surgeon is best to perform your breast augmentation. During your consultations, you can learn about each doctor’s technique, view before and after pictures and discover which doctor makes you feel the most comfortable.

It is important to remember not to select a surgeon based soley on price, because it may cost you later. For breast augmentation itself, you can choose from silicone or saline. Both are safe options and both can provide natural looking results, however, silicone implants are often preferred because they feel more natural.
You also must decide which incision site you would like – periareolar (through the areola), inframmamary (under the fold), transaxillary (through the armput) or through the belly button – which is actually not recommended because of the high complication rate and prolonged downtime.

Other aspects of the surgery that will discussed at your consultations include: exactly how each procedure works, placement of the implant (over or under the muscle), texture of the implant, projection of the implant, possible risks and side effects of the procedure, etc.

It is also important to research what to do before and after surgery. For example, arnica montana tablets can help reduce swelling and bruising and should be taken two days prior to surgery. Kelocote scar treatment can help the healing process and reduce the appearance of scars.
Also, massaging is a very important part of the healing process and helps prevent the instance of capsular contracture. Reading blogs from patients or talking to friends who have had the procedure is a helpful way to prepare both physically and emotionally.

Differences between saline, silicone and “gummy bear” breast implants

Form stable or the so called “gummy bear” breast implants are the newest types of breast implants to be released on the market. These implants are filled with a highly cohesive silicone gel, which is much thicker and stiffer than a traditional modern silicone gel implant.

These implants are now made in an anatomic, tear drop shape and because of the stiffness of the gel inside, they maintain this shape in all positions.

This means that unlike a natural breast, the form stable implant will maintain its teardrop shop whether upright or laying down.
Other potential disadvantages of this type of implant include the extra firmness of the implant and the obvious problems associated with rotation of the implant within the pocket if it were to occur. Obviously, there are advantageous of form stable breast implants or they wouldn’t have generated such a buzz on the market recently.

Particularly for small breasted women who want a very conservative increase in size, the gummy bear breast implant can provide a more natural shape to the breast. Additionally, the gel on the inside, being even more cohesive, will virtually never migrate outside of the shell even if the implant cracks or ruptures.

The capsular contracture rates of these implants is the lowest of any breast implant ever developed. Like virtually every other decision in plastic surgery, each of the various implants has advantages and disadvantages. For this reason, it is important to have a detailed consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon you feel comfortable with prior to deciding which implant will be best for you.

Breast Implant placement

Implant placement, above the muscle or below the muscle, depends on:

1. How much breast tissue you have to cover the implant
2. Saline implants need more protection, and under the muscle gives more protection
3. There is less interference with mammogram under the muscle, may be less capsular contracture.
4. Under the muscle the implant will move with contracting the muscle Once you understand all this, then it is your option.

You need to compare apples to apples, so to speak. If you’re starting with a full B cup and your girlfriend started with a mid-A, you won’t end up the same size when you both get 375 cc implants.
