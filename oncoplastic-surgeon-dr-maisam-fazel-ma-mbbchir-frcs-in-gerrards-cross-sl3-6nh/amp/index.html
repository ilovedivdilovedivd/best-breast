Recommendations on having breast implants and wanting to get pregnant?
Breast implants and pregnancy
Nov 30, 2020
Thanks for your question. Pregnancy and breast feeding tend to effect all breasts, whether you have implants or not. There is no reason why you cannot have breast implants now as long as you appreciate the changes that will occur with pregnancy and breastfeeding. Many women successfully breast feed after implants but in some cases, the ability to breast feed is reduced.
