Some photographs of the puffy areas on your forehead would be helpful to evaluate the problem. Fatty lumps in the forehead are usually removed with a minor surgical procedure, not liposuction. What kind of breast deformity do I have?
Oct 17, 2014
Your right breast has grown larger and is ptotic (droopy), while your left breast is tubular or tuberous. The right breast will need to be reduced (the nipple gets elevated to match the other side), while the left breast will need an implant and a circumareolar (donut) mastopexy. This should improve the symmetry and appearance of your breasts and nipples significantly.
