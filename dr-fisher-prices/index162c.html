Moderate Plus Vs High Profile Silicone Gel Implants

Although they may seem subtle when you’re looking at before and after breast augmentation pictures, there are definitely differences between moderate plus and high profile breast implants. High profile implants are actually constructed differently than moderate plus implants.

The implants are taller and narrower, while the moderate plus implants have a wider base. Even though the two implants might have the exact same volume, high profile implants will project forward more than moderate plus.

Most women find that high profile implants give their breasts a greater amount of fullness in the upper part of the breast. Some patients love this dramatic look, while others think it makes their surgery more obvious.

It really comes down to personal preference. Moderate plus implants give the breast a more natural teardrop shape, with more fullness at the bottom than the top. This is what most of the women visiting my practice choose for their breast augmentations.

Pick your surgeon well

Regardless of what type of surgery you elect to have in the long run, it is incredibly important to feel that you have a good honest open relationship with your surgeon. That way you will feel that you have made the best decision possible, and that you were listened to and understood.

I would interview as many board cert plastic surgeons as it takes to establish that relationship.

This tends to come up very often during my consultations for breast augmentation; how can I achieve a natural look. I suggest that my patients bring any pictures of friends, celebrities, or online photos of their desired look.

I will then refer them to a website created by Mentor and have them choose someone with a similar height and frame. From that website, they will save a few post operative pictures of their desired look.

All these post op photos have the patient’s height, weight, and style of implant. I will then have them place silicone sizers into a bra or T-shirt in the office.

By using these three techniques, I can then get a more precise assessment of what size implant will achieve your desired look.

I would not get hung up over 25cc difference between the implants since this will make a minimal difference in the post op look.

Breast implants for asymmetry

you should be in good hands if your surgeon is a board-certified plastic surgeon and has experience with implant surgery. I would trust his or her opinion. At surgery for cases of asymmetry, we often use sizers, which are temporary implants, to determine the best size prior to placing the final implant.

If you have concerns about the recommendation the fitter made,speak to your doctor directly. It’s best to be comfortable with the decisions made prior to surgery. /nsn.

Lift by breast implants

Although there is no actual lift, a deflated breast will look better and more youthful with added volume. That can be accomplished with breast implants. Sometimes they can be placed under the muscle, sometimes they need to be on top on the muscle.

This depends on a number of factors. Overall if your breasts are not too droopy you may be pleasantly surprised with breast implants.

Lift needed?

If you have a consultation the surgeon will determine your nipple position, tissue thickness, skin tone, other factors and of course your expected outcome to determine whether implants alone will be satisfactory.

Breast implant failure is not caused by excessive activity or mammography.

I don’t know where you read the information in your question but it is untrue. Implants fail because of a manufacturing flaw (almost never) or from fatigue (the implant has been in place for a long time).

Any incident that triggers failure would be doing so to an implant that was imminently to fail anyway. Watch the video and see how much force can be placed on in implant. They are very resistent to bursting.

Implants and Lungs

The implants will not damage your lungs, but I would have the deflated saline implant removed.

650 cc implants

A 650 cc implant will probably make you considerably larger than a small D, and most certainly will give you much upper pole fullness. In my catalogs, there actually are no 650 cc moderate profile saline for Mentor.

The nearest size is close to 15 cm wide and high–about six inches. The bottom of your implant will sit at or slightly below your current breast fold. If you measure up six inches you will see how high these will go, and it may well be that most of the fill you get will be in the upper portion of the breast.

Advice? -have another talk with your surgeon.

You can re-operate and using a fill tube adjust the volume of saline implants in either direction. However, there are limitations. It is best not to go below the recommended fill volume for the implant.

On the high side you can overfill the implant; however, as the implant becomes larger, it becomes more spherical. As a rule I do not like to overfill more than 15%. In my experience, when a patient wants to go larger, it is necessary to go 70-100cc to make it worth an additional surgery.

You may or not be able to achieve that with your implant, depending upon its size.

Improving recovery after breast implants

There are proven techniques to improve your recovery after breast augmentation that reliably lead to a 24 hour recovery after surgery. They have been validated in plastic surgery studies but have not yet been adopted by all plastic surgeons.

Blunt dissection, the technique that many of us were trained with, tends to be more painful and has a longer recovery. It is still being taught at national meetings. Dr. Tebbetts from Dallas reported that by using gentle technique, special instruments and electrocautery dissection with a hand-switched monopolar cautery that there is less bleedng and trauma to the tissues, and a faster recovery.

There is a description of his technique in “A thoughtful patient’s guide to plastic surgery” that you might find helpful. A good recovery starts with gentle surgery.

Breast implants

I believe that you could get a good result with a implants alone and that a breast lift is not necessary. Oftentimes, an augmentation can give the illusion of a lift. After a minimum of 9 months, when the implant has settled, you’ll have a better idea whether or not you think your breasts are perky enough.

Breast Options

To achieve the best results, you need a lift and implants. The acne scarring is difficult to improve in that area but devices such as the Venus viva will help give a smoother appearance.

Placing implants under the muscle will create a more natural appearance

I definitely feel that for the vast majority of patients placing the implant under the muscle will create a more natural appearance. The muscle covers the upper and middle part of the breast implant allowing for a more natural slope in the upper breast and for a better appearance in the cleavage area.

Additionally, the muscle coverage provides more soft tissue padding over the implant, which helps to hide ripples and irregularities and gives a smoother, better appearance to the breasts. When the muscle of the chest is flexed, it can create some distortion of the implant.

However, this is usually relatively minor and a very fair tradeoff for the many advantages of being under the muscle as discussed above. It also lowers the risk of scar hardening called capsular contracture.
