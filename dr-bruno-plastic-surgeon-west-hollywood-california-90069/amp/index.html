Is it safe to have lipo to the upper abdomen with the same time as a TT?
Liposuction combined with a tummy tuck
Oct 6, 2020
Liposuction of the upper abdomen at the same time as a tummy tuck carries risk to the blood supply of the abdominal skin flap. This means that the liposuction can potentially cause the tummy tuck incision to heal poorly or become infected. The safest approach is to avoid liposuction of the upper abdomen with a tummy tuck and instead perform direct removal of the deep fatty layer- not the fat close to the skin.
Consult with a few plastic surgeons before making your decision.
Best wishes,
Dr.Bruno
