Sculptra or Radiesse for neck?
Sculptra works well with Crepey skin (Neck area)
Oct 27, 2021
Both Radiesse and Sculptra have the ability to create lumps, so there is no guarantee when using any type of filler. I prefer Sculptra over Radiesse for the neck because I believe that collagen production greatly benefits the crepeyness often found in this area. Also if other procedures are performed in conjunction, i.e. Radio Frequency (RF) micro-needling, or fractional CO2 erbium, this will work synergistically and not cause degradation of the product.
