What are my options for breast reduction?
breast reduction at young age.
Jan 20, 2023
There is no age limit for breast reduction. Large droopy breasts, can happen at any age, even below 20. If the breasts feel too large, heavy, give you back pain or other symptoms, breast reduction can be a very rewarding procedure to improve a patient’s quality of life.
You should be reviewed by a plastic surgeon, to discuss the pros and cons of the surgery and examine you to give you best advice if a breast reduction is suitable for you.
I have fat lump on one of my arms. How can this be treated?
fat lumb under the arm
Jan 20, 2023
The fat lump can be removed. You should however have some investigations ( ultrasound) prior to make sure this is fatty tissue only, as at times other tissue ( for instance blood vessels can form similar lumps) can be the cause.
The lumb can be removed, either with liposuction alone of liposuction and skin resection, to make both arms symmetric.
Labiaplasty and Childbirth – is it best to wait until after I’ve had my final child before gettin
labiaplasty and childbirth
Jan 20, 2023
You can go ahead with a labiaplasty even if you are having more children. You should however leave a gap between the surgery and the last child of minimum 6 months, to allow the labia to fully settle again.
Labia will change with the normal aging process, they tend to get thinner and laxer.
