Breast implants are very durable

Breast implants are very durable and can be used by professional athletes and body builders. I once had a professional snow skier whom I had placed implants involved in a nasty crash on a Colorado ski slope.

I saw her afterward, she had 3 broken ribs and bruising up and down her body, but her implants were intact and good as new! These implants are designed to hold up to rather severe trauma and even if they did break or rupture, implants have a life time warranty, and will be replaced by the manufacturer for free.

Saline implants and rippling

All saline implants eventually ripple in my opinion. This occurs most commonly when a woman has diminshed breast tissue or when an implant is placed subglandular. As women age, the breast tissue decreases and this will lead to an increased risk of rippling.

Very Large Breast Implants are More Problematic Over Time

We have a good variety of implant sizes and dimensions available in the United States. For patients that want to use larger implant sizes, often they will be disappointed if they don’t choose a higher profile implant.
There are limits and trade-offs, however, and in no case would I suggest that a patient use an implant that too wide for the breast. For women that do desire higher profile implants, it is important to recognize that this will stretch the tissues more and is expected to cause more relaxation and thinning of the tissues over time, particularly if a person’s breast tissues are thin and less supportive.

It is likely that revision surgery and/or breast lift would be required more rapidly in such a patient, particularly if such a patient chooses to “downsize” the implant size in the future. For breast implant surgery, it is important to discuss your goals and your implant size choice with an experienced plastic surgeon.

Breast implant position over or under muscle

Breast implants can be placed on top of the muscle or under the muscle. One way is not better than the other but there are differences. Generally, any implant placed under the muscle has less risk of palpability and rippling than an implant placed on top of the muscle.

However, because of the anatomic shape of the pectoralis major muscle, it is still possible to develop palpability and rippling in the lower, outer region of the breast.

Massaging Breasts after Breast Augmentation

Most surgeons recommend that patients massage the breasts after surgery for 2 weeks. The proper massage technique will be taught to you at your post-operative appointment. Breast implant massage performed the right way is very important to ensure that the scar tissue around the implant heals in such as a way as to provide ample room for the implant to move around comfortably.

Breast implant size.

There are many variables that go into a breast implant including the base diameter and the quality of the skin and the patient’s goals and expectations regarding a look at that they are seeking. As a general rule a high profile implant is a more narrow implant with more projection that tends to cause a round look.
A moderate and or moderate profile plus implant is a slightly wider diameter implant slightly less projection. Discussed your goals and expectations and some potential representative postoperative pictures that you find attractive with your potential plastic surgeon.

Many physicians may also choose some intra-operative sizing which can help with this decision.
