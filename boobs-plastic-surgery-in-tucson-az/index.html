Massage Following Breast Augmentation

When a breast implant in Tucson AZ is placed, your body will naturally form a lining around the implant called a capsule. This capsule is a natural response of your body to the foreign object. It is probably your body’s way of separating itself from the foreign object.

Ideally, this capsule should be open enough to provide for movement of the implant. If this capsule should shrink in volume then it can restrict the movement of the implant leading to a more firm and less natural feel of the breast.

Breast massage after breast augmentation is intended to help keep this capsule open. Usually, massage can be begun three to four weeks after the procedure. It can be performed in the first three weeks if it is done very carefully.

Excessive movement or trauma to the breast in the first three weeks can cause postoperative bleeding. Each surgeon will have their own approach and opinion regarding postoperative massage. It is very important that you follow the direction of your surgeon.

Breast implants – 6 key factors that should help you choose a size

The choice of implant size depends on multiple factors, including, amongst other factors:

  1. Current breast size and shape;
  2. Extent of ptosis (droopiness);
  3. Chest wall shape and more specifically the base width of the breast;
  4. Extent of enlargement that you are hoping to achieve;
  5. Type of implant being used (profile of implant, saline vs. silicone gel implants, shape of implant (round vs. contoured implants);
  6. Body habitus, such as height, waist and hip size.

I find that the wider one’s chest is, the larger the implant that would be tolerated. Also taller patients as well as those that have wider hips do better with larger implants.

The goal of the operation for most patients is not to look ‘top-heavy’, but rather for the implants to complement the rest of the body, so that if you wear a tight shirt or bathing suit, the operation will be evident, however if you choose to wear a loose shirt people may not know that you did anything.
