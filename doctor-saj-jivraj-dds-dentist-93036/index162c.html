How long does an All-on-4 denture implant last, in years?
last
Dec 18, 2016
How long it lasts depends on a number of things

1. Cleaning and looking after them

2. If you wear a night guard – inevitably you will put more force on your teeth

3. Material used to fabricate the teeth – Zirconia is the strongest and may last longer

You should be prepared for some complications of a period of time such as teeth chipping and breakage. The restoration will be designed to be retrievable so these problems can readily be fixed.
