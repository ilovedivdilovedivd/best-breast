Can You Exercise After Botox or Will It Decrease the Results?
No strenuous exercise for the first 24 hours after exercise
Jan 29, 2018
I ask that my patients do not perform strenuous exercise for the first 24 hours after botox injections. This means no weight lifting or exercise where they are straining the facial muscles. I also recommend not massaging the area and not lying down for a few hours immediately after the injections. Mild cardiovascular exercise such as treadmill has not been shown to cause any adverse reactions in my 18 year experience with injecting this product.
