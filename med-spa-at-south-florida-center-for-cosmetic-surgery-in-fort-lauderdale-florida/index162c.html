What are the disadvantages of having hanging columella?
Hanging columella is a purely cosmetic issue
Mar 9, 2022
Hanging columella can cause aesthetic discomfort, however, in most patients, there are no known functional problems associated with it. Hanging columella can occur naturally or as a result of the previous rhinoplasty. It can be fixed surgically if you wish to do so.
