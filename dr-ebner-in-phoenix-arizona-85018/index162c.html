What is my best option for a discoloured front tooth due to a root canal procedure many years a
Root canal teeth are generally crowned
Jul 31, 2017
Because of the tooth structure lost preparing a root canal in a tooth, a full coverage crown will provide the strength needed for longevity. There are multiple options for blocking out a discolored tooth. whether a crown or veneer is placed. Erring on the side of conserving tooth structure by providing inadequate strength or cosmetic results, especially for a front tooth, is not recommended. Its the standard of care in the US that an endodontically treated tooth be covered with a full crown. Endodontically treated teeth are weaker. They’ve been filed through the center. Its also thought that the tooth structure in general is more brittle.
