The bone my dental implant is anchored to is gone and my implant is getting looser. What do I do if the wh
Loose implant repair
Nov 16, 2015
If you lose the implant, you can re-graft the site, wait a few months depending on where the implant is located, and then re-place the implant. However, the more important question is determining what caused it to fail the first time or else you are doomed to failure in the future as well. Is the bite on it bad?, Was there lack of hygiene control? Would having a wider or longer implant work better? The “why?” is more important than the “how”.
