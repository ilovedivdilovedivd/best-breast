Can a mid facelift help the jaw line?
Mid Facelift and Jaw line
Mar 16, 2016
A mid facelift mainly improves the look of the cheeks and nasolabial folds. It is too far removed from the jaw line to really help with that area. A mini facelift or lower facelift will typically addresses the jaw line.
Will Breast Keep Growing After 23?
Fully Developed Breasts
Mar 16, 2016
At age 23 your breasts should be fully developed. If you maintain your same body weight it will be unlikely that you will see an increase in breast size.
Will my breast even out and look the same size after draining a hematoma and how long will it take
Six Months
Mar 16, 2016
When you have two different size breasts to begin with and add a hematoma on top of that, it can take as long as 6 months for your breasts to even out. As long as you are seeing an improvement each month there is a good chance it can still happen. I suggest taking photos every month to help you keep track of this.
Does Medicaid cover this type of problem?
Medicaid coverage of breast surgery
Nov 1, 2015
Every state is different in what Medicaid will and will not cover. Best to check with a Plastic Surgeon in your State and see what they can do.
Breast Augmentation only?
Breast augmentation after pregnancy
Nov 1, 2015
Most women will not require a lift if the nipples are at or above the breast crease. Nipple asymmetry typically remains the same after breast augmentation. Your Plastic surgeon can help you choose the best size to meet your desires.
Boob in your armpit?
Post-operative concerns after breast augmentation
Nov 1, 2015
Any concerns about the results of your breast augmentation should be directed to your Plastic Surgeon. There are a number of possible reasons the implant(s) might feel like they’re in your armpit. It really takes 6 months for everything to settle down and for you to see your final result. I strongly recommend that you be up front with your surgeon about any such concerns.
I hate my breasts. What can I do?
Breast enlargement without surgery
Nov 1, 2015
Breasts are typically enlarged with either an implant, fat grafting or the use of a suction device (Brava Bra). Unfortunately they all have a similar price. I am not aware of any other treatment that will successfully enlarge the breasts.
