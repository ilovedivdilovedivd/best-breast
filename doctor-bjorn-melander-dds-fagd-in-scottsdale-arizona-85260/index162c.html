Do I need an orthodontist for my Invisalign?
Invisalign providers are different
Jun 28, 2019
Invisalign is very much a tool for moving your teeth, just as a hammer is for building a house. Experience and knowledge using Invisalign can yield vastly different outcomes. You might ask for example before and after cases and how long they took to finish to evaluate your provider’s expertise using Invisalign.
