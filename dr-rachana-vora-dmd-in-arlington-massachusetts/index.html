What is the best option to fix my broken front tooth?
Restoring broken front tooth with crown
Jun 17, 2019
Since the tooth is broken in half, I would recommend a stronger restoration like a crown. There would be a high risk of breaking/chipping the tooth again if it is bonded. Depending on the vitality of the pulp, root canal therapy may be needed if the nerve is affected due to trauma. First step would be to get a xray and assess the health of the tooth.
I chipped my tooth, is it small enough to just polish or do I need to do bonding?
Chipped tooth
Jun 17, 2019
The tooth will need to be bonded to restore it back to its original shape.
If you are not particularly concerned about the cosmetic appearance of the tooth, then you can choose to leave it alone or polish it, but you may risk chipping more of the tooth over time.

Hollywood Smile.
