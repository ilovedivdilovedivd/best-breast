Statement: Dr. Michelle Barnes
Medical Director for Dermacare Brickell

Dr Michelle Barnes is the Medical Director for Dermacare Brickell Laser Rejuvenation and Anti-Aging Center of South Florida. She has dedicated the last 18 years of her career to aesthetic medicine. Dr. Barnes is board certified in Internal Medicine and Anti-Aging and Regenerative Medicine. She specializes in aesthetic anti-aging rejuvenation.

Known for her talent and artistic skill with facial cosmetic injectables, Dr. Michelle Barnes believes in helping patients look and feel their best by creating a beautiful, youthful, yet natural looking appearance. She takes an individualized approach and customizes her technique based on the desired outcome of each patient in her care.

Dr. Michelle Barnes received her Medical Degree from the Medical School at the University of Miami, and completed her post-doctoral training and residency program in Internal Medicine at the University of Miami and Jackson Memorial Medical Center in Miami.

Her goal for every patient is to help them achieve a better version of themselves in the safest and most effective manner. Dr Barnes leads the Dermacare team and is lovingly referred to as our Queen “B” by her loyal Dermagirls.
