Dear Mrs Al-Beyrouthy,
Your face shouldn’t get disfigured if you get your nasolabial folds injected with hyaluronic acid fillers by a certified plastic surgeon or dermatologist.
Gaining weight might make your nasolabial folds look better; however moderate to severe weight loss (generally more than 6 kgs) can make your nasolabial folds look worse. Mild weight loss (2 kgs in your case) shouldn’t affect the nasolabial fold filler injection in a negative way.
