How much longer will I nee to wear braces since since my teeth have moved so quickly?
when someone is ‘straight’ enough to remove braces ?
Oct 31, 2015
Intial alignment of front teeth can happen with in the first 6 months BUT making the bite ( occlusion and overbite) correct and allowing for the teeth to remain straight for years to come takes longer. An orthodontist can usually predict how fast that is happening IF cooperation with elastics etc is happening.
Front teeth still not straight after braces. Any suggestions?
final appearance of upper teeth after braces are removed.
Oct 30, 2015
Often times after ideal alignment of front teeth ( even the roots that you can’t see) the teeth are in a very healthy position BUT the edges of the teeth AND the shape of the teeth are still distracting ( don’t look ‘straight’). By simple contouring artistically the edges and shapes a almost perfect result can occur. Cosmetic dentist are artistic and adept at performing this procedure called ‘ENAMELPLASTY’. Some orthodontist perform this routinely at the retainer delivery.
