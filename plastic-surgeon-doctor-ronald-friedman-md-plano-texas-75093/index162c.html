How old you generally have to be to get Dermal Fillers and is there a difference between surgeon's
Dermal fillers can be used at age 18 and up
Apr 10, 2022
Dermal fillers can be useful in enlarging the lips, camouflaging a nasal hump, or treating deep acne scarring as young as age 18, the age of legal adulthood. Typically fillers are not used in a 20 year-old for facial wrinkles because you haven't had enough time to form significant wrinkles. I'm not aware of differing policies for minimize age at a plastic surgeon's office versus a medspa.
