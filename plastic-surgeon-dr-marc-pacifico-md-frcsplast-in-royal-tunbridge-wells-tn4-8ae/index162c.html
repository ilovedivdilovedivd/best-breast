Will a mini-facelift work?
Mar 6, 2021
Many thanks for your question. From what I can see in your photographs, you really would require a lower face and neck lift to achieve meaningful and lasting results.
I would not expect too much longevity from a more mini approach, even when combined with radiofrequency skin tightening.

Can a deep plane facelift worsen malar bags?
Can a facelift worsen malar bags?
Mar 6, 2021
Many thanks for posting your question. Without seeing your photos it is hard to comment specifically, but yes, some people's malar bags can be more prominent after a facelift (deep plane or other approach). If you have concerns about this, discuss with your plastic surgeon and see if there are additional procedures that might benefit you - either at the same time as the facelift or perhaps in the future once things have settled down.
Good luck taking things forwards.
