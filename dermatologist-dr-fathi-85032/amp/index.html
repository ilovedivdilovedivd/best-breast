How do I get rid of these tiny bumps on my face/perioral dermatitis?
Fordyce spots
Aug 7, 2020
Hi Kashaia222, these bumps are called Fordyce spots. They are normal, superficial oil glands seen in approximately 80% of the population. They can appear at any point in life but the incidence of appearance increases with age. These are typically not treated, however, in the motivated patient, a good place to start is a topical retinoid.
