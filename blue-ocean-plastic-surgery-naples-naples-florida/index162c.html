William A. Ross, MD

Name: William A. Ross, MD
Statement: Dr. William A. Ross is a Plastic Surgeon who was born in St. Louis, Missouri. He was trained to perform the full spectrum of Plastic Surgery, including reconstructive and aesthetic surgery. He began his career in the university setting, training residents, students and nurses in Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery. He now focuses exclusively on aesthetic surgery. Dr. Ross performs brow lifts, blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery), face lifts, neck rejuvenation and laser procedures. He has published multiple peer-reviewed articles, including the use of fat grafting for facial rejuvenation. He has extensive experience in breast reconstruction, breast enhancement and body shaping, which includes liposuction, abdominoplasty (“tummy tuck”), body lifts and rejuvenation of the arms, legs and hands. Dr. Ross utilizes both traditional and innovative therapies to deliver the best results possible.
