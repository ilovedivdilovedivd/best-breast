Statement: Dr. Aaron D. Gorin is a board-certified plastic surgeon with over 16 years of experience, serving patients in Tualatin, Oregon, and the greater Portland area.

Dr. Gorin specializes in breast augmentation, breast lifts, tummy tucks, and complete mommy makeovers. His practice, Gorin Plastic Surgery & Medspa, also offers a wide range of noninvasive treatments, including neuromodulators like Botox and Disport, dermal fillers, and laser resurfacing, chemical peels, and microdermabrasion.

Dr. Gorin is a native Oregonian who graduated with honors from the University of Oregon before earning his medical degree from the George Washington University School of Medicine in Washington, D.C. He then completed a five-year residency in general surgery at Boston’s St. Elizabeth Medical Center before returning to Oregon for a three-year fellowship in hand, plastic, and reconstructive surgery at Portland’s Oregon Health Sciences University.

Sixteen years ago, Dr. Gorin opened his private practice in Tualatin, Oregon, where he is renowned for his warm bedside manner, thoughtful approach to consultations, and impeccable, natural-looking results. He ensures that every patient is informed of all their options, and every procedure is carefully tailored to their needs and desired aesthetic goals.

His practice boasts a fully accredited outpatient surgery suite, so patients can enjoy private, attentive treatment in comfort, instead of being treated at a hospital.

Dr. Gorin is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgeons. In addition to his work at his Tualatin practice, Dr. Gorin stays active in the medical community as a member of several professional organizations, including the American Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgeons and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.

When he’s not working, Dr. Gorin enjoys cheering on the Oregon Ducks football team in addition to playing golf, snowboarding, and hiking, as well as spending time with his wife, twin boys, and dachshunds, Mozart and Schubert.
