Question about “roll” under eyes
Bunching of under-eye muscle and skin
Dec 7, 2022
The circular muscle around your eye (orbicularis oculi) has fibers in this area that bunch up, along with the underlying skin.
A tiny sprinkle of botox in this area can soften this, but it will not eliminate the issue.
Addressing the skin and/or the muscle is the definitive fix. Laser, blepharoplasty are two options that may be considered.
