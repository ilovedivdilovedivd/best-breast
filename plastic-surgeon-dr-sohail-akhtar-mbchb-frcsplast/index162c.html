Statement: Dr. Sohail Akhtar is a General Medical Council certified plastic surgeon with over 20 years in practice. Specializing in plastic surgery for more than a decade, Dr. Akhtar performs a wide range of surgical procedures to improve the appearance of the breasts, body, and face. His greatest expertise lies in breast enhancement, tummy tucks, liposuction, and complete mommy makeovers.

Dr. Akhtar was first inspired to pursue a career in surgery while completing an elective placement in cardiac transplant surgery in London. After graduation from medical school at Liverpool University, he embarked on surgical training and was awarded membership into the Royal College of Surgeons (FRCS).

He further honed his craft in cosmetic surgery with the National Health Service as a consultant plastic surgeon in Durham. He then became a consultant at the prestigious Wrightington Hospital in Lancashire, where he currently performs a wide range of plastic surgery procedures.

Beyond the NHS, Dr. Akhtar frequently performs cosmetic procedures in three CQC Certified hospitals: the BMI Highfield Hospital in Rochdale, North Manchester; the BMI Huddersfield Hospital in West Yorkshire; and the Wilmslow Hospital in Cheshire. He believes that the quality of the patient journey is as important as the result.

In addition to his plastic surgery practice, Dr. Akhtar is deeply involved with charitable medical aid. He provided emergency surgical relief in Pakistan after the devastating 2005 earthquake and he has gone on several charitable missions to Sierra Leone.
