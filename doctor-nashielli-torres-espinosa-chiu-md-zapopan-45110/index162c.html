Is it safe to combine a facelift and brow lift with liposuction? 43 year old healthy
Combining Facelift with other Surgeries
Nov 4, 2023
In my opinion and in my practice, I will only perform a facelift and will not combine with other procedures. The reason is safety as doing a proper facelift involves around 6 hours in the operating room. Trying to add liposuction, as posted in your question, is too much time under anesthesia. I advise patients to decide which procedure is their priority and start with that surgery, then return for the second surgery. Health and safety come first!
I am hearing that a lot of facelifts don't last past 5 years. Is this true?
Longevity of Facelift Results
Nov 4, 2023
This is a complex question as longevity is not just the surgery itself. Results involve the quality and elasticity of the skin as well as following post op care instructions and using sunscreen at least once per day. Other factors than can effect longevity are genetics, diet and smoking.
