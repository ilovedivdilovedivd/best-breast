Remove skin from jawline?
Direct excision over the jawline to excise skin would not produce improved contour of the jawline
Oct 25, 2021
A facelift is designed to conceal the incision while removing some excess skin and tightening deeper structures to create the results you are inquiring about. Direct excision over the jawline to excise skin would not produce improved contour of the jawline as it would not address the deeper structures; and it would not be an optimal incision location. I would suggest being evaluated by a plastic surgeon so that you can receive a recommendation tailored to your goals.
