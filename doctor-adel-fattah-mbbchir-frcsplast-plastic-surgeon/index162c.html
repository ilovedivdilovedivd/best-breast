After several months, new channels form across the scar and you find that such swelling subsides.

Should I get tummy tuck or just liposuction?
Excess skin?
Aug 13, 2019
The answer lies in whether you would be left with excess skin or not. Something that only really be decided in person. Basically, liposuction will remove fat and deflate the skin - in many cases this skin tightens. But if you have lots of fat removed loose skin can be left behind. A tummy tuck will remove fat and skin leaving you with a flat abdomen.
