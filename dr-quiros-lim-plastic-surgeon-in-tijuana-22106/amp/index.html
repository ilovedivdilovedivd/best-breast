What is the best procedure to tighten skin after liposuction?
Case analysis
Sep 9, 2023
Hi, It's hard to analyze skin looseness by seeing a picture, but in case you have loose skin, there are multiple radiofrequency devices that can help you tight skin, this can be performed as a ambulatory procedure with topical anesthesia. In case you have severe skin laxity then a surgical procedure will be your best option. Hope this is helpful.
Am I candidate for liposuction without a mini tummy?
Case analysis
Sep 9, 2023
Hi, after analyzing your pictures I can say that you have a nice body shape, with minimal excess of skin (based on the pictures). If you are trying to avoid a mini tummy tuck you can consider a Liposuction (to address the small amount of subcutaneous fat) in combination with BodyTite to tight the skin and have a soft definition in your abdomen. Ask your plastic surgeon if he offers this treatment, or in case he doesn't have it what are his recommendations to tight the skin in your abdomen....

Weight loss after BBL & tummy tuck - what surgery would I benefit from?
Case analysis
Sep 9, 2023
Hi, After analyzing your photos, I can asses that you have some extra skin in the arms and armpit area. You are a candidate for a Brachioplasty. The importance of having an In-Person consultation is that we can physically examine the quality of the skin and the amount of tissue that can be removed in order to have the best results. Schedule a consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon so he can give you option for your specific case. Hope this is helpful.
Is lipo the best choice?
Personal opinion
Sep 9, 2023
Hi, In case you have small amount of excess of skin and fat in the subgluteal area (banana rolls), liposuction can help decrease this rolls, in some cases skin tightening devices combined in the same liposuction can help you improve that area. Cellulite can be difficult to treat. In my opinion go to an In-person consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon so he can suggest you the best option for treatment. Hope this is helpful.
BMI 27, age 39, is it a 'waste' to get stomach lipo as I am trying to conceive for 2nd child?
Opinion
Sep 9, 2023
In my personal opinion you should first finish having babies, and then try to undergo an aesthetic procedure. With some fertility treatments women tend to gain weight and have some changes in their skin, compromising the possible results obtained with a Lipo. Hope this will help you.
