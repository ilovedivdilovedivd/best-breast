Dr Pousti Breast Augmentation Prices

Breast augmentation is the most common cosmetic surgery in the United States because it provides a consistent way for a woman to change the shape, position and size of her breasts.

After a breast augmentation surgery, it is possible that the patients may heal differently and may develop some asymmetry.

It is also possible that one implants may appear slightly lower than the other.

If you are concerned, be sure that you’re working with a board-certified plastic surgeon who has a great deal of experience in breast augmentation and breast lift surgery.

They will be able to examine your implant, assess your breast tissue, and provide you with techniques that will help correct your concerns.

Relax, a qualified Plastic Surgeon wants to answer your questions

Most qualified doctors should feel comfortable answering your questions and should take the time with you. Here are a few you should ask.

1) Are you a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon, by the American Board of Plastic Surgery? You don’t just want a “cosmetic surgeon”. That can be anyone from a Dermatologist, Internist to a Dentist.

2) How many Breast Augmentations and Breast Lift procedures do you perform in a year? If they only do breast surgery occasionally, go somewhere else. You want a doctor who does at least 50 augmentations per year.

3) Do you use both Saline and Silicone Gel implants? What percentage of each do you use? Do you use just round low profile implants or others like moderate and high profile? You want to have some options.
Your body type will dictate which implant is best for you. I prefer a silicone gel implant, but there is a cost difference which can affect your decision. A gel implant also requires a bigger incision.

You will also be recommended to have an MRI after 3-5 years to evaluate the implant. You need to feel comfortable with the doctor. Ask to see pictures of before and afters. Realize that doctors won’t show you bad results, but you must feel good about the results you do see.

Make sure your expectation of size and shape match your Plastic Surgeon’s Good luck with your consultation

Small degree of lifting possible with breast implants

There is a SMALL degree of lifting that can be acheived with an implant, but this is only useful for patients that have excess lower breast skin, not patients who have low nipples. If your nipple is below the level of the fold of the breast behind, then only surgical elevation of the nipple will give an acceptable result.
Also, as you mention, any implant used to fill up the breast will also increase your size. When it comes to decisions like this the best answer is to have a face to face consultation with a board-certified plastic surgeon who can examine you and help decide the best procedure for your particular situation.

Steam Room, Sauna, Jacuzzi’s and Breast Implants

Steam rooms, saunas, jacuzzi’s, hot baths will not affect your implants wheather they are saline or silicone gel. Your core body temperature (where the implants are) will remain within a few degrees of normal regardless of the outside temperature.

Silicone by the way can withstands large degrees of heat without breakdown

It’s great that you’re doing this research. I always say that the best patients are informed patients. You might also want to research your options for breast implants. Choosing the right implant is the number one concern among women considering breast augmentation.

Did you know, there’s actually a way to select a implant shape, size, and profile that is perfect for you? A term that I use with my patients for the perfect implant is the “Pony Implant”.

So what do I mean by “perfect”? Well, a Pony Implant has three qualities to it. First, the implant meets your beauty goals. For example, you want to your breasts to look fuller while still appearing natural.
Second, when you chose your Pony Implant, you walk out of your consultation 100% confident that you’ve chosen the right shape and size for you. In other words, you won’t be second guessing your decision, and you won’t be afraid of having gone too big or too small.

And third, after your procedure, you are thrilled with your results, and say, “I’m so happy. This is exactly what I wanted!” That’s the Pony Implant. And the great news is that there is a simple process to go about finding yours.

This issue of selecting the right implant is so important when it comes to patient satisfaction or dissatisfaction that, again, I really encourage you to learn more about it.

Do I need a lift or will a dual plane do the trick?

Thin skin and sagging breasts are a combination that will inevitably stretch more with time after an augmentation. Even if you look okay initially, your tissue will probably stretch and sag more in a short period of time (months to a year).

Your best overall result will be with a lift and augmentation combined.

Breast implants whenever you are ready…

I agree- as long as your breasts are no longer swollen and engorged with milk, and as long as you are not planning more children in the near future, I think it would be safe for you to proceed whenever you feel ready for your consultation.

My usual rule of thumb is to wait 4-6 months after delivery or the last breast feeding, or until the patient feels a complete return to her pre-pregnancy breast size and consistency.
Breast implants at over 50

There is no reason specifially based on age to limit having breast implants. I think that if you are in good health and desire a fuller look, there is nothing wrong with that and you certainly can have surgery.

Given your goals, it appears that you could achieve a nice result with 475 cc implants. As long has you have clearly voiced your surgical goals to your operating surgeon and he or she is board-certified in plastic surgery as well as an expert in the breast augmentation with implants procedure, I would proceed with what you have chosen.

Breast augmentation sizing

Sizing can be a very personal decision when it comes to breast augmentation. I would recommend revisiting your surgeon’s office (or additional offices if you want more than one opinion) and try different sizes.
I often encourage patients to bring different clothing and even friends or significant others to help in determining a range of sizes that they would be comfortable with. For every patient, there is a range of sizes and shapes that can be used.

If you are wanting smaller, then this should be feasible.

Inverted nipples and breast implant augmentation

It is possible for the pressure of the implants to push out inverted nipples a bit but not fully correct the problem. Make sure these are long-standing issues as a new inverted nipple can be the first sign of breast cancer.
