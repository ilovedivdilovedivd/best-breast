Statement: Dr. Gökhan Haytoğlu is a plastic surgeon based in the Nişantaşı neighborhood of Istanbul, Turkey. At his private practice, he specializes in body shaping, liposuction, tummy tucks, and breast augmentation.

“I’m known for using the safest and detailed methods for realistic results,” says Dr. Haytoğlu. “I love seeing dramatic transformations on patients’ bodies.”

Dr. Haytoğlu earned his medical degree from Hacettepe University Medical School in Ankara, Turkey. During medical school, he traveled to the United States as a visiting student at the Washington University Orthopaedic Research Lab, in St. Louis, Missouri, then returned to Hacettepe University to complete his studies. He ranked in the top 1% in his Turkish Medical Board Examination.

He went on to complete a residency in Plastic, Reconstructive, and Aesthetic Surgery at Istanbul University, Cerrahpaşa Medical School. After passing his United States Medical Licensing Exam, he was granted an Educational Commission for Foreign Medical Graduates certificate to begin a residency at Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio to study Aesthetic Facial and Nasal Surgery and Migraine Surgery under Dr. Bahman Guyuron.

Dr. Haytoğlu returned to Turkey to work for two years at Kirklareli State Hospital, followed by a stint in the Istanbul Training and Research Hospital. He then headed back to the U.S. for a fellowship in Post-Bariatric Surgery – Body Contouring at El Paso Cosmetic Surgery in Texas, where he trained with Dr. Ozan Sozer.

Dr. Haytoğlu is a member of the Turkish Society of Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, the Turkish Association of Trauma and Emergency Surgery, the Turkish Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, the International Society of Aesthetic and Plastic Surgeons, and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons. He has also co-authored numerous published studies.
