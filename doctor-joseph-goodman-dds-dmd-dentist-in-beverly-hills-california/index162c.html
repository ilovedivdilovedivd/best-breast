Using veneer or crown on current tooth to mimic a canine tooth, is it possible?
porcelain veneers on canines
Nov 2, 2020
Porcelain veneers can be used when you have a tooth that is not a canine, but is in the canine position. Changing the shape to make it look like a canine is easy and very commonly done with veneers. It’s important to achieve the right bite, ” canine guidance” is very important.
You should see if your current bite is causing you headaches. Best of luck
Joseph Goodman DDS Cosmetic Dentistry Beverly Hills
