Keloid Scaring
May 18, 2019
Hi, thanks for your question.
Yes, from your photos, you have post-operative keloid scars. These are a known complication of any surgical procedure and are very difficult to manage. Treatments range from massage, pressure clips, steroid injection, silicone application and in some cases even radiotherapy. Surgery is of course an option but with a high risk of recurrence. Hopefully you will have had good advice following your consultation with a plastic surgeon.
All the best.

When can I start showering after tummy tuck? Nurse says I can’t.
Showering following abdominoplasty
Jan 6, 2019
Thanks for your question. The aim with showering advice, as well as general wound care advice, is to potentially reduce the risk of post operative infections and wound complications. Any advice that your surgeon has given regarding wound care in the first few weeks following surgery is important to follow. In my practice, as long as the drains are removed then light showering can commence. Up until that point, sponge washes are recommended.
