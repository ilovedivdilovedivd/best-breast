As the founder, owner, and medical director of Madison Plastic Surgery, Dr. Tornambe keeps up-to-date on cutting-edge technologies in plastic surgery. In addition to performing a wide range of surgical procedures—including breast augmentation, liposuction, facelifts, rhinoplasty, eyelid surgery, and lip augmentation—he also offers nonsurgical aesthetic services, such as Botox, thread lifts, fillers (like Juvéderm and Radiesse), and fractional CO2 laser resurfacing.

Dr. Tornambe is known for his signature facial rejuvenation treatment, the Non-Surgical Makeover: a combination of Botox, laser resurfacing, chemical peels, and dermal fillers to address age-related concerns, such as wrinkles, volume loss, hyperpigmentation, and more. This combination results in a refreshed, rejuvenated, and younger-looking appearance, all without the downtimes or risks associated with surgery.

First and foremost, Dr. Tornambe is a physician, dedicated to each of his patient’s unique needs and individual beauty. In his book, “The Beauty Quotient Formula,” he encourages women to let go of the urge to compare themselves to unrealistic celebrity beauty standards. Instead, he invites them to “embrace those attributes that make them beautiful, and learn to accentuate them. Learn to celebrate your best features and set realistic, achievable goals regarding your own beauty,” he says. “It is OK to look like yourself and not someone else.”

Guided by this empowering philosophy, Dr. Tornambe works with his patients to customize each procedure to create natural-looking results that enhance their natural beauty. Building strong relationships with his patients, with honesty and communication, are key to his practice.

Dr. Tornambe embarked on his surgical training with both a general surgery residency and hand surgery fellowship training at Cabrini Medical Center in New York City. Dr. Tornambe served as chief of the division of plastic surgery at Cabrini Medical Center in NYC for almost 20 years.
