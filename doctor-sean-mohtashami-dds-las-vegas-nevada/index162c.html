Implant material. Can zirconia implants be used instead of titanium implants?
Zirconia Implants
Apr 26, 2018
Hello Jo1001,Yes, it is possible to do all zirconia implants for single implants, but we don’t usually recommend them for patients. Research has found that because the implant and abutment have to be one piece with a zirconia, there is then no flexibility, there is no give which can cause issues in down the road.

Is it normal to have throbbing and somewhat intense pain in your mouth after getting a dental impl
Pain with Implants
Feb 5, 2018
There certainly can be some pain and discomfort, especially only an hour after surgery. Take your pain meds as directed and make sure to take the next round BEFORE you start to feel the effects of the last round wear off. If you take your meds after you’re starting to feel pain, it’s probably going to hurt as the medicine needs some time to circulate through your body and start working.If the throbbing continues for a few days, then it would be prudent to see your dentist.Good luck!
