Should I get a crown on my lower molar?

Apr 26, 2019
It is always better to crown the tooth if a crack is confirmed in a tooth to protect the tooth from complete fracture. Usually if the tooth is not root canal treated then there is pain to biting on little hard object. Pain vanishes instantly. Bite test with Tooth slooth by the dentist will confirm it. Better to crown the tooth soon.
