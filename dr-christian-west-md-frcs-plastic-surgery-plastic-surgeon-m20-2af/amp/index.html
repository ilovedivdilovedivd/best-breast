Mini or full tummy tuck?
Mini or full tummy tuck?
Jun 28, 2018
You’d get a much better result from a full (standard) tummy tuck.
A mini tummy tuck deals only with loose skin below the navel, or it is sometimes good for improving an unsightly Caesarian section scar. However is could distort the skin above the navel and leave loose skin and bulges in the upper tummy.
A full tummy tuck removes excess skin and flattens the tummy both above and below the navel. I believe you’d be more satisfied and happy with the results.
