Asian Rhinoplasty
Apr 9, 2019
In your situation, a limited procedure such as an alar base reduction, would not be enough to address your concerns.  Typically in Asian Rhinoplasty, the bridge is raised, the tip is reshaped/projected and the alar base is narrowed.  I feel you would need all of these maneuvers to achieve an optimal result.  Please seek a board certified surgeon with sufficient experience in Asian Rhinoplasty
