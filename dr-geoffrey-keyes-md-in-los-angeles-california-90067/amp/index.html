Statement: Dr. Keyes is a double board-certified plastic surgeon in Los Angeles with more than 40 years of surgical experience. He offers procedures for the face, breast, body, and skin, but his primary expertise is in rhinoplasty and nasal reconstruction. “Rhinoplasty is one of the most important operations for improving the appearance of the face, and it has the most dramatic change on assimilating and balancing facial features,” says Dr. Keyes. “There is nothing more rewarding than taking a cast off of a patient and seeing the surprise and happiness on their face because they’ve had the transformation that they wished to achieve.”

Dr. Keyes received his medical degree from Loyola University Stretch School of Medicine and completed an internship at Washington University. He then completed three residencies, in otolaryngology (head and neck surgery), general surgery surgery, and plastic surgery.

Dr. Keyes is board certified by both the American Board of Plastic Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology — Head and Neck Surgery. He’s a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, and a fellow of the American College of Surgeons.

In addition to a commitment to create the best surgical results possible, Dr. Keyes prioritizes patient safety. He performs surgery at accredited surgical centers and hospitals, and works with a dedicated team of board-certified anesthesiologists and registered nurses to provide the highest level of care. “There are two components to having surgery: one is the technical result to achieve the outcome, while the other is going through the intraoperative and postoperative course safely,” says Dr. Keyes. Having served as president of the American Association for Accreditation of Ambulatory Surgery Facilities, he is considered an expert in patient safety.
