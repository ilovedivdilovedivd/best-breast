Is it safe to wax my face after a tca peel?
Waxing after TCA peel
Oct 28, 2013
I would suggest waiting a full month from the date of the peel, or until all signs of inflammation (redness/irritation) have subsided, whichever comes first. If waxing is done too soon, the risk of discoloration due to inflammation (also known as postinflammatory hyperpigmentation) is greater.
How Can I Get Rid of Under Eye Wrinkles?
Under-eye wrinkles
Oct 2, 2013
If under-eye wrinkles are present because of loose skin of the lower eyelid (and not a different cause such as atopic dermatitis or allergies), then tightening of the skin with chemical peeling or laser resurfacing can reduce the wrinkles. You may want to undergo a trial of a good eye cream first, for a month or two, before undertaking the risks associated with a laser or peeling procedure.
Is It Safe to Use Doryx 100mg Capsules Twice Per Day for the Treatment and Maintenance of Clear Skin
Long-term use of doxycycline
Oct 2, 2013
It is best to avoid daily doses of doxycycline higher than 40 mg long-term (that is, longer than 2-3 months continuously). 100 mg twice daily of doxycycline intermittently for acne flares could be considered. The risk of long-term use of antibiotic doses of doxycycline is that of bacterial resistance, not to mention a tendency for a decrease in effectiveness of the medication over time.
