Nano fat vs micro fat
Oct 21, 2022
Nano fat is prepared using filters with the goal of creating a liquid that can be injected directly into the skin. Because the process kills most of the fat cells, it is not used for volume. It is used for skin rejuvenation thanks to the stem cells that survive the process. Many plastic surgeons incorrectly use nano fat for volume. Can fat for a micro-autologous fat transfer come from the chin? Should I consider other areas?
Harvest sites for fat
Oct 21, 2022
The best fat for transfer will come from the abdomen flanks, or medial thigh. Hence, the survivability of those cells may be inferior.
