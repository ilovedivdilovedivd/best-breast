Necrotic and asking for advice

Hello
Looking at your photos, it is clear you have necrosis of the skin at the vertical scar. As the necrotic tissue is wet and the surrounding skin appears inflammed I would recommend the necrotic tissue is removed. The subsequent raw wound will need to be washed and dressed regularly to encourage wound healing. Scar revision can be performed at a later date once the tissue has completely settled and the scar matured.
Please see your surgeon as a matter of urgency.
