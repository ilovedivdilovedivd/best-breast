Silicone implant cost

The cost for breast augmentation will vary but the lowball fee will be at just over $5000. Most importantly, you must know what the cost is including. Some surgeons may operate in their own office based operating room, some may do it at a hospital and others may do the surgery in a surgery center.

The quality of the surgical location is critical and most patients dismiss this as secondary.

If your doctor’s surgical suite is something that appears as state of the art, it tells you something about that surgeon and what level of quality he expects.

Some may use a nurse anesthetist while others an anesthesiologist – ask why he/she is using a nurse instead of a doctor.

Would a nurse do the operation if they had plenty of experience? Whom is supervising the nurse in case of trouble (often it is a doctor).

The cost of the actual silicone implants are typically about a thousand dollars more than the cost of saline implants and the cost of the full package delivered should vary by about that amount as well.

I based my implant recommendation on each patient’s body (chest height, chest width, breast tissue, overall body shape) as well as her desired goal. Please share your desired goal with your plastic surgeon.

How to fix breast asymmetry?

As others have mentioned, in some patients simply changing the size of the implants on both sides is the answer. In others, there is more of a difference, requiring that something else be done to the skin.

In those patients, a breast augmentation along with a breast lift is the answer. A different amount of lift on one side compared to the other is often needed. Seek out a well-qualified plastic surgeon and trust their opinion.

Breast Implants and stretch marks

The occurrence of stretch marks after breast augmentation is rare. It may be more likely to occur in younger patients, patients with pre-existing stretch marks, and those seeking very large implants.

Your doctor should know the best implant size

It is true, your doctor will know the best breast implant size. It is also true that only you will know the best look for you.

Spend your effort on the look you want, and let your surgeon know clearly, and the implant size need not be your worry.

Ruptured breast implants

Saline breast implants are inert, so replacing them when ruptured can be done on your schedule. Silicone breast implants, when ruptured, should be replaced immediately.

Smoking and Surgery.

It is impossible to say for sure whether 12 days off cigarettes before surgery is long enough or not. In fact, I cannot even say whether you absolutely need to stop before surgery. All I can do is give you the statistics on wound healing, skin loss and poor scaring in smokers when compared to non-smokers.
Those statistics show that smokers are 20 times more likely to have the above-mentioned problems than are non-smokers in procedures such as the one you are contemplating. Smokers are also more likely to have pneumonia and infections, though the incidence is less than with the other problems.

Research has shown that it takes at least 2 months after quitting smoking to reduce these risks to acceptable levels, and up to a year to totally reverse them. What this means is that if a non-smoking population had a wound disruption rate if 0.

5% with the operation you are planning, the smokers would have a 10% rate. Therefore, there would still be 90% of the patients who would do well. I personally do not think that, in a cosmetic procedure, where we are trying to improve appearances, that this difference is worth the risk.
I, thus, ask all my patients for a lift (and especially one with an augmentation where the tissue is under more tension) to be off cigarettes for AT LEAST 2 months. That does not mean that I will not operate on someone who has been off cigarettes less than this.

For them, I require a thorough education of the risks and a separate permit accepting the increased risks of smoking. Ultimately, it is your choice. If you do decide to proceed with the procedure, you need to do so with full knowledge of the possible problems that can occur.
