Statement: Dr. David Nicholas Csikai, MD is a board-certified plastic surgeon in Jacksonville, Florida and Waycross, Georgia. The founder of First Coast Plastic Surgery, he offers a wide variety of cosmetic and plastic surgery procedures, including tummy tuck (abdominoplasty), breast augmentation, breast lift, rhinoplasty, liposuction, and mommy makeover. Dr. Csikai is proud to treat each patient that walks into his clinics with the latest innovations in plastic surgery treatments, alongside the kind of expertise that only comes from spending over 20 years in practice.

“My goal is to help people to function better, feel better about themselves, and have the confidence and ability to enjoy life to its fullest,” he says.

In addition to surgical procedures, First Coast Plastic Surgery offers facial rejuvenation and non-invasive treatments, including Botox, dermal filler injections, chemical peels, and more, at its med spa.

Dr. Csikai received his medical degree from Temple University Lewis Katz School of Medicine in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. After serving three years in the U.S. Navy, he later completed his general surgery residency at the University Medical Center in Jacksonville, Florida and trained in plastic and cosmetic surgery at the University of Kansas Medical Center and the Charlotte Plastic Surgery Center in North Carolina. There, he became an expert in state-of-the-art technology and techniques, including endoscopic surgery and laser surgery.

Dr. Csikai is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and is an active member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.
