Chemical peel before or after labiaplasty?
labiaplasty and peel
Oct 27, 2023
Hi there I would recommend to have the chemical peel after the labiaplasty is performed. There is a 8-12 weeks healing period. The peel will help to improve the labiaplasty scar if it is visible. Some of the dark areas are removed during the operation so less chemical solution will be needed. Best of luck and swift recovery!

It is not possible to answer this question with certainty due to lack of sufficient data. So far opinions are divided. We don’t even now for sure whether if you had a capsular contraction there is a higher risk with next surgeries. it is important for the surgeon to minimize the risk , there are well known factors such as bleeding and infection for example
Does breast implant removal reduce breast tissue?
Breast implant removal
Oct 11, 2023
Over time breast tissue will thin and stretch and loose volume and this happens even when you have breast implants. Removing the capsule, cauterization and any cutting results unfortunately in tissue loss. So you will be smaller when the implants and capsules are removed . It is impossible to say how much smaller than your original size because it depends on your weight as well
How would weight loss after breast reduction surgery affect my results? Would this
Weight loss after breast reduction
Sep 19, 2023
We always recommend to lose weight before any cosmetic surgery because there may be additional loose skin. If the breasts are very dense it may be due to breast gland and less fat so weight loss may not have such a severe effect. Many patients find it difficult to exercise with heavy breasts so it makes it challenging to lose weight. I would advise to lose maybe 10-12 pounds at least before the surgery and the rest after.
