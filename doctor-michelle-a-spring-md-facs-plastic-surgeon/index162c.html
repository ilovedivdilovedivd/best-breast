It is not uncommon to have small wounds during healing after an abdominoplasty, even if you are of a normal body weight and healthy.  Sometimes a small suture can cause an infection ("suture abscess"), similar to a large pimple.  The best option is to keep in close contact with your plastic surgeon's office and they should be able to guide you through this healing phase.Best wishes,Michelle Spring, MD
