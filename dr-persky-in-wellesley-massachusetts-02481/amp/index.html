Whitening Teeth with Braces on
Jan 31, 2019
Its very hard to whitening your teeth with the braces on. Its impossible to “bleach’ the areas behind the wires and attachments. If you attempted to whitened them now, you could end up with areas that have different shades of white. Its better now to use a good whitening toothpaste with an powered toothbrush and wait until the braces come off. There are really not many options at this time.
