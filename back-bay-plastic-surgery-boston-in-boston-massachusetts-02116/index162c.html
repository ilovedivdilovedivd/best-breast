Facial fat and RF Microneedling
Aug 25, 2019
RF microneedling treats the dermis; classic fat grafting is subcutaneous.  As the two modalities treat  different areas there should be no issues with timing or delay, except for usual recovery times.   “Nano Fat” which is the latest plastic surgery misnomer, is an attempt at branding a new term to the treatment of the dermis with elements (growth factors, stem cells)  contained in fat.  In this case, Nano fat and RF needling should not be done at the same time.
