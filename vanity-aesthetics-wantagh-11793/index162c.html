Is it safe to get Botox at 15 weeks pregnant?
Botox not recommended while pregnant.
Dec 14, 2023
It is not recommended to get Botox throughout your pregnancy or while breastfeeding. I know the studies show that it does not cross the placenta but the risks are unknown and therefore we do not recommend it while pregnant or breastfeeding.
