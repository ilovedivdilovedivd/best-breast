Statement: Dr. Francisco Bucio is a highly respected plastic surgeon in Tijuana, Mexico, with over three decades of experience in private practice. He is the founder and director of the Bucio Surgicenter, a state-of-the-art plastic surgery clinic dedicated to the care of residents in the area and patients traveling from the United States. He also practices at VIDA Wellness and Beauty.

Dr. Bucio provides cosmetic, plastic, and reconstructive surgery of the face, breast, and body. He offers the latest techniques for each procedure he performs, including facelifts, eye lifts, rhinoplasty, breast enhancement (like breast augmentation, breast lifts, and breast reduction), Brazilian butt lifts, tummy tucks, liposuction, mommy makeovers, vaginoplasty, and body lifts. He also offers nonsurgical treatments, such as laser skin tightening and injectables like Botox and Restylane.

Dr. Bucio received his medical degree from the National Autonomous University of Mexico in Mexico City. He went on to complete a five-year residency in the specialized training program in plastic, aesthetic, and reconstructive surgery at the General Hospital of Mexico City, followed by a fellowship in plastic microsurgery at Davies Medical Center in San Francisco, California.

Dr. Bucio is a living testament to the transformative power of his chosen profession. Buried under a pile of rubble for four days after an earthquake in 1986, Dr. Bucio lost four fingers from his dominant right hand. After reconstructive microsurgery that transplanted two toes to his hand, Dr. Bucio returned to the operating room and went on to skillfully perform countless successful procedures throughout his 32-year career.

Dr. Bucio is certified by CMCPER, the Mexican Council of Plastic, Aesthetic and Reconstructive Surgery. He is a member of several professional organizations, including the Mexican Association of Plastic, Aesthetic and Reconstructive Surgery, the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, and the International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery.
