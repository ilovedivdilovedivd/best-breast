Is this scarring normal?
Scarring post op
Jun 5, 2018
Hi. Thanks for your question and photo. At 5-6 weeks post-op, it is normal to have red and raised scars. It is advisable to massage and moisturise the scars. Silicone can also help however I would suggest you speak to your PS about this. You should expect your scars to improve over a period of 6-12 months. All the best!
