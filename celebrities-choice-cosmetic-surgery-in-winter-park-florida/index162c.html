Doctors

Thomas Trevisani, MD

Name: Thomas Trevisani, MD
Statement: Dr. Thomas Trevisani is a board-certified plastic surgeon in Orlando, Florida. He is an expert in closed rhinoplasty and all aspects of body contouring, including breast lifts and augmentation, neck lifts, abdominoplasty, and neck lifts. His results are exemplary and incomparable, and he is an experienced surgeon.

Dr. Trevisani started his practice, Celebrities Choice, with the idea that each of us can be a celebrity in our own life. “Celebrity status is conferred upon those of us who achieve a level of existence that matches our potential,” he says.

After graduating from the University of Nebraska School of Medicine, Dr. Trevisani went on to complete his general surgery residency at Jackson Memorial Hospital through the University of Miami School of Medicine. He then received his plastic surgery residency training at the Medical College of Wisconsin in Milwaukee. There, he trained under world-renowned plastic surgeons, honing his skills in all forms of plastic and reconstructive surgery while finding the work simultaneously stimulating, challenging, and interesting — the cornerstones of a purposeful career. Dr. Trevisani believes that every human has at least one passion, and at least one gift, and when those two find each other, “we have a life full of meaning.”

Dr. Trevisani is certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, and is a member of the American Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery.

He has been honored with a number of awards throughout his career, including as the Physician’s Recognition Award for Continuing Medical Education by the American Medical Association and the Orlando Sentinel Award for five years running. He has also been frequently named a RealSelf 100 Top Doctor, an Orlando Sentinel “Best Bets” Winner, and a Top Physician by Orlando Family Magazine.
