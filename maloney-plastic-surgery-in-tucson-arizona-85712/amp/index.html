Christopher T. Maloney Jr., MD

Name: Christopher T. Maloney Jr., MD
Statement: Dr. Christopher T. Maloney, Jr. is a Harvard-trained, board-certified plastic surgeon who returned to his hometown of Tucson, Arizona, and founded Maloney Plastic Surgery and Skin Care in 2002. Since then, he’s become a sought-after plastic surgeon in Arizona, offering surgical and nonsurgical procedures for the face, breasts, and body. Voted one of the Best Doctors in America since 2005 and a Castle Connolly Top Doctor multiple times, Dr. Maloney is known for delivering outstanding results with exceptional care. His surgical forte: beautiful, natural facelift results, which draw patients from all over the country.

Dr. Maloney received his medical degree at Columbia University (Vagelos) College of Physicians and Surgeons. He completed a residency (in general surgery) and a burn fellowship at Vanderbilt Medical Center, and did his formal plastic surgery training at Harvard in the combined plastic surgery program.

Dr. Maloney is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, and he is a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons. He also has a clinical appointment as a clinical associate professor of plastic surgery at the University of Arizona.

The safety of his patients is of utmost importance to Dr. Maloney, who operates at an AAAHC-certified freestanding surgical center that he co-founded and that uses a regular team of board-certified anesthesiologists. Staff members who assist with in-office surgery performed under local anesthesia are trained in basic life support or advanced cardiac life support.

Dr. Maloney also participates in the Maintenance of Certification (MOC) program with the American Board of Plastic Surgery, which reviews surgical experience and safety and requires ongoing written testing, competency exams, and continuing medical education.
