I have olive tone skin. Will a facelift leave me visible scars?
Facelift Scarring Is Different
Oct 16, 2020
Hi,
Everyone heals a little differently but facelift incisions are made in very precise areas that curve along (or behind) the ear. This area of the face heals better than other areas of the body. A facelift scar will be not be raised and will appear less noticeable than a scar on your stomach.
I recommend you receive a full consultation with your plastic surgeon on what kind of scars you would expect post-facelift procedure.

Is a Mid-face Lift a Problem when a Person Has Dentures?
Midface lifting
Feb 9, 2012
Midface lift with dentures.

Having dentures should not pose a problem with midface lift. In fact, having dentures long term can produce midface deficiency by gradual loss of the underlying skelton, causing shortening of the vertical height of the maxilla (the midface bone) and recession. Midface lift can be safely performed through the lower eyelid, through the temporal region or through the mouth.
