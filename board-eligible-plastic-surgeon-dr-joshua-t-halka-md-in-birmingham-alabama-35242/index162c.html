Non healing tummy tuck scab?
You likely have an area of chronic inflammation
Nov 7, 2023
When an area heals over and then closes back up only to open up again there is usually an underlying cause of chronic inflammation in the area, such as a fluid collection, non-dissolved suture, or permanent suture which is colonized with bacteria. I have seen this a couple times and the only way to deal with it after you are this far out from surgery is to explore the area and try to remove the cause of the chronic inflammation.
