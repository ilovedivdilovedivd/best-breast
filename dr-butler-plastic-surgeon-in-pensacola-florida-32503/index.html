My silicone implants feel different a few months post-op - What could it be? How can I fix it
Breast asymmetry
Dec 7, 2021
It is difficult to determine the cause of breast asymmetry without an in-person exam, however, the firmer side may be developing a capsular contracture (tightening of scar tissue surrounding implant). I recommend following up with your original surgeon if possible, or seeking out a local plastic surgeon for exam.
Significant Breast ptosis - am I a good candidate for a Lift?
Breast lift
Dec 7, 2021
You may be a great candidate for a mastopexy to lift the breasts and create more symmetry to the areolas. In addition, a patient may be a candidate for auto-augmentation or fat transfer instead of breast implants.
Can I consider a fat transfer in my temples?
fat transfer to temples
Aug 12, 2021
Yes, fat transfer to the temples is an option if you are happy with the result of filler. You may need to wait until the filler is mostly dissolved before having fat transfer so the surgeon can see the true volume deficit.
