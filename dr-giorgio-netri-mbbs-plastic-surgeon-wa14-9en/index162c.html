Why do some doctors prescribe blood thinners for a TT? I am curious to know if this is something I
blood thinner and tummy tuck
Oct 4, 2019
A tummy tuck is a long procedure (usually over two hours) and this is on its own a risk for deep vein thrombosys. I do prescribe blood thinners to all my patients before surgery to reduce this risk
Can you have a baby after a tummy tuck?
Can I have a baby after a tummy tuck?
Sep 28, 2019
There are no medical contraindications to have a baby after a tummy tuck. If somebody is planning to have a baby in the near future I would advice to delay the procedure as pregnancy can affect the result stretching the skin.
After pregnancy you may or may not need further skin excision and muscle plication.
Can they remove the fat from the abdominal area when they do a tummy tuck?
is it possible to have liposuction to the abdomen during tummy tuck?
Sep 28, 2019
Thanks for your question. There is a procedure called lipoabdominoplasty where liposuction to upper abdomen and flanks is performed at the same time of the abdominoplasty. Particular attention needs to be given to the blood supply to not incur in severe complications. I advice you to visit a board certified plastic surgeon with great experience in this procedure.
What happens with weight gain after a tummy tuck?
Weight gain after a tummy tuck
Sep 28, 2019
Weight gain and weight loss can affect the result of a tummy tuck if significant. It is still possible to put on weight after abdominoplasty and liposuction it will just distribute differently. weight gain and loss can also cause loss of skin elasticity, stretch marks and skin excess
Is losing your belly button part of the risk of a tummy tuck?
Can I loose my belly button with a tummy tuck?
Sep 28, 2019
Thank you for your question. The risk of loosing the belly button in patient with abdominoplasty is an extremely rare circumstance but can happen if the blood supply is compromised. Your plastic surgeon should list this complication during consenting process.
Will TT increase acid reflux even if the muscles aren’t tightened?
tummy tuck and acid reflux
Sep 28, 2019
Thank you for your interesting question. A tummy tuck procedure with muscle tightening will not improve or worsen your hernia or reflux so it can be performed safely in every patient with your issues.
