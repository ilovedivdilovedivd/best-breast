“I recognize that most clients who want to have their bodies sculpted or re-proportioned want it done by the best lipo doctor available, who specializes in that particular procedure. That’s why cosmetic liposculpture is what we focus on,” says Dr. Hall.

Dr. Hall is known as a liposuction expert in the Southwestern United States. He has dedicated his education and practice to perfecting his body sculpting technique, and is constantly engaged in ongoing education to learn the latest advances in the field of body contouring.

Does Renuvion just tightens skin and/or actually removes excess skin long term?
Renuvion
Aug 1, 2019
Hi tabby 79,

Renuvion J Plasma is solely a skin tightening treatment, it does not remove excess skin. The only way to remove excess skin is to surgically remove it by cutting it off, hence doing some sort of a lift or tuck. Yes, Renuvion J Plasma can be done multiple times in the same area until you have achieved your desired result. The treatments should be spaced nine to twelve months apart to allow for full healing and retraction of the skin
