Why do the back of my teeth feel so grainy after Veneers?
Most likely residue from bonding cement
Sep 7, 2023
Most likely there is residue from the bonding cement. The back of your tooth should feel the same as prior to treatment. There will be a difference, however, in how the smooth porcelain would feel vs natural tooth structure. But, to me this sounds like you should return to the dentist to evaluate and to see if there is bonding residue present that can easily be removed and polished.
