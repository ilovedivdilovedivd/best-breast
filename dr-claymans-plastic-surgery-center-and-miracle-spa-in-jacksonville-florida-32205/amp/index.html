Dr. Clayman's Plastic Surgery Center and Miracle Spa

Doctors

Mark Clayman, MD

Name: Mark Clayman, MD
Statement: Dr. Mark Clayman is a board-certified plastic surgeon in Jacksonville, Florida, with over 12 years in private practice. He performs both cosmetic and reconstructive surgeries of the face and body, including facelift, rhinoplasty, blepharoplasty (eyelid lift), mommy makeover, tummy tuck, liposuction, breast augmentation, labiaplasty, and male breast reduction (gynecomastia surgery).

His meticulous attention to detail, sharp surgical skills, natural-looking results, and commitment to patient care have made him one of the most sought-after plastic surgeons in Northeast Florida.

At Dr. Clayman’s Plastic Surgery Center & Miracle Spa—the practice he shares with his father, Dr. Loren Clayman—he and his staff develop tailored treatment plans, designed to meet their patients’ aesthetic goals in the safest way.

Dr. Clayman was the first plastic surgeon in Northeast Florida to open a medical spa, where patients can enjoy a wide range of minimally invasive cosmetic procedures. His injectable offerings include the wrinkle treatments Botox and Xeomin, dermal fillers like Juvederm, Restylane, Belotero, and Radiesse. He also provides various skin resurfacing treatments, such as chemical peels, microdermabrasion, and facials. For body contouring and fat reduction, Dr. Clayman offers CoolSculpting, or Kybella double chin treatment.

One of his signature nonsurgical treatments, the “No Sweat Special,” benefits those dealing with the medical condition hyperhidrosis, also known as excessive sweating. The treatment uses Botox to block the nerves that allow the sweat glands to produce excess perspiration.

After graduating from Harvard University with a biology degree, Dr. Clayman earned his medical degree at Jefferson Medical College in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. There, he was recognized as the top medical student by the Department of Surgery faculty.

Dr. Clayman completed his general surgery residency at the Shands Hospital at the University of Florida and his subsequent plastic surgery residency training at the North Florida Veterans Association—both in Gainesville, Florida. He became the most published plastic surgery fellow in the history of the university’s fellowship program.

Dr. Clayman is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. An active member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, he lectures at plastic surgery conferences both in the U.S. and abroad. He has authored many peer-reviewed medical research publications over the years.

Dr. Clayman has also been the recipient of many awards, including Best Presentation, an honor given to him twice by the American Society for Aesthetic Surgery. He was also voted number one in the 904 by Void Magazine multiple years in a row, as well as Bold City’s Best Plastic Surgery Practice and Best of Jax’s Best Plastic Surgeon. Dr. Clayman has also been recognized as a Top 10 Doctor in Northeast Florida by Vitals and received both the Patient Satisfaction Award and Compassionate Doctor Award numerous times.
