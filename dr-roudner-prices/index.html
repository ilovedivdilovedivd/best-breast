Natural look after breast augmentation in a thin patient.

I would recommend a small implant placed under the muscle. This should provide a very natural look. At times I perform fat injections to the breast to give extra volume between the skin and the implant. Although you are thin, we can always obtain a good amount of fat for this.

In my practice I perform breast augmentation with saline implants under the muscle mostly and my patients are very happy. Other doctors prefer silicone implants and I am sure that their patients are happy too.

You should choose a doctor because of the results and not the type of implants. The cost of saline implants is 1/3 of the silicone implants, so that can be a factor too in your decision.

As far as the size, it depends on your measurement, whether you are a solid A or small A. With a 375cc implants, your end result can range from small 34 C to full 34 C. Consult with an experienced board certified plastic surgeon and check the before and after pictures to make sure that you like the results.

Above or Below The Muscle ?

It would appear that you are slim and would have thin tissue above an implant. This would make the implant more visible and susceptible to rippling if placed on top of the muscle.

Here are some of the benefits of under the muscle, the difference between saline vs silicone, and textured vs smooth implants. Less visibility of the implant and rippling under the muscle. Silicone will be less visible (more natural looking) more natural feeling and less rippling vs a saline implant.
Lower capsular contraction rate under the muscle. This is well documented in the literature. Better visualization of breast tissue on mammograms when placed under the muscle. Textured implants tend to adhere to the surrounding tissue when placed on top of the muscle and cause more rippling.

Using textured implants under the muscle in primary breast augmentation is used less commonly than smooth implants. However, in secondary revisions sometimes a textured implant may be more beneficial. This is just a summary. Please consult with an experienced board certified plastic surgeon for a more in depth discussion of breast augmentation.

500 cc implants

Whether or not a 500 cc implant is right for you will depend upon many factors. Base diameter and skin stretch are just two components. An exam in person is key.
Implant options

We have seen a recent wave of new implants come to market since silicone was declared safe by the FDA. Standard rounds smooth implants have been the mainstay of practices and will continue to be because of the quality of the devices and the desirable results they produce.

Allergan, Mentor and Sientra are the three manufacturers allowed to sell their devices in the US. The form stable implants have just been introduced to the American market but have been available to the rest of the world for many years.

The implants are firmer than the rounds to maintain their shape and they can not move in the space whereas we massage round implants to foster movement. This leads to a slightly firmer breast over time.

The design of the form stable implants also yield less upper pole fullness of the breast and so the aesthetics are slightly different. Seek out a board certified plastic surgeon with significant breast experience to help you with this important decision.

Breast augmentation after breast feeding

Once you’ve stopped producing milk, and your breast has reached a stable situation where it no longer seems to be changing, you are ready for your augmentation. If the breast you used to breast feed is still engorged and firm, or if it seems like it hasn’t shrunk down fully yet, then you should wait a little longer.

Breast lift or not with augmentation

First, let’s dispense with the myth that implants above the muscle lift the nipple. They don’t and they risk looking like a ball in sock later. So go under the muscle especially for better mammograms.

Second, you can always do the implants and defer the lift for 6 months until you are convinced that you need it.
Round implants do create natural upper poles

There are many variable involved such as native breast tissue, size and profile of the implant, and chest wall dimensions. Shaped implants do not guarantee a more natural look.

In the end, there is nothing natural about implants so you shouldn’t expect a natural result, but if you achieve one, then great.

Is there a price difference between Saline and Silicone Implants?

Yes, saline implants are definitely less expensive than silicone gel implants. Just how much less depends on what pricing the company is offering to the surgeon (which may depend on how much business the surgeon is doing with that company or if the company is trying to attract that surgeon’s business and offers them discounted pricing), and whether the surgeon is passing all or some of that cost savings on to you.
Also be aware of the pros and cons between saline and gel implants as you make your choice. I see many patients who later switch their saline implants for gels due to implant wrinkling and edges being visible or palpable, but it is rare for someone to go from gels to saline.

Life span of breast implants

Old implants do not necessarily have to be changed especially if you are not having any problems such as hard and uncomfortable capsule formation or breast deformity. However, if a routine mammmogram or MRI demonstrates any significant anomalies, then a consultation would be required to ascertain the nature of the problem and the need for implant removal or replacement.

At 4 weeks, it’s a bit late for settling breast implants

At 4 weeks, the real question is will anything other than more surgery work to get things to settle. I would try what your surgeon suggests but start up the discussion of what you will do if this doesn’t work and how long you are going to wait.

It is a matter of degree sometimes and if things are close more pressure garment type things may help. If they are “way off,” you are probably looking at more surgery.

Minimizing rippling after breast augmentation

The best you can do is to go under the muscle with gel implants that are of the firmer variety ie Inspira by Allergan. If you try to overfill salines, they will be hard and unappealing.

If you are super bony thin and have very little breast tissue, you will have some rippling almost no matter what you do. As long as it is limited to the lower and outer regions it is usually acceptable for what you get positively from breast implants.
So, rippling depends on what implant is used plus under the muscle plus your body’s starting point.

Breast work

Much of this answer depends upon whether or not you are happy with the current volume.

When you lift your breasts into a better position, such as in a bra, or you happy with her breast volume? If so, you can get away with just a lift.

If you would like to be larger, you would need a lift and an augmentation, though I would not recommend adding too much volume. If you would like to be a smaller volume, then you would need a reduction, which includes a lift.

So, as you see, this all depends upon your satisfaction with your current volume.

Huge Implants

Without before and after pictures, it is hard to give specific advice. However, you are immediately post op and you should expect to see changes over the next few months. As the tissues settle and the swelling resolves and you get used to your implants, you may find that they are a good size.

If not, you should wait several months before considering any type of revision surgery.

Breast implant secondary surgery

Usually, secondary breast implant surgery is not as painful as the first surgery. However, in your case where the implants were removed, it may hurt a bit more than say a person who had an exchange.
