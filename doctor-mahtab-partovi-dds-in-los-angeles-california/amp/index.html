I’m wondering if braces or jaw surgery would result in better facial balance than rhinoplasty with
Jaw Surgery
Dec 21, 2016
Great Question!

Jaw surgery, also know as Orthognathic Surgery, is the most stable treatment for jaws that are further back (class II).

Chin implants can resorb (eat away at) the bone behind where the implant is placed, and over time the chin gets small again.

Orthognathic surgery (in conjunction with braces) will help give you better facial harmony, as well as a balanced bite, and the ability to close your mouth at rest.
