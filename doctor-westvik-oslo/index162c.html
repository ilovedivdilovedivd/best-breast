I want to get an upper eyelid surgery. Should I do an internal browpexy as well?
Brow pexy vs Brow Llift
Oct 10, 2020
Brow pexy is not a good solution for lifting your brow which is low due to poor nerve function. Based on this, you will always have a difference in position and animation of the two brows, but a better way to correct the asymmetry is by performing an endoscopic brow lift. In this way your eyebrow will be lifted to a more favorable position, and the result is more long lasting. This can easily be combined with an upper blepharoplasty to address the excess skin on your upper eyelids.
