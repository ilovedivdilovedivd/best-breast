How do I correct staining at the edges of my veneers?
polishing veneers
Nov 20, 2015
Hi, polishing the veneers with super fine burs and use of rubber wheels with polishing paste will typically do the work in removing any surface stains and bring back the luster to porcelain. If you’ re continually getting stains, it is going to be either because polishing is not being done properly or you have stains that are deeper and require replacement of the veneer.
