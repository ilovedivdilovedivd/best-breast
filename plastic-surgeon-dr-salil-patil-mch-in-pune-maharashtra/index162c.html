Butt implants or BBL: which one requires more downtime?
Butt augmentation
Dec 21, 2018
Good morning.
Butt augmentation either with implants or BBL is safe. BBL is a more natural method and has the advantage of reducing fat in other areas as a part of the procedure.The only down side is if you want a significant large increase in size, you may have to consider a repeat procedure after a few months. With implants, a single surgery will give you what is desired.
Get your treatment done from a qualified plastic surgeon and you should have no reason to worry.
Thank you
Dr Salil Patil
