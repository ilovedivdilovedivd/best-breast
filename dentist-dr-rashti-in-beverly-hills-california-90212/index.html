Implants same day as extractions and bone graft doomed to fail?
Same day implant placement.
May 1, 2018
Hi;

Same day implant placement is a very common way to replace missing teeth so long that your surgeon feels confident about the primary retention. If your surgeon can get a good amount of remaining bone to place the implant and graft around it, the success rate of same day implant placement is very high.

Good luck,
Dr. Mahnaz Rashti.

My upper right lateral incisor is missing. I currently have a Maryland (sic) bridge and want an impl
Missing lateral incisor
Apr 30, 2018
Hi,

The best option would be orthodontically moving your central incisor and create room for placement of a dental implant in the position of your missing tooth. With orthodontic treatment the entire tooth moves bodily that means the root of your tooth would move as well to create room for your implant. I also do not recommend extraction of a canine. It is better to keep your canine.

Best of Luck;
Dr. Mahnaz Rashti.

Is dental crown on front teeth supposed to look high from the gum line and too bulky?
Front tooth Dental Crowns
Apr 30, 2018
Hi;
When your dentist prepares your tooth for the crown or veneer, if he removes just enough of your tooth structure to make a crown, then your crown should not be bulky at all.

Best of luck.

Dr. Mahnaz Rashti
