At your age and after having 3 kids you will most likely need a tummy tuck with the BBL for the best result. I would only go to a board certified plastic surgeon and RealSelf is great with telling you what type of doctor you are looking at by telling you what they are board certified in if you have any uncertainty.

Interested in a BBL with liposuction. Will I need a tummy tuck first?
Sorta disagree but everyone is different
Feb 28, 2023
Personally I would do the liposuction and the fat transfer FIRST and then do the extended tummy tuck second; I think doing the liposuction first allows you to take more skin out at the second surgery because the bulk is not there. That being said, every surgeon has a different vision and a path to get there with you.
