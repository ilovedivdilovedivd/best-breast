Would I be a good candidate for a TT? Hoping to not have any visible stretch mark
TT for stretch marks.
Sep 24, 2019
I think you are likely to do well following a TT procedure with liposuction to the flanks and upper abdomen. TT surgery will typically remove all the stretch marks below the umbilicus although occasionally even stretch marks above the umbilicus can be removed, dependant on skin laxity. The position of the scar will also depend on the level of skin laxity. Your PS will assess this at the time of your consultation.
Good luck!
Cameron
Do I need a tummy tuck?
TT or lipo?
Sep 24, 2019
From the photograph it looks like liposuction alone may result in further laxity of the lower abdominal skin. Even though you are looking to limit the down time I think you would get a much better result with a TT procedure in combination with liposuction to the flanks and upper abdomen. The lower back may also be treated at the same time as part of a circumferential approach should you wish.
Good luck!
Cameron
Mom of 2: Tummy tuck or lipo or both?
TT or lipo?
Sep 24, 2019
With a history of two C-sections I think you will achieve the best result with a full TT and muscle repair combined with liposuction to the flanks and upper abdomen. The TT scar will remove the C-section scar. Circumferential liposuction would allow recontouring of the lower back.
Good luck!
Cameron

Can Diastasis recti be repaired as part of a mommy makeover? If so, is a regular
Muscle repair during TT
Sep 19, 2019
Muscle repair is often undertaken at the time of TT surgery. It is not always possible to achieve the same figure as prior to pregnancy but a noticeable improvement is expected. An extended TT is typically offered to patients who have skin laxity extending around their sides.
