How long do I have to wear Invisalign every day?
You can take a well deserved vacation, your retainers should not.
May 11, 2016
Hi JBSeattle,You should wear your aligners for 22 hours a day as instructed. If you take a break for a few days, the teeth start to relapse back to their previous position, risking the aligners starting to slip and not engage. It is not worth the risk as it may extend the time you need to be in active treatment significantly, and depending on your Invisalign plan, it may incur extra costs.It will be worth it in the end.
