Areolas folded 4 years after gynecomastia surgery. Possible to revise?
Nipple depression after gynecomastia excision
Apr 30, 2020
Excising all breast tissue under the nipple will often result in a depression. It the depression can be grabbed and pulled upward indicating it's not tethered to scar tissue. If so fat injection or filler can be injected to fill the depression. If the depression is fixed then using the existing incision to release the scar will then allow fat injection to work better.

Liposuction and gynecomastia
Apr 30, 2020
Gynecomastia implies breast gland is excess not just surrounding or associated excess fat. Breast gland is firm and rubbery and can not be suctioned. This breast gland tissue is easily removed with a small incision at the lower half of the nipple. This can often be done under local anesthesia.
