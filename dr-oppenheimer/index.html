Can someone with lichen sclerosus on clitoris have labiaplasty?
Consultation Needed
Aug 7, 2023
It’s possible to have a labiaplasty with pre-existing skin and inflammatory conditions of the vulva. Depending on the severity of the lichen sclerosus, a Labiaplasty may or may not be possible.
