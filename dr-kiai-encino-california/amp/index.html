Can i still wear braces even if my teeth have fillings?
Fillings vs. Veneers
Apr 21, 2016
The way veneers look is much more aesthetic than filling on front teeth.  White fillings look for mat and veneers are more shiny which resemble tooth structure.  The color on fillings will change over time and it will be obvious that you have something on your teeth.  To address your braces question, if you’re getting orthodontic work done, you need to hold off on the venners until you’re done.
My two front crowns keep break. Bad product?
Front Crown
Apr 21, 2016
There are a few reasons why a crown would keep coming off.  One is if there’s not enough tooth structure underneath to retain the crown in place, another is the type of cement used.  One more contributing factor is your bite.  If you have a deep bite anf keep biting hard into the crown then the crown will come off.  You need to have this re-evaluated by your dentist and look at other options.
