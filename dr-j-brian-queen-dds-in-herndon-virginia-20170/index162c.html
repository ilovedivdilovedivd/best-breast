Failed Root Canal with Chronic Abscess. Re-treatment or Abstraction and Implant?
Extraction and Implant
Aug 12, 2019
Maxillary First molars statistically have the highest failure rate after endondontics of any tooth in the mouth. If a conventional root canal fails on the Mx first molar; I counsel my patients to extract the tooth, graft bone in the socket and then proceed with implant and restoration after 3-4 months. Time is critical after the extraction as bone that is not stimulated will atrophy and possibly require additional procedures to buttress bone prior to implant placement.
