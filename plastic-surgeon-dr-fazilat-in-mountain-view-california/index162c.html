Statement: Dr. Shahin Fazilat is a board-certified cosmetic and reconstructive plastic surgeon and founder of SF Bay Area Plastic Surgery, with offices in Mountain View, Los Gatos and Morgan Hill, California. He has expertise in a wide variety of procedures, but Dr. Fazilat specializes in breast enhancement, tummy tucks, and complete mommy makeovers, as well as facial procedures like rhinoplasty, eyelid lifts, and lip lifts. He also offers popular nonsurgical facial rejuvenation treatments like Botox, dermal fillers, laser resurfacing, Microneedling, and vaginal rejuvenation, as well as body contouring treatments like BodyTite, Facetite, Morpheus8, CoolSculpting, and CoolTone.

Dr. Fazilat received his Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Maryland and went on to earn his medical degree at Howard University School of Medicine. He completed two residencies, one in general surgery at The Washington Hospital Center, and another in plastic surgery at Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. Can I have chin filler two days after chin liposuction?
Chin filler after liposuction...
May 17, 2023
Hello! It is not recommended to add filler to the chin a couple days AFTER chin liposuction. Liposuction under the chin will cause a decent amount of swelling, making you look fuller than before the actual surgery. This will throw off how you truly look and what the final outcome will be. It is best to wait a full 4-6 weeks minimum prior to adding chin filler and chin liposuction. Once cleared by your surgeon, you should be good to go!
