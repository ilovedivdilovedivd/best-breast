Can I get a BBL or is my BMI too high?
BBL in high BMI
Mar 5, 2023
I often advice against having BBL in BMI more than 35 as the risks of surgery are much higher (chest infection, DVT/PE, fat necrosis and delayed healing). Also you don't get the best outcome from surgery due to the remaining internal visceral fat tissue. Generally speaking the best BMI to have this surgery is between 22-25 these are not 100% accurate as people tend to store the weight in different parts of the body. Do i require a full / mini tummy tuck or lipo?
Liposuction or tummy tuck
Apr 2, 2022
Hi, From the pictures seems that you have fullness as well as skin laxity in the area. Liposuction will reduce the fulness (podge) but will not tighten the skin. A mini tummy tuck can tighten the skin on the lower abdomen only. If you have skin laxity above the belly button, then that would require a full tummy tuck.
