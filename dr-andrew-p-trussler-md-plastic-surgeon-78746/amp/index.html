Can I benefit from a breast lift and implants?
Breast Lift is needed with or without implants
Jul 23, 2020
A breast lift or mastopexy repositions the nipple to a higher position and tighten the breast. The nipple is well below the equator of the breast and a lift is in order. A lift with an auto-augmentation could be a great procedure to balance and shape the breast. If additional volume is needed, a low profile implant to meld with the rounded chest combined with a lift could be ok though younger patients will have more dense tissue which is more difficult to position on the implant.

Apr 28, 2020
It looks like your nipple is set fairly low with large diameter areola and moderate sagging. A periareolar or around the nipple lift with an implant would be enough to lift the nipple and centralize it on the breast after a breast implant.

Tummy Tuck For Core Muscle Repair and Alignment
Apr 27, 2020
A Tummy Tuck or abdominoplasty does remove and tighten abdominal skin, removes fat and aligns the rectus muscle which typically with separate with pregnancy, surgery including spine surgery and weight loss. This is referred to a Diastasis Recti. There are different types of tummy tucks which differ in the degree of skin removal, but the diastasis can be repaired with all types and there are some studies and experience that the diastasis repair can help strengthen the core and relieve back pain.
