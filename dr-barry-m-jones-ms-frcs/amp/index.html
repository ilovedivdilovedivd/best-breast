Facelift Recovery
Facelift recovery
Dec 15, 2015
When this type of surgery is carried out carefully, recovery return to daily life is in the region of two weeks. It is wise to leave a week to relax and do very little and a second week to take life relatively slowly.
Is cheek liposuction dangerous???
Is cheek liposuction dangerous?
Dec 3, 2015
The short answer is potentially yes. Buccal fat pad removal which is done from inside the mouth, has the potential to damage the facial nerve as does superficial liposuction in the cheek area. The facial nerve works all the expressive muscles in your face. As others have said it would be unusual for liposuction of subcutaneous fat above the jawline to be a good choice.
I had a facelift with fat transfer a week ago. Can I still ice my face?
Can I still ice my face after facelift
Nov 19, 2015
It is not a good idea to use ice on the face after surgery. It is too cold and can cause serious damage via frost bite. It is better to use damp cloths cooled in the fridge (4 degrees) not the freezer.
When wiil I get feeling in my face?
Recovery of sensation following facelift
Nov 17, 2015
In most people sensitivity will recover in the cheeks over 4-6 months. In some it may be slower and recovery can continue for as long as 2 years.
