Statement: Dr. Francis Johns is a board-certified plastic surgeon with over two decades of experience, practicing in Greensburg, Pennsylvania. Known for his surgical expertise in plastic and reconstructive procedures involving the face, breasts, and body, Dr. Johns specializes in breast enhancement surgery, tummy tucks, Brazilian butt lifts, mommy makeovers, and face and neck lifts.

At his private practice, Artisan Plastic Surgery, Dr. Johns also performs nonsurgical procedures such as laser skin resurfacing, dermal fillers, Botox, microdermabrasion, chemical peels, and spider vein treatment. He and his staff ensure that safety and comfort are prioritized for each patient at their state-of-the-art facility.

With degrees in both medicine and dentistry and years of specialized training in both maxillofacial and plastic surgery, Dr. Johns has built his 20-year career with unique surgical skills and innumerable hours of experience delivering stellar patient results—one reason why he enthusiastically invites potential patients to peruse his reviews and photos on RealSelf.

“I think that people need to look at before-and-after photos, look at the number of reviews a surgeon has and what patients are saying,” says Dr. Johns, who enjoys a near-perfect patient rating on RealSelf.

After graduating Summa Cum Laude from the University of Pittsburgh and earning his doctorate in dental medicine from the University of Pittsburgh School of Dental Medicine, Dr. Johns went on to complete his medical degree at University of Pittsburgh School of Medicine. This was followed by an oral and maxillofacial surgery residency at the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center and a plastic surgery residency at the University of Virginia Medical Center. During this time, Dr. Johns placed first in the Virginia American College of Surgeons Research Competition for his work in breast reconstruction.

Dr. Johns is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, the American College of Surgeons, and the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery.
