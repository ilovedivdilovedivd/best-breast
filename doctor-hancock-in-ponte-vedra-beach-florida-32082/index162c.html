23, Chinese, 5’4, 125lbs, am I a good candidate for buccal fat removal?
Buccal fat removal in Asians
Jan 20, 2023
This is a common complaint in the Asian population: Your chubby cheeks would be corrected more with buccal fat removal than chin liposuction. Chin liposuction addresses excess fat than you can pinch underneath your chin, right above your neck. It does not address excess chubby cheeks. The buccal fat removal would lessen your chubby cheeks just lateral to your smile. It does not remove from the cheek above your smile.
