Statement: Dr. Navid Jallali is a specialist registered consultant cosmetic and reconstructive surgeon, practicing in Central London. One of the UK’s plastic surgeons, he specialises in breast augmentation, reduction, and reconstruction, as well as surgical body contouring procedures like liposuction and abdominoplasty. He also has expertise in and facial plastic surgery, including facelifts, eyelid lifts (blepharoplasty), and rhinoplasty.

“There is a big overlap between aesthetic and reconstructive surgery,” he says. “There is often a functional benefit from most of the aesthetic procedures that I carry out.” One of the most rewarding aspects of this work is how his surgery makes his patients feel better about themselves. In many cases, the results can be life-changing.

At his boutique clinic in the West End, Dr. Jallali and his highly experienced team are known for being compassionate, honest, and committed to giving their patients the best, most comfortable experience possible.

“We aim to provide the highest level of care, from the initial inquiry to the last follow-up,” he says. “I have a dedicated team, including anesthetists, and have carried out thousands of successful procedures together.” Dr. Jallali prides himself on consistently providing natural-looking, beautiful results that often exceed his patients’ expectations.

Dr. Jallali obtained his medical degree with honors from Bristol University, followed by advanced training at distinguished hospitals and clinics in the UK, Australia, and Los Angeles. He pioneered tissue engineering research in partnership with the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in Cambridge, Massachusetts.

Today, he is a Fellow of the Royal College of Surgeons (FRCS) with a specialty designation in plastic surgery, and is on the General Medical Council’s specialist register.

Dr. Jallali’s expertise has earned him highly prestigious honors in London’s medical community. This includes being named the president of the Royal Society of Medicine, Plastic Surgery, in 2018, Chairman of the Medical Advisory Committee at London’s Welbeck Hospital, and being elected the lead clinician in plastic and reconstructive surgery from 2014 to 2017 at Imperial College.

Dr. Jallali is also often called upon as a resource on plastic surgery for various media, including being the plastic surgery advisor to Holby City on BBC.

When not practicing medicine, Dr. Jallali shares his knowledge in a variety of ways, including by training the future generation of doctors at Charing Cross Hospital, where he is the leading surgical tutor for the well-renowned microsurgical training department. He also leads the Imperial College FRCS plastic surgery revision course and is a frequent guest speaker for undergraduate and graduate students.

Dr. Jallali is passionate about academia and research, and has authored over 40 articles published in peer-reviewed journals, including the British Medical Journal.
