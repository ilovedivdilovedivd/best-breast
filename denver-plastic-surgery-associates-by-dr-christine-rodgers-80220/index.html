HI,
Typically, a trim techniques is a better way to reduce or eliminate the dark area at the end of the labia.
With a wedge, the surgeon is removing more tissue from below the very ends of the labia.
With the trim techniques, the surgeon is removing the dark area at the very ends of the labia.
Discuss these issues with your surgeon so that she/he understand what are your main goals with the
surgery.
