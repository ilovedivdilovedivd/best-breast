Statement: Dr. Shahram Salemy is a board-certified plastic surgeon in Seattle, Washington, who is best known for his expertise in breast augmentation and tummy tucks.

“My approach is to listen to what the patient wants, give them an honest assessment, and tailor a plan to blend the patient’s goals with what their anatomy will allow me to do safely and effectively,” says Dr. Salemy.

At his Seattle practice, Shahram Salemy Plastic Surgery, Dr. Salemy and his staff cultivate a safe, private, and comfortable atmosphere. Facilities include their operating room, “which offers patients tremendous convenience and cost savings,” Dr. Salemy says.

Patients have an outstanding experience from consultation to the final follow-up—with consistent results. But what really sets Dr. Salemy’s practice apart is the quality of his entire staff. “There are a lot of good plastic surgeons in our area, but I think I have the best staff in the business.”

A graduate of Yale University with a B.S. in Molecular Biophysics and Biochemistry, Dr. Salemy received his MD from Yale School of Medicine. He was drawn to aesthetic medicine by both the artistry and the impact it makes on individuals. “I enjoyed the procedures and having the ability to improve people’s lives,” Dr. Salemy says. He completed his residency at University of Washington, where he is now an Assistant Clinical Professor in the Department of Surgery, Division of Plastic Surgery.

Dr. Salemy is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS), and a fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS).

Over the course of his career, Dr. Salemy has been honored with several awards, including being named as one of Washington’s 10 Best Plastic Surgeons by the American Institute of Plastic Surgeons (AIOPS) and winning the Seattle Magazine Reader’s Choice Award for Best Plastic Surgeon.
