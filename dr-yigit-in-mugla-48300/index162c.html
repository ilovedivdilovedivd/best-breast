Statement: Dr. Barış Yiğit is a plastic surgeon with over 17 years of experience based in Fethiye, Muğla, Turkey. He prides himself on his body contouring expertise, for which he is internationally renowned. He performs a broad range of procedures on the face, breast, and body, ranging from mommy makeovers, VASER liposuction, and neck lifts to noninvasive treatments like injectable fillers, laser skin rejuvenation, and thread lifts.

Dr. Yiğit received his medical degree from the Ege University Faculty of Medicine in İzmir, Turkey, and completed his residency at the Istanbul Medical Faculty (Çapa) at Istanbul University, where he was named the top student in his microsurgery course. After completing his education in 2010, Dr. Yiğit fulfilled his national duty of service at Mardin State Hospital in Artuklu, Mardin, Turkey.

Passionate about education, Dr. Yiğit spent several years training future doctors at Düzce University Faculty of Medicine in the Aesthetic, Plastic, and Reconstructive Surgery department, starting as an assistant professor in 2013 and serving as the chief of the department just one year later.

In 2019, Dr. Yiğit opened his beachfront clinic in Fethiye, Muğla, Turkey. While he sees many local patients, most of his clientele travels from abroad, primarily the United Kingdom. Many patients fly to his clinic from around the world for his body contouring procedures, including full mommy makeovers, HD liposuction, and 360-degree lower body lifts.

Dr. Yiğit is a frequent guest lecturer, both at home and abroad, and has published numerous studies on topics related to plastic surgery, microsurgery, and reconstructive surgery in scientific journals worldwide.

He is an active member of the Turkish Reconstructive Microsurgical Society; the Turkish Society of Plastic, Reconstructive, and Aesthetic Surgery; and the International Society of Lymphology.

Dr. Yiğit wholeheartedly welcomes medical tourism patients, so his clinic offers virtual consultations and surgical packages with local accommodations. He points out that visiting his seaside practice for a procedure could be the perfect, unique vacation opportunity.
