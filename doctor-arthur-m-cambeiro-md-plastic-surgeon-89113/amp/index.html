Statement: Dr. Arthur M. Cambeiro is a double board-certified, Mayo Clinic-trained plastic surgeon and the founder of SurgiSpa Cosmetic and Plastic Surgery, with locations in Las Vegas and Henderson, Nevada.

Dr. Cambeiro specializes in breast enhancement, tummy tucks, liposuction, and complete mommy makeovers. He also has expertise in facial rejuvenation procedures, including facelifts, brow lifts, rhinoplasty, eyelid surgery.

He and his team of experts also offer non-invasive aesthetic procedures such as Botox and Dysport to smooth wrinkles; Kybella double chin treatment; dermal fillers such as Restylane, Juvéderm, and Radiesse; and body-sculpting treatments like CoolSculpting and SmartLipo, along with Cellulaze cellulite treatment.

Dr. Camberio’s SurgiSpa locations are also state-of-the-art skin rejuvenation and laser centers, offering an array of energy treatments including Ultherapy, Profound RF microneedling, laser resurfacing with ThermiSmooth, IPL photofacials, dermabrasion, chemical peels, laser hair removal, and more.

After graduating from the psychobiology (now neuroscience) honors program at University of Southern California, Dr. Camberio went on to obtain his medical degree from the University of Colorado. He then underwent general and vascular/trauma surgery residency training at the Phoenix Integrated Surgery Residency.

For his cosmetic, plastic, and reconstructive surgery residency, Dr. Gamberio trained at the world-renowned Mayo Clinic in Rochester, Minnesota. While he was there, he worked with Global Health Outreach, performing volunteer corrective surgery to help Vietnamese children with congenital facial defects.

Dr. Cambeiro’s background in psychology, combined with his extensive surgical experience and technical skills, gives him a unique approach when it comes to understanding his patients’ goals.

He is board-certified by both the American Board of Surgery and the American Board of Plastic Surgery, and is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.

Along with being featured as an expert in publications such as Allure and Newsweek, Dr. Cambeiro has been honored with numerous awards throughout his career. He has been named a Castle Connolly America’s Top Doctor for seven consecutive years, a Top Doc by Las Vegas Life magazine, a Top Plastic Surgeon by the Consumers’ Research Council of America, one of Nevada’s Favorite Physicians, and has received multiple Vitals Patients’ Choice awards.

He was also appointed to the National Advisory Council for Leaders in Breast Aesthetics, a prestigious group of plastic surgeons who are at the forefront of cosmetic breast surgery.
