I have a crossbite and spaces between my top teeth. What are my options?
Opening of the bite
Jul 15, 2018
Orthodontic treatment should be the primary choice of treatment, and if done well, it should not negatively affect your periodontal disease. However, if braces are absolutely not an option, then this can be fixed rather quickly by opening the bite. In your case, this means that a smile and bite design is done where your lower teeth are shortened a bit and your upper teeth are lengthened. You would need a minimum of 20 veneers (10 upper/10 lower) to acheive this.
