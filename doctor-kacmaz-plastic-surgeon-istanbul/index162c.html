Statement: Dr. Caner Kaçmaz is a plastic surgeon in Istanbul, Turkey. Guided by over 14 years of experience, he offers a range of plastic surgery and cosmetic procedures, including all kinds of breast enhancement surgery, tummy tucks, Brazilian butt lifts, complete mommy makeovers, rhinoplasty, and hair transplant surgery.

Recognized by WhatClinic with the 2019 Patient Service Award for his excellence in patient experience, Dr. Kaçmaz prides himself on providing natural results, high-quality care, and all-inclusive service without fail.

Patients from around the world travel to Istanbul to seek treatment at the Dr. Caner Kaçmaz Clinic. There, all medical procedures are performed by Dr. Kaçmaz himself in his top-grade, exclusive clinic or in first-class hospitals working at world standards with ISO, TUV, and JCI accreditations.

For those coming from abroad, transportation and accommodation services are provided in line with the patients’ requirements. Dr. Kaçmaz speaks excellent English and welcomes virtual consultations, to make the decision process easy for his patients who travel. His expert team will be at your service for your safety and comfort before and after the operation, by providing support in English, Turkish, German, French, Spanish and Arabic languages.

Born in Iskenderun, Dr. Kaçmaz received his medical degree from Ondokuz Mayıs University in Samsun, Turkey. After working as a general practitioner for one year due to Turkey’s state service requirement, he completed his advanced training in plastic, reconstructive and aesthetic surgery at Lütfi Kırdar Kartal Training and Research Hospital in Istanbul. There, he studied a number of microsurgical, reconstructive, and aesthetic procedures.

Throughout his career, Dr. Kacmaz has remained committed to learning innovative advancements in his field and traveled the world in order to deliver the latest treatments to his patients.

In Florence, Italy he trained at Dr. Paolo Bonan’s renowned Villa Donatello Clinic and received certificates in facial rejuvenation, skin treatment, vaginal rejuvenation, and laser lipolysis. He also received training in advanced body shaping, breast aesthetics, and post-bariatric surgery from Dr. Ozan Sozer, a well-known plastic surgeon from El Paso Cosmetic Surgery Clinic in Texas.

Dr. Kacmaz is a member of the Turkish Plastic, Reconstructive, and Aesthetic Surgery Association and the Turkish Medical Association and the International Society of Plastic Surgery (ISAPS). He was awarded Best Plastic Surgeon-Istanbul in 2018, as well as the 2019 Patient Service Awards’ by WhatClinic.

Is taking a couple of drags of a cigarette 11 days before septorhinoplasty a cause for concern?
Effects of smoking before and after the surgery
Nov 30, 2023
That is not a reason to cancel your surgery but we do recommend patients to quit smoking at least 4 weeks before their surgeries. Smoking significantly delays the healing process. Therefore we do not want our patients to smake after their surgeries as well. Couple of drags will not hurt you but please do not smoke anymore until your surgery day. Good luck
Uneven and bumps 2 years after rhino
Hard bump forming after Rhinoplasty surgery.
Nov 30, 2023
Thank you for your question. Swelling would be completely gone and the look should be finalized after 3 years. In some cases, bone reaction occurs and sticks out on one side. That is a very easy fix. Your surgeon will be able to file the bone that is sticking out with local anesthesia at his clinic. It is a simple, short and painless procedure. Best of luck to you.
Could driving over a bumpy road ruin or effect rhinoplasty results?
Rhinoplasty and driving
Nov 30, 2023
Driving on a bumpy road will not effect the results of your rhinoplasty surgery at all. Only thing we recommend is to protect your nose and avoid contact sports and direct hit or bump to your nose. Other than that you are perfectly fine driving on a bumpy road.
Crows feet and comma line worse after forehead Botox, will it wear off?
Wrinkles after Botox
Nov 28, 2023
No new wrinkles or lines will be caused by Botox injection. Most of the time,people think lines are forming after the botox wears off which looks worse than before. This is simply your face returning back to its natural state.
Would I be a good candidate for under-eye Botox?
Botox vs Blepharoplasty
Nov 28, 2023
Botox is great when it comes to getting rid of crows feet. However, it will not get rid of the wrinkles under your eyes. I do recommend you upper and lower blepharoplasty for fresh and younger look.
How common are complications due to having implants?
Complications after breast augmentation surgery
Nov 20, 2023
We don’t expect complications such as waterfall deformity, bottoming out or capsular contraction in many patients. If these problems occur, it will be at least 3 years after the surgery
Immediate complications such as bleeding and infection is very rare and happens in the first 48 hours after the surgery
Are textured implants as safe as smooth implants?
Risk of breast cancer with textured implants.
Nov 20, 2023
BIA-ALCL is a type of lymphoma that can develop around breast implants. BIA-ALCL occurs in patients who have breast implants with textured surfaces. This is a cancer of the immune system, not breast cancer. With that said, your chances of developing this type of cancer is extremely rare, approximately 1 in 300000 people
