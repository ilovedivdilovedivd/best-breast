Braces question?! Does your midline matter when you get your Braces off?
Midlines
May 5, 2017
There have been many studies done to determine whether a non-dentist notices the midline being off. There is actually a pretty big range before it is noticed or commented on. Midlines are a little harder to fix, sometimes teeth are different sizes and shapes and they may not be perfect. As an Orthodontist, mine are slightly off but nobody has ever commented on it.

Best of luck!

Is it possible to get Invisalign without having existing cavities filled?
Better to do the cavities
Apr 5, 2017
Yes, it would be better to take care of the cavities first. Even though they may not bother you, they can continue to grow and eventually cause much worse problems (which can be much more costly in the long run). I totally understand that you want to have straight teeth and a beautiful smile but it is very important to also make sure your teeth are healthy.
All the best,

Dr. Pam

Would Clear aligner or Spring aligner provide me with straighter teeth?
Clear aligners
Apr 5, 2017
Yes, clear aligners would help to bring those top front teeth back. You may be able to do just the top only if there are no concerns with the bottom, however sometimes it is best to do both to get the proper bite established. Your doctor will be able to do a thorough exam and give you a better estimate on how long it would take.
Good luck!
Dr. Pam

Why are my trays feeling tighter at the end of 2 weeks?
Tray fit
Apr 5, 2017
In general, it is best for the tray to feel “loose” before you move to the next one. However, it is not common for the tray to start to feel tighter all of a sudden. I would express your concerns with your Dr. and maybe you can go in for a quick exam to make sure nothing else is going on.
All the best,
Dr. Pam
