Would a breast lift be necessary or can fat transfer give more volume? (No implants)
Breast Lift is Recommended
Mar 14, 2020
Hello and thank you for the question and photos. Since the Areolas and Nipples are considerably lower than the breast Crease (Medical Term is Ptosis Level III) you definitely need a Breast Lift, with or without implants. The difference is that a Breast Implant would provide upper half of the breast fullness. If you consider that your breasts are firm, then a breast lift alone might be the best choice. The type of Breast Lift would be the Inverted “T” scar or also called “Anchor” scar.
