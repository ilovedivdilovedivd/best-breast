Doctors

Kevin Dieffenbach, MD

Name: Kevin Dieffenbach, MD
Statement: Dr. Kevin Dieffenbach, MD is a board-certified plastic surgeon with over three decades of experience and the founder of Plastic Surgery Oahu in Aiea, Hawaii. Though he performs a wide range of plastic surgery procedures across the face, breast, and body, Dr. Dieffenbach is best known for specializing in treatments including Asian blepharoplasty and rhinoplasty, breast enhancement, and mommy makeovers. “Choosing to have plastic surgery is an important decision, but choosing the right plastic surgeon is even more important,” says Dr. Dieffenbach.

After graduating cum laude with a neuroscience degree from the University of Rochester, Dr. Dieffenbach received his MD from Case Western Reserve University, He then went on to complete three residencies, one in general surgery and two in plastic surgery. He is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and is a member of multiple professional organizations, including the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.
