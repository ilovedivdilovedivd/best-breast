Is a breast auto augmentation better than a lift plus fat transfer?
Please read below
Apr 4, 2023
The autologous breast augmentation is carried out in the context of an uplift and it requires adequate amount of breast tissue for your aesthetic goals. Fat transfer would add to the volume of your breasts. Those 2 are not necessarily mutually exclusive, in fact very often they are offered in conjunction with each other. A thorough consultation will help understand more and discuss your concerns.
Breasts 3 cup sizes bigger with fat transfer?
Please read below
Apr 4, 2023
Due to the limited retention capacity long term in the breasts but also the need for some tissue expansion for this cap size (from A to DD) fat transfer by itself would not be a reliable technique. Maybe consider a hybrid augmentation (implants + fat). Keeping the implant smaller for limited complication risks and also using the fat as a natural filler for a more bespoke look. A thorough consultation will give you the opportunity to discuss your concerns and your options
Can you have fat transfer to breasts with existing implants?
Read below
Apr 4, 2023
This surgery is known as “hybrid breast augmentation “ and indeed can be done whilst keeping the existing implants. In fact it is often used to address rippling following a breast augmentation with implants. The fat transferred will add some padding to the breast to conceal the ripples. A thorough consultation will answer all of your very valid safety concerns.
