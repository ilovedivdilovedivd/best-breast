Should I wear a bra for sleeping to help with gravity?
Yes, Wearing a bra even while sleeping is a good idea.
Jan 15, 2019
The effects of gravity on the breast are unrelenting.  No one past the age of 40 has to be reminded of this.  The ligaments that keep the breast in position during ones youth and early adulthood are called Cooper’s ligaments.  These stretch as time passes and anything that you can do to minimize this is only for the better.  Wearing a bra even while sleeping is a good idea.
