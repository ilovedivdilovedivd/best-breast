Want upper lip to be fuller: what type of lip surgery would give the appearance of more volume
Fat grafting is an option
Nov 1, 2022
No injectables, no lip lift, no problem. It sounds like you may be a candidate for fat grafting to your lips for more volume. It's a permanent solution, whereas fillers typically last 6 months, and it wouldn't change your philtrum distance.
Looking into lip augmentation: which is better a lip lift or fat transfer? Maybe both?
Fat transfer is great for more volume; a lift is most appropriate for long philtrums
Nov 1, 2022
If you want more volume and fullness to your lips, fat transfer makes the most sense vs a lip lift. A lip lift is a procedure specifically to shorten philtrums (with the added benefit of revealing more of the top lip) so yes, it's most appropriate for people with long philtrums.

A second round of fat grafting can be done at least 6 weeks after the first.

Do I need a brow lift/anchor blepharoplasty?
Sound like ptosis and good news is it's treatable
Nov 1, 2022
Upper eyelid surgery with ptosis repair is appropriate in this case. It's a relatively quick and easy procedure that can be done under local anesthesia. It would effectively address the feeling of heaviness and unevenness that you experience.
Can you combine hydroquinone with Retin-A or do you have to apply them separately?
Perfect combo
Nov 1, 2022
Use hydroquinone on its own in the AM. Then use both hydroquinone and Retin-A at night - doesn't matter which one goes first. You can mix it if you want. If you experience redness, sensitivity, or irritation, use the Retin-A less often.
Can I get that snatched waist look with lipo? Will it do anything for my stretch marks? (Photo
A tummy tuck would be most appropriate
Nov 1, 2022
Liposuction would be helpful in removing excess fat, but you will likely be left with loose skin. A tummy tuck is more appropriate in removing the skin as well as fat. Some of your stretch marks will be removed in this process and what's remaining will be flattened but not completely gone.
Would I benefit from a chin implant and rhinoplasty? Concerned about lack of face definition.
Perfect is the enemy of good
Nov 1, 2022
You are beautiful and do not necessarily need any surgical enhancements - unless you want to. Your concerns are valid and can be addressed in a variety of ways (surgeries included). However, before considering permanent surgeries, you may want to consider injectables like nose, chin, or jaw filler.

Yes, you can get a BBL
Nov 1, 2022
As long as it's not actively infected, you can absolutely get a BBL. That area could be avoided entirely and the necessary incisions needed for the operation can be made elsewhere.
How do IG models get these results?
Likely a great combination of editing and BBL or buttock implants
Nov 1, 2022
No shade to these ladies, some of them just look a little anatomically impossible. However, it's totally realistic if they had a lot of fat grafting or implants. Many places have limits on how much fat can be removed in single operation (I have a 5 liter limit at my practice) for safety. Would I benefit from a BBL or liposculpting?
Only a BBL or buttock implants can give you volume and projection
Nov 1, 2022
A tummy tuck and liposuction alone will help flatten out your stomach and contour your body. Your waist will be smaller and by comparison, you may feel that your buttocks is more prominent. However, the only way to give you more volume and projection to your buttocks is with fat injections or implants.
Should a doctor be cosmetic board certified or certified in plastic surgery? Which one is bett

Nov 1, 2022
A Board Certified Plastic Surgeon is guaranteed to have extensive experience in cosmetic, plastic, and reconstructive surgery - whereas a cosmetic surgeon may not. The best way to find out if your doctor is board certified is to look them up on Plasticsurgery.org. Good luck!
