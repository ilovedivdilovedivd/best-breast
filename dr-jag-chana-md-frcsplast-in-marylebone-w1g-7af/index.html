Will I need a revision? Upper blepharoplasty and mini lift done 5 months ago, unsure if resu
Timing for revision surgery
Nov 13, 2023
It is too soon for adjustments after surgery. Often it is best to wait between 9 months and a year after surgery in order t wait for the scars and tissues to settle. It may be possible to trim a little extra skin from the inner aspect of the upper right eyelid in due course under local anaesthetic but best to wait in the first instance.
Age 40 & at a loss for deep tear troughs - what can I do?
prominent tear trough groove
Nov 13, 2023
Looking at your photographs one option may be to use a transconjunctival blepharoplasty. In this surgery the existing fat pockets of the lower eyelid are redraped downwards over the teartrough and they fill the groove. Its is a scarless procedure since the surgery is performed from the back of the lower eyelid. You may also need some fat transfer in combination along with possible laser tightening of the skin. These assessments can be made at an in person consultation
Can this bulge 6 months after upper eyelid surgery be corrected?
Bulge after upper blepharoplasty
Nov 13, 2023
It may be possible to revise. It is best to bet in touch with your Plastic Surgeon since an examination in person is best to make assessments rather than on a photo.
What if I panic during upper & lower eyelid reduction under local anaesthetic?!
Anxiety about local anaesthetic procedure
Nov 13, 2023
If you are anxious about this it is best to have sedation or twilight anaesthesia which is straightforward to arrange .
Is there anything I can do for my eye asymmetry?
Eyelid asymmetry
Nov 13, 2023
Asymmetry in the face is a normal feature and arises from a slight difference in the bone structure, strength of the muscles of facial expression etc. In your case the differences are within a normal range and I would not suggest any interventions
How to restore volume on hollow eye corners (tear trough)?
Hollowness at the lateral teartough
Nov 13, 2023
A good option is fillers . PRP and skin boosters do not provide volume. Fillers will need repeating and therefore another option is fat transfer.
Best to get an opinion from a Plastic Surgeon who can discuss the pros and cons of these procedures with you.
What are my options for minimizing the appearance of hollow eyes besides a fat transfer and
Correcting hollows avoiding fat transfer or fillers
Nov 13, 2023
An alternative option is a transconjunctival blepharoplasty which is performed from the back of the lower eyelid so no external scar. The fat pockets are not removed but conserved and redraped over the tear trough. This means the existing lower eyelid fat is shifted in position to fill the hollow area.
