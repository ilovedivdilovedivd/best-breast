Am I good candidate for a full tummy tuck?
Tummy Tuck Candidate
Jan 17, 2020
To achieve a tighter and flatter abdomen, a tummy tuck would be ideal.  A tummy tuck with lips will eliminate the loose overhanging skin and repair your core muscles.  Typically an overnight stay and most patients return to work by two weeks out from surgery.  Lipo alone will not eliminate the loose skin and may make it worse.  You are correct in getting to your ideal weight prior to the procedure.  Good luck!
