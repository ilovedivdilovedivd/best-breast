Should I have the umbilical hernia fixed prior to a tummy tuck with a general surgeon or af
Dr John Newton
Mar 20, 2018
Hi J
Have them done at the same time. Ask your plastic surgeon if he can do it for you as he will probably be able to. If not ask if he can arrange for you to see a general surgeon in advance and ask if they can operate together.
One thing to be aware of is the potential of damage to the blood supply of your umbilicus (belly button) when an umbilical hernia and an abdominoplasty are performed together. It should be quite OK if both surgeons are aware of this potential problem.

BA size recommendations? I am looking for some professional recommendations for size/profil
Dr John Newton
Mar 18, 2018
Hi Fort Lauderdale
Your implant size should fit your fame and overall physique.
The width of your breast is the single most important measurement. It dictates the correct width of the implant for you.
Next is the height of your nipple measure from your collar-bone. This helps determine the vertical height of your implant.
Last is how much you want your breasts to project forward. this is really up to you.
