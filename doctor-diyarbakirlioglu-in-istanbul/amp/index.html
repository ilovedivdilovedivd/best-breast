Statement: Dr. Diyarbakirlioglu is a specialist in Plastic Reconstructive and Aesthetic Surgery and graduated from Istanbul University Istanbul (Çapa) Faculty of Medicine in 2008. He underwent his specialized training at the prestigious Cerrahpasa Faculty of Medicine, specifically in the Department of Plastic, Reconstructive, and Aesthetic Surgery Clinic, renowned as one of Turkey’s premier centers for plastic surgery.

In pursuit of further expertise, Dr. Diyarbakirlioglu sought training at the renowned clinic of world-famous plastic surgeon Bahman Guyuron, located in Cleveland, Ohio. There, he focused on specialized training in facial and nasal aesthetics. Simultaneously, he collaborated with Dr. Mario Rietjens on various cases of aesthetic and reconstructive breast surgery at the Istituto Europeo di Oncologia in Milan, Italy.

His professional interests encompass a wide range of specialties, including post-bariatric surgery to address body sagging and deformations following significant weight loss, body contouring through liposuction, mummy makeover procedures, aesthetic and reconstructive breast surgery, as well as facial and nasal aesthetics. Dr. Diyarbakirlioglu remains dedicated to advancing scientific knowledge in the field of post-bariatric surgery, continually conducting research to refine the techniques and algorithms he has pioneered.
