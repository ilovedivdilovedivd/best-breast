Can Botox fix sagging skin on buttocks, legs and arms?
Botox for softening facial lines
May 6, 2022
Botox is FDA approved to relax facial muscles and in turn, soften the lines created from moving the face. Unfortunately it will not tighten skin on the body, but I recommend looking into nonsurgical skin tightening treatments for the body! You may be interested in CoolSculpting or JPlasma.
Can I achieve a flat stomach without a tummy tuck?
Tummy Tuck or Liposuction
Feb 21, 2022
Great question! Based on your photos, I would advise you'd be happiest with a tummy tuck and liposuction. Your abdomen can become flatter with solely liposuction. However, you will still have excess skin and the laxity won't be solved. I recommend scheduling a consultation with a board-certified plastic surgeon to receive a professional opinion in person.
Botox, gum contouring or both for gummy smile?
Botox for gummy smile
Sep 8, 2021
Great question. I treat many patients with the same concern. Approximately 4 units of Botox can be a great solution to relaxing the muscles that pull the upper lip up. I recommend scheduling a consultation with a provider near you.
Botox, gum contouring or both for gummy smile?
Botox for gummy smile
Sep 8, 2021
Great question! I would recommend a few units of Botox to the upper lip to relax the muscles that pull up and show the gum. I've seen great success with just a few units to relax gummy smiles.
Is 37 too young for fillers around the mouth and cheeks? What are the long term effects?
Nasolabial folds filler
Mar 17, 2021
Great, question. No, you aren't too young! If you choose an experienced injector, they will use the correct product for your face and goals. They may recommend filler in the cheeks for lift, and a touch in the nasolabial folds (around mouth and nose). Hyaluronic acid fillers such as Juvederm dissolve naturally within 6 months to 2 years. Check out Juvederm's site that I linked. Hope this helps!
