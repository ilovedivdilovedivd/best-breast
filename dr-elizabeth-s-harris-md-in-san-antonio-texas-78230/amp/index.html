Can I get a full tummy tuck done with BMI 40?
Is a patient a candidate for a tummy tuck with a high BMI?
Sep 19, 2018
The risks of any surgery increase with a high BMI.  Risks such as wound healing abnormalities, infection, seromas, DVTs and many other complications are significantly increased with a high BMI.   In addition to that, you have nerve damage, multiple hernias, and mesh which all add to the increased risks.  Reduction of your weight may reduce the incidence of complications and would certainly also improve the aesthetics of this type of procedure.   
When will I be able to wear a bra after a breast reduction and lift?
When should a patient wear a bra after breast reduction?
Sep 19, 2018
Patients should wear bras immediately after surgery.   A sports bra may be more comfortable initially because underwires may irritate the base of the breasts.   After 2-3 weeks, an underwire bra is well tolerated.   Sometimes an ace wrap maybe used to reduce localized swelling in addition to a sports bra.  Patients should sleep in a bra during the first several weeks as well.

Breast lift before kid(s)?
Breast lift before kids?
Jun 15, 2018
Breasts are certainly changed after giving birth and breast feeding.  Sometimes the changes to a woman’s breasts are minor but sometimes the changes result in significant sagging, flattening, and stretch mark development.  Because of the potential of skin laxity as well as the possible difficulty with breast feeding, it is usually best to wait until after children to have cosmetic surgery to your breasts.   To answer your question, I would “sit tight and wait”.
