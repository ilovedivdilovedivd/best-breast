Can orbital rim implants rupture and would blindness result?
Infraorbital Facial Implants
Jun 16, 2020
Thank you for your question. Facial implants in general are made from silicone, of firm consistency, strong and supportive. The most important potential risk is infection and so a post operative prophylactic course of antibiotics is recommended (and having a patient who is a non-smoker). Although one can never say never, the implant really shouldn’t rupture or split.
Is it possible to have upper eyelid surgery with SVTs?
Upper Lid Surgery under Local Anaesthetic
Jun 16, 2020
Thank you for your question. Upper Lid Blepharoplasty is commonly undertaken as a local anaesthetic procedure, however due to your underlying history SVT adrenaline should be avoided. A careful, controlled approach should really make this safe to manage. **blood thinning medication would be much more of a concern**. Do please double check with your Cardiologist their view prior to booking with your Plastic Surgeon, with very best wishes.
