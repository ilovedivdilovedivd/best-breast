Amazing Breast Implants In Denver, CO

Breast augmentation is the most common cosmetic surgery in Denver CO because it provides a consistent way for a woman to change the shape, position and size of her breasts. In our practice, it is extremely common to perform a breast augmentation in small for petite women.

The essential point is to work closely with woman in picking a breast implant volume that will help them meet their aesthetic goals but will look balanced on their torso. It is also important to carefully choose the profile of the implant.

Petite patients should not use a low profile implant as this can make their breasts look too wide or slide off the chest and under the arms. To learn more about breast augmentation, see photos, and help you decide which one is best for you, please visit us at the link below.

Appropriate size is in the eye of the beholder

This is a very common question and does not have a definitive answer. To answer the question you and your plastic surgeon need to talk and you need to try on implants and ideally show the plastic surgeon some pictures of what you would like to look like and what you don’t want to look like.

Putting all that information together will usually give you and the plastic surgeon a “neighborhood” of implants that would work for you.

Saline Breast Implants & Weight Loss / Gain

Saline or Silicone Breast Implants do not inherently cause weight gain or loss. With time the weight of the implant cn cause the overlying breast tissue to stretch especially when implants in Denver are not placed under the muscle.
Rippling after Breast Augmentation

Rippling is due to several factors. If a patient has very thin skin, rippling is more likely regardless of silicone or saline implant placement. If the implant is wider than your chest and is pushing out against your skin, rippling is more likely.

Textured implants in Denver, especially if placed over the muscle have a higher chance of rippling. The best way to minimize chance of rippling with saline implants is to pick a smooth implant which is not wider than your chest, place it sub-pectoral and slightly over-fill it (10% or so).

However, there is no guarantee you will not have any rippling.

Breast implant position

Implants in Denver can be placed above or below muscle and they can also be changed into a new pocket.

Patients who are better candidates to have implants placed over the muscle are those who have enough breast tissue so that the shape of the implant and any rippling or edges can not be seen under the skin.
Other patients who may benefit from implants in Denver above the muscles are bodybuilders or highly active individuals who use their pectoralis muscles for strength or high-level activity. There are other pros and cons to having implants over or under the muscle and it is best to have an in-office examination to determine which placement is safest for you while also achieving your goals.

As a tear drop implant is shaped so that there is less volume in the top half of the implant, there will be less fullness. Fullness of the top half of the breast is desired by some but not by other patients.
