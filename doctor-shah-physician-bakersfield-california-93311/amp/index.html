Radiesse removal – what are my options?
Radiesse removal – what are my options?
Dec 12, 2023
Removing Radiesse typically involves waiting for the filler to naturally dissipate, as it’s not reversible. If you’re unsatisfied or experiencing issues, consult the injecting professional for guidance. In some cases, hyaluronic acid fillers can be used to dissolve certain types of fillers, but Radiesse is calcium-based, so it may not respond to this. Surgical removal might be considered in extreme cases, but it’s less common. Always consult with a skilled healthcare professional for guidance.
