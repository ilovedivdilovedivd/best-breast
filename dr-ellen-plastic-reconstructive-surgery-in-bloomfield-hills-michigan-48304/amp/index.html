Ellen A. Janetzke, MD

Name: Ellen A. Janetzke, MD
Statement: Dr. Ellen A. Janetzke, MD is a board-certified plastic and reconstructive surgeon practicing in Bloomfield Hills, Michigan. She performs a wide variety of face and body procedures for men and women, with special expertise in breast augmentation and reconstruction, mommy makeovers, and skin tightening, including body lift surgery. Over two decades in practice, she has gained a reputation among her patients and colleagues as an exceptionally skilled, supportive surgeon.

“I chose plastic and reconstructive surgery because I love seeing the smiles on my patients' faces, and the feeling that comes along with helping someone reach their goal,” says Dr. Janetzke. “I love connecting with patients and helping them achieve the look they desire.”

Dr. Janetzke received her medical degree from Wayne State University School of Medicine in Detroit. She completed a fellowship in Hand Surgery at Detroit Medical Center and completed her General Surgery requirement at William Beaumont Hospital in Royal Oak, Michigan. Following that, she headed to Philadelphia for her plastic surgery training at Temple University Hospital.

Board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, Dr. Janetzke is a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.
She is also a member of Plasty-PAC, an organization of plastic surgeons dedicated to improving patient care and the future of plastic surgery through promoting new policy initiatives. And she sits on the RealSelf Medical Review board, where she provides oversight on the medical accuracy of consumer educational content.
