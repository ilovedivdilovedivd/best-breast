Greetings,
Your chest wall shape (wide sternal area) and current lateral position of your breasts will limit creating cleavage. There is risk for further lateral rotation of your nipples outward, the closer to the midline the implants are placed. And lastly, if you try to place the implants closer together, the skin is much thinner toward the area in front of your sternum. This could put you at risk for a very visible and palpable implant (rippling and worse).
