Tear Drop Vs Round Breast Implants for Petite Build?
Tear Drop vs Round Implants
Sep 15, 2015
I tend to prefer round implants. I am still concerned about implant rotation creating a poor result. However I do think it is reasonable if someone has very little breast tissue and wants a natural result. In this case you need to make a tight pocket to prevent rotation. Most of my patients are often looking for more upper pole fullness and tend to go with round high profile. The tear drop is flatter on top and that is often not my patients goals.
How Long is Breast Implants Recovery Time?
Breast Implant Recovery Time
Sep 15, 2015
I tell patients no heavy lifting for about 6 weeks. The amount of pain depends on where the implant is placed above vs below the muscle. Below tends to hurt more. Also peoples pain thresholds are very different. Overall patient recovery with breast augmentation is relatively quick.
How Much Weight Can I Lose from Tummy Tuck Surgery?
Weight Loss After Tummy Tuck
Sep 15, 2015
Tummy tuck surgery is more about removing excess skin. The tissue removed usually weighs 2-5 pounds but varies depending on patient size. I think what is more important is that once the hanging tissue is removed patients are much more likely to exercise and lose weight. I often see my patients back 6 months later and its not unusually to see them a lot skinnier.
Tummy Tuck Recovery
Tummy Tuck Recovery
Sep 15, 2015
I think all patients have a different pain threshold. Usually patients are have a difficult 1st night so I have them go to a recovery center. They are almost always ready to come home the next day. For about 5-7 days it is usually difficult to stand up straight. I allow patients to go back to regular activities after 6 weeks.
