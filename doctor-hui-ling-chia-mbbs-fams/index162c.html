Am I a good candidate for neck and jawline Vaser liposuction?
A more sculpted jawline

Vaser Liposuction will help to slim the under-chin area and sculpt a better contour between your and chin and neck. I understand that you do not want a genioplasty to address the wear chin. Less / non-invasive options you may want to consider include chin fillers or fat graft or implant (scarless). Botox will help to reduce chin skin dimpling.
Can I achieve parallel eyelids without medial epicanthoplasty?
Possible with incisional technique
Sep 6, 2019
Yes it is possible to create parallel crease without medial epicanthoplasty. The inner corner of the crease needs to be fixed more securely during the surgery, so the incisional technique is recommended for you. Hope this information helps.
