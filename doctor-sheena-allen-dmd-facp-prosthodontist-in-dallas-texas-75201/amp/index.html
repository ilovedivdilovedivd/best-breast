Should I have my teeth pulled prior to getting screws in for permanent top denture?
A consult is needed
Sep 6, 2019
In most cases, the teeth are pulled before implants are placed. Occasionally, we will stagger extractions and implant placement so that we have something to support the denture. This is why a consult is needed. But in general, the teeth are removed, a denture is placed, and then the implants are placed.

How long can a tooth capped stay on?
This is not easy to predict.
Aug 10, 2019
The dental literature shows that a very good crown has the average lifespan of 7-10 years, with excellent ones lasting even longer. I see patients all the time with crowns that last 30 years or more. A dentists checks for issues with the crown when you get your regular dental exams. They look for leakage or possible cavities under the crown as well as breakage. If your dentist can't see anything wrong with the crown and you approve of the esthetics, you are in good shape!
