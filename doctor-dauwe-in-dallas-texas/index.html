Why do I have ripples underneath my breast and on the side? Is this normal? Can it be fixed?
Rippling after breast implants
Jan 5, 2019
Rippling occurs more commonly in thin patients. If you have less soft tissue covering the implant, then it is more likely that you will see the rippling in the shell of the implant. Going larger is not the right answer to fix this. This will only stretch your soft tissues leading to worse rippling. If you exchange your implants for “form stable” implants, which comes in the Allergan Soft Touch or Cohesive or Sientra 106 gel, this will help.
Uneven Nipples After Breast Augmentation and Lift
Give it time
Jan 5, 2019
Your results look great for 2 weeks. It is not natural to have “perfectly circular” areolas, and very difficult to achieve that surgically, especially with the amount of lift and size implant that was used. Give it time and allow your tissues to adjust to the implant. Time fixes a lot!!
Should I get areola scar revision?
Areolar Pigment on Lower Breast
Jan 5, 2019
Hi there, When a breast lift is performed the skin from the areola is sometimes left at the lower part of the breast when the nipple is moved up. This is a simple revision, but if you want all the pigment out, you will likely need to have a surgical revision. I would consider this more of a “second stage” to the operation rather than a “revision”. Your results are really great, and this is a pretty simple fix. Generally, I recommend 6 months before doing surgery again.
Breast Implants for a Smoker?
Smoking with Cosmetic Surgery
Nov 22, 2018
I always recommend that my patients stop smoking altogether, but my patients must be completely nicotine free for 4 weeks before and after surgery. I do not make exceptions for the type of surgery being performed. Every surgery requires wound healing, and it is scientifically proven that nicotine impairs wound healing. In my opinion, you are just asking for problems.
Breast Implants Won’t Drop?
Implant position
Nov 22, 2018
Implants “drop” into position over 3 months following surgery. Everyone progresses at different rates. What is actually occurring in your breast, is the implant is expanding the lower pole of your breast. Breast bands can help tremendously. It would be helpful to see some photos of your implants. Ultimately, I recommend that you speak with your surgeon about your options at this point.
Which Breast Implants Are Most Likely to Last a Lifetime?
Implants are not permanent devices
Nov 22, 2018
There is no one implant that will consistently outlast another, as there are too many variables leading to revision surgery. The most common reason for implant revision is malposition or droopiness of the breast. In these cases, the implant has not been compromised at all. The factors that do increase longevity of your implants are choosing the implant appropriately in terms of the size and match to your anatomy. Why would I have lingering pain 9 months after breast lift and augmentation?
Long term pain after breast surgery
Nov 21, 2018
This can occur for several reasons. The cutaneous nerves could be stretched from either the implant or the donut lift. You could also have an implant malposition, which can place more stretch on the nerves going to your skin and nipple. There are some medications you can take to help with nerve pain, but I would address these concerns with your surgeon. Ultimately, it can take 12-24 months for nerve pain to completely resolve.
How long does this procedure take and what is the recovery time length?
Cost of Breast Augmentation

Breast augmentation costs vary depending on the geographic area you are in. RealSelf does a really nice job of analyzing average cost on each area and supplying that information for consumers. Should I go for a surgeon based on these titles?
Awards Vs. Training and experience
Nov 21, 2018
I would choose your surgeon based on his/her level of experience and training rather than number of awards or special feature in magazines. These awards are often not given on merit. However, your general level of comfort with the surgeon during the consult means a lot. Go with your gut! Don’t be swayed by ads and awards that can be bought.
Is this redness on both breasts after a BA normal?
Breast redness following breast augmentation
Nov 21, 2018
Redness of the skin following surgery can be normal, but you discuss this with your surgeon. If you have any tenderness, fever, or chills this may be a sign of infection. The fact that it is on both sides indicates that it is likely just an inflammatory response to the surgery, but you should certainly get it checked out by your plastic surgeon.
Breast augmentation and racket sports: will breast implants affect me playing Badminton?
Breast Augmentation in athletes
Oct 24, 2018
If you choose your implants correctly, your activity level and arm movements should not affect your results. I recommend that my patients wait 6 weeks before returning to any strenuous activity, so the implants can develop a strong capsule to keep them in place during such activities. The only movement that I have seen displace implants is bench press. The action of the pectoralis muscle will gradually move the implant down and lateral.
What Are the Effects if I Keep Wearing my Sports Bra After Breast Augmentation?
Bra after Breast Augmentation
Sep 23, 2018
It is difficult to make a general statement about wearing a bra after breast augmentation. If your implants are in good position, then it is a good idea to provide support to them in an effort to keep them in good position. Some surgeons avoid an underwire bra postoperatively to avoid pressure on the healing incision. I would ask your surgeon what he/she prefers and follow those instructions. Good luck!

When Can I Start Raising my Arms over my Head After BA?
Raising your arms over your head after breast implant surgery
Sep 18, 2018
Every surgeon is different in postoperative care, so I would definitely clear it with your plastic surgeon. I allow my patients to raise their arms over their head the day after surgery to keep their joints mobile. I think early activity hastens the recovery process. Now, I would be careful about returning to work to soon as a flight attendant. That is a very active job, and you don’t want to have any issues while you’re on the plane!
Breasts healing differently post breast augmentation!? 2 weeks post op sub muscular silicone implants
Implant asymmetry
Aug 19, 2018
It is still too early to be worried. I would suggest that you discuss this with someone at the office while your surgeon is out of town. They might be able to reach him and express your concern. I generally ask patients to wear a compressive band across the top of their breasts if their implants are riding too high. This may help, but I would go with what your surgeon prefers you do.
Post-op 9 days. 59 year old in great shape. When can I expect to drive and resume normal activities?
Driving after surgery
Aug 19, 2018
In general, I would wait to drive until you are completely off narcotic pain meds and any other meds that may affect your judgement. Also, wait until you are able to react quickly in traffic if you need to. Speak to your surgeon too! Everyone has slightly different advice, and its important to follow the postoperative protocol that your surgeon has designed for you.
