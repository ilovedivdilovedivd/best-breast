Screening after breast implant

High profile breast implant
May 21, 2022
Hello. In my opinion profile of implant is for projection of breast. Clevage will depend on plane of implant placement and not profile. So you can go with high profile implant with good clevage as well. Thanks for posting your query. Hope i have cleared your doubt. All the best!
29 Y/O- Would facial fat grafting help me get rid of nasolabial wrinkles?
Fillers and fat transfer are the options for you
May 21, 2022
Hello there,
You can try fillers and see what amount is adequate for you. It is temporary and will stay for a year.
If you are happy with the change, you can ask for similar volume of fat transfer later. This will give permanent results. Consult a board certified plastic surgeon for the best care. Hope this helps.
If I have GERD does it increase my risk of infection for having breast implants?
Breast implant with GERD
May 21, 2022
Hello. GERD will surely not increase the risk of infection after breast implant. But you will have to take course of antibiotics after implant surgery. Better to visit board certified plastic surgeon and get examined. Thanks for posting your query. Hope i have cleared your doubt. All the best
Breast implants with fat transfer to reach desired size or just bigger implants?
Breast implant
May 21, 2022
Hello.Thanks for posting your query.Size of breast implant depends on the width and base diameter. Hope I have cleared your doubt. All the best.
