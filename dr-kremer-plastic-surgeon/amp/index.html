Statement: Dr. Dirk Kremer is a board-certified plastic surgeon in London, England, with over 25 years of experience. At his boutique practice, Harley St. Aesthetics, Dr. Kremer combines precise surgical artistry with advanced noninvasive treatments. He specializes in breast enhancement, tummy tucks, and complete mommy makeovers, as well as face and neck lifts and eyelid surgery. He also offers a range of popular nonsurgical treatments, including Botox dermal fillers, PRP, and his signature Centennial Lift, a three-step facial rejuvenation procedure that targets wrinkles, sun damage, and scarring.

Dr. Kremer’s medical education began in Munich, Germany, and continued at Mount Sinai Medical School in New York City, where he earned his medical degree. He continued his studies at the University of California, San Diego before returning to Munich to the Ludwig Maximilian University, where he began his cosmetic surgery training.

After earning board certification, Dr. Kremer completed his plastic surgery residency at the Teaching Hospital of Technical University in Munich. He then returned to the United States for fellowship training in Los Angeles, California, where he refined his facial plastic surgery skills and mastered some of the techniques he is known for today, learning from some of the most respected plastic surgeons in the field.

Dr. Kremer has been featured in numerous publications—including Vogue, Glamour, W Magazine, and National Geographic—particularly for his expertise in breast enhancement surgery. He has also made Tatler’s Best Surgeons list in the eye lift, facelift, and neck lift categories.
