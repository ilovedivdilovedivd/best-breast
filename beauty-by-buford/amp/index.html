Doctors

Gregory A. Buford, MD, FACS

Name: Gregory A. Buford, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Gregory A. Buford, MD, FACS is a board-certified plastic surgeon with two decades of experience, based in Denver, Colorado. He is an exceptionally skilled Voluma, Juvéderm, Botox, and Kybella Master Injector who trains other injectors across the nation. Additionally, he is renowned for his expertise in breast enhancement surgery, having performed more than 6,000 surgeries on clients from around the world. Dr. Buford also specializes in facial rejuvenation, full body restoration, and hair restoration, including NeoGraft hair transplants and nonsurgical treatments such as PRP.

After obtaining his medical degree from Georgetown University School of Medicine, Dr. Buford completed both his general surgery and plastic surgery residencies at the Cleveland Clinic Foundation. He is also a fellow with both the American College of Surgeons and Plastic Surgery Associates. Additionally, he’s a graduate of the Baker/Stuzin/Baker Cosmetic Surgery Fellowship in Miami, making him one of only a few plastic surgeons in the Rocky Mountain and Denver region to complete formal postgraduate training.

Throughout his impressive career, Dr. Buford has received numerous awards. Since 2014, he has annually received the Doctors' Choice Awards City Winner award for Denver, and won the 2017 Top Plastic Surgeon of April Award from the same organization. In 2017, he was the recipient of Aesthetic Everything’s prestigious Diamond Crystal Award. Dr. Buford is also a RealSelf "Top Doctor”—a title given to only 10% of the RealSelf doctor community—which means he has exceptional patient ratings and positive feedback.

In addition to his ongoing practice, Dr. Buford is a consultant, advisor, and speaker for Allergan, Evolus, Revance, and Apyx Medical. He also serves on Advisory Boards for RealSelf, The Aesthetics Guide, and Modern Aesthetics magazine. In 2010, he released the book Beauty and the Business, in 2017 he wrote Eat, Drink, Heal, and in 2021 he published The Boob Job Bible, Your Ultimate Guide To Breast Surgery. All of which have been sold around the world, and used by both consumers as well as industry to help grow their businesses and to help their patients heal more optimally.
