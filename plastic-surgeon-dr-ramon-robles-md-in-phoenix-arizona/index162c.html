Do you think I’ll need a tummy tuck too with my BBL?
Recommend weight loss first
Jan 9, 2020
Isabella, thank you for you question. I know you are excited to get a Tummy Tuck, but you have too much intra-abdominal fat(fat underneath your abdominal muscles) that is pushing your belly out. A tummy tuck will not give you a flat abdomen. I recommend you try to lose some weight and decrease the amount of intra-abdominal fat before a tummy tuck. In the long run this will give you the best chance at the result you want.
Best of Luck

How long will the tightening effects of a tummy tuck last?
Correction of Diastasis Recti(seperation of the abdominal muscles) is coreected with permanent sutures

This is called Diastasis Recti. During an abdominoplasty(Tummy Tuck), permanent sutures are placed to bring the muscles back together in the midline. Could butt implants give me more shelf, rear projection and lift to my butt?
Buttock Implant projection
Oct 1, 2019
Buttock implants give buttock projection, upper buttock fullness, and some hip volume. The buttock implant will slightly improve the the skin laxity in the gluteal fold. The scar in the gluteal fold can be mildly improved, but this area is a high motion area(walking,sitting,standing) and scars in these type of areas will almost always widened. However, when the underlying fascia is sutured correctly, the scar can be minimized.
I had 2 BBLs. Would butt implants help with the cellulite and lumps in my butt from those BBLs? (photo
Buttock Skin Smoothness
Oct 1, 2019
As previously mentioned, cellulite is caused by tissue under the skin that is pulling down the skin. You have a combination of cellulite and areas of skin that are not smooth due to lack of volume under these depressed areas. Buttock Implants and Buttock Fat grafting will slightly improve the areas, but the skin will not be completely smooth.
