Statement: Dr. Victoria Veytsman, known by her patients as Dr. V, is a cosmetic dentist with over 17 years in practice. She is the founder of Cosmetic Dental Studios, with locations in Manhattan, New York City, and Beverly Hills in Los Angeles.

Dr. Veytsman’s niche in cosmetic dentistry has led her to create gorgeous smiles for some of the world’s top beauties, including contestants in the Miss Universe and Miss America organizations, as well as members of professional dance teams for the Nets and Giants. She has also helped celebrity clients achieve perfect smiles for the Emmy Awards.

Dr. Veytsman has expertise in everything from teeth whitening, dental crowns, and Invisalign to porcelain veneers and dental implants, but her specialty is smile makeovers.

“I chose cosmetic dentistry because of the life-changing effect it has for our patients. What I love most is the transformations we have the privilege of being part of on a daily basis. We do more than smile makeovers—we improve confidence,” says Dr. Veytsman. She adds, “Aesthetic medicine is the perfect blend of art and science. The smile makeovers that we do allow me to be a doctor while being creative at the same time.”

To earn her position as a top cosmetic dentist in the New York and Los Angeles areas, Dr. Veytsman obtained her dental degree (DDS) at New York University. Afterward, she completed a residency at Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons at St. Luke’s-Roosevelt Hospital. She also pursued a fellowship in implantology at New York University, making her an expert at dental implants.

Today, Dr. Veytsman and her team provide a range of cosmetic dental services in both New York and Beverly Hills. In addition to serving patients bicoastally, she welcomes people from around the world who seek out her services.

“We strive for patient comfort and satisfaction above all else. Every smile is bespoke and custom-made—tailored to the patient’s needs and desires,” she says. “Because we make our patient experience our number one priority, we have earned over 1,000 five-star reviews.”
