My braces were removed 4 years ago but now I realized the gap is not filled properly. Can I
Relapse after Braces
Jun 24, 2019
Relapse after orthodontic treatment is the single most common issue once braces treatment has been completed. This primarily occurs if retainers are not worn for an adequate period of time after Braces are removed.
You can wear braces or Invisalign again to correct the gaps that have come up. We usually place a fixed lingual retainer on the inside of your teeth to reduce the chances of relapse from occuring.
Can I get veneers now that my braces are off and then use a retainer on the veneers?
Veneers after Braces or Orthodontic Treatment
Jun 24, 2019
Once braces have aligned your teeth, veneers can be done to further enhance the shape and colour of your existing teeth. Doing them after orthodontic treatment has the advantage of the veneers being very conservative in nature, and not requiring too much tooth preparation.
You can wear a transparent essix retainer after the veneers are placed to prevent teeth from moving.

Post Zoom Whitening, What Can I Do to Alleviate the Pain?
Sensitivity after Zoom Whitening
Dec 12, 2018
Sensitivity following Zoom Teeth Whitening is usually transient and almost always gets ok within the first 2 days. During this time you may use Ibuprofen to help alleviate the pain. You could also use a desensitising toothpaste to help with the pain. Your dentist may also give you a desensitising gel, with custom made trays to wear if the pain is too much.
