What do you recommend to improve my tummy and give me larger breasts?
A Mommy Makeover would benefit you
Sep 6, 2023
I would recommend a tummy tuck to help with sagging skin. If you have abdominal muscle separation, a tummy tuck can also repair that. For the breasts, implants would help with increasing their size. You may also want to consider a breast lift if you can hold a pencil under your breasts.
How long after getting a tummy tuck do the drainage come out?
Drains are in for 10-14 days. You can return to work in as little as a week.
Jul 21, 2023
Drains typically come out 10-14 days post-op. Depending on your occupation, light-duty work is acceptable usually a week or so after surgery. Just be sure there is no lifting, tugging or pulling anything over 10 pounds. Most patients stop taking pain meds 5-7 days after surgery. All restrictions are released at 6 weeks post-op.
5’6, 138lbs & diastasis recti – is a full tummy tuck still the best option?
There are different styles of tummy tucks depending on the skin laxity present.
Jul 13, 2023
If there is hanging skin and muscle laxity below the waistline then the best procedure is a tummy tuck. Most women who have had twins typically have laxity in the muscle or diastasis recti that can remain, even after proper diet and exercise. There are different styles of tummy tucks depending on the skin laxity present. It is best to visit a board-certified plastic surgeon and see which procedure will adequately address your concerns.
