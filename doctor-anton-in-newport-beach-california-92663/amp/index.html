“After more than 31 years as a plastic surgeon, I’ve learned that how we feel and think about ourselves is reflected in our self-confidence,” says Dr. Anton. “A good plastic surgeon is like a sculptor who enhances each patient’s natural beauty, without losing the integrity of its individuality.”

Dr. Anton obtained his undergraduate degree in biology at Tufts University before earning his medical degree at Wake Forest University Bowman Gray School of Medicine in Winston-Salem, North Carolina.

He also completed two fellowships: one in Facial Plastic Surgery at Eastern Virginia Medical School in Norfolk, and a second in Breast Plastic Surgery at St. Joseph Hospital in Atlanta, Georgia.
