Dr. Leber Aesthetic Plastic Surgery – Boston

Doctors

Geoffrey E. Leber, MD

Name: Geoffrey E. Leber, MD
Statement: With offices in both Boston, Massachusetts and Scottsdale, Arizona, Dr. Geoffrey E. Leber is a double board-certified plastic surgeon who works with patients from coast to coast. He is the founder of Dr. Leber Aesthetics Plastic Surgery, specializing in plastic surgery of the face, body, and breast, including procedures for both women and men.

Dr. Leber is best known for his excellence in face and neck lifts, breast enhancements, tummy tucks, and complete mommy makeovers. With every patient, he strives to achieve their aesthetic ideal and surpass their expectations with beautiful, natural results that fit their lifestyle and figure.

Dr. Leber earned his medical degree at the Hahnemann University School of Medicine. Following medical school, he pursued a residency in general surgery at Tufts University School of Medicine, as well as a residency in plastic surgery at the Medical College of Wisconsin. Furthering his training, he applied for the Ellenbogen Advanced Aesthetic Plastic Surgery Fellowship.

Today, Dr. Leber is double board-certified by the American Board of Surgery – General, and the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He’s a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ASAPS) and American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS), and also a fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS).

In pursuing a career in plastic surgery, Dr. Leber was following in the footsteps of his father, who was also a surgeon. With a robust education, passion for aesthetics, and precision in his craft, Dr. Leber has established himself as a plastic surgeon.

In 2001, he founded his Scottsdale, Arizona plastic surgery center, and began catering to both local clientele as well as patients from around the globe. He also served as the clinical instructor for the Mayo Clinic’s plastic surgery residency, and achieved recognition from peers and patients for his unique, cutting-edge techniques. This success, as well as his fondness for the east coast, led Dr. Leber to establish his Boston, Massachusetts plastic surgery center.

Dr. Leber’s patients have described him “absolutely the best,” “a remarkable surgeon,” and “just fantastic.” They’ve said “he is well-worth the 15-hour flight to Scottsdale”; “I literally cannot believe the magical change in my appearance”; and that “Dr. Leber will make you feel like you have had your best friend transform your entire life.”

Whether you’re seeking a mommy makeover, breast enhancement, tummy tuck, face lift, necklift, or other surgical procedure, schedule a consultation with Dr. Leber at his Boston or Scottsdale centers.
