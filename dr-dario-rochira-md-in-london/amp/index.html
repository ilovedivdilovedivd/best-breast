Nov 18, 2018
Hi, thanks for your question.

2 months post op, why is my nose starting to look bigger after my rhinoplasty?
swollen nose after Rhinoplasty
Nov 18, 2018
It looks like that you still have some swelling (perhaps due to the recent trauma) or you are developing some scar tissue which is very common to see in a nose with a very thick skin like yours. Give it some more months and then see your surgeon.
Which would be the best rhinoplasty option for a bulbous supratip and undefined nose?
Bulbous tip
Nov 18, 2018
Most of the problem is due to the tip cartilage.
You are a great candidate for a "preservation rhinoplasty" which modifies the bridge very slightly resulting in very natural look and can reduce and support the tip efficiently and safely.
What procedure should I consider? Rhinoplasy or septoplasty?
Septorhinoplasty
Nov 18, 2018
All my procedure involves some kind of Septoplasty. Therefore my procedure is called Septorhinoplasty.
With the nose you cannot really think that the septum is a separate structure. They work and act together. It is extremely rare not to touch the septum at all, particularly if the nose is not straight.
What is this pea sized lump on the side of my bridge after rhinoplasty? Should I be concerned?
peas sized lump on your bridge after rhinoplasty
Nov 18, 2018
Swelling or graft or scar, it will certainly improve over time. Wait at least 6 weeks and then ask again your surgeon. A second opinion is really something not necessary at this stage. Relax and do not look at your nose too much for the next couple of weeks, though I know it is difficult not to.
Bleeding/Oozing Two Weeks After Breast Reduction, Normal?
oozing and bleeding from nipples after breast reduction
Nov 18, 2018
It is a bit hard to address your concerns without at least a picture.
Anyhow, the situation your are experienced is not rare and it should cause too much distress and worry.
Clean the wound and change the dressing every day and start to worry if the wound opens up or you develop temperature or if the breast becomes much larger than the other or painful to pressure/touch.
