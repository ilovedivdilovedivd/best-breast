Is it possible that I can take pills too enlarge the size of my penis?
No Easy Pill for Penile Enlargement
Aug 3, 2016
There are no pills that will just make the amount of erectile tissue, or the tough fibrous covering that encompasses the erectile tissue, just grow. Medications such as viagra are useful in maintaining the health of the erectile tissue as having erections helps to maintain the quality of the erectile tissue. There are treatments available to improve the small blood vessel flow in the erectile tissue, but this will increase the fullness of erections, not the overall size.
