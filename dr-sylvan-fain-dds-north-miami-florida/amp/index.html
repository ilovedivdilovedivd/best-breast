I want a invisible teeth aligning treatment. Is this possible?
Invisalign treatment
Dec 1, 2016
There are limits to what Invisalign can do. You will get some improvement in your smile with Invisalign but you may not get a perfect result. If your goal is to have a perfect smile then you will need traditional braces. 
Are my Invisalign buttons/attachments the only ones to stain?
Staining Buttons
Dec 1, 2016
Yes, sometimes the attachments can stain. You can try to get the hygienist to polish them. If that does work, speak to your dentist about your concern. 
Teeth not aligned properly – Invisalign?
Teeth alignment
Nov 29, 2016
It looks like you have a “midline discrepancy” which means that your two upper front teeth do not line up with the center of your face. If you wanted corrected properly then you should get traditional braces. Invisalign is not a good treatment option to correct midlines. If you accept that the midline will be off (some notable celebrities like Tom Cruise have midline discrepancies) then you can elect to have Invisalign as long as you understand the limitations of the treatment modality. 
Lost my bottom Invisalign tray and switched into a new tray four days early.
Timing of trays
Nov 29, 2016
Yes, Invisalign is not an exact science. If you switch the trays a few days earlier it should not make a noticeable difference in the treatment outcome. As long as the next tray seats completely then you should be okay. 
Uneven lower lip, I’m an Invisalign patient and trays don’t effect it, what are my options?
Lip asymmtetry
Nov 29, 2016
You may want to consider seeing a dermatologist or plastic surgeon who specializes in lip fillers. I would stay away from permanent lip fillings and choose the something like Juvederm which last 6-9 months.
