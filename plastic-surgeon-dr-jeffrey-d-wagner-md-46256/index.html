Can my tuberous breasts be corrected without implants?
tuberous breast
Aug 4, 2019
Based upon your pictures, you have an element of the tuberous breast shape, however it is not particularly constricted or severe.  Shape correction is possible and depends on your size desires.  If you like your current size, you would get a decent shape with a mastopexy alone.  However, I believe your best result may be a combination of implant and mastopexy, as a combined or staged procedure.  This is preferable to fat grafting in your case.
