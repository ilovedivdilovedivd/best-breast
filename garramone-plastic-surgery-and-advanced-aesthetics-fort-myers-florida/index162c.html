Is it possible for your breasts to get bigger
Mar 28, 2018
Everyones great size is made up of the implant size or volume and the volume of breast tissue and body fat.  The implant size will not increase so any change that you are experiencing is due to an increase in breast tissue or body fat.  If you want to be smaller you may want to decrease the size of the implant.  Depending on the degree of size change you may need a lift.  Your surgeon will be able to guide you with regard to this once you have an in person consultation.
